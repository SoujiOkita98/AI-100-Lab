# Coactive AI

## One-Liner

Enterprise multimodal AI platform that enables organizations to search, understand, and extract structured intelligence from massive volumes of unstructured visual content (images, video, audio) -- without manual tagging or metadata.

## Founders

### Cody Coleman -- Co-Founder & CEO

- **Education**: PhD in Computer Science, Stanford University (advised by Matei Zaharia, co-founder of Databricks and co-creator of Apache Spark); MEng and BS in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science, MIT.
- **Notable research**: Co-creator of DAWNBench and MLPerf; founding member of MLCommons. His work spans high-performance deep learning benchmarking and data-centric AI.
- **Prior experience**: Data roles at eBay, Google, Facebook, and Pinterest.
- **Origin**: Coleman and Gaviria Rojas met through the MIT Interphase Edge program before their freshman year. They collaborated on bringing MIT OpenCourseWare content to Mexican universities, which sparked Coleman's interest in entrepreneurship.

Sources: [Stanford DAWN](https://dawnd9.sites.stanford.edu/people/cody-coleman), [Crunchbase](https://www.crunchbase.com/person/cody-coleman-a982), [MIT News](https://news.mit.edu/2025/coactive-helps-machines-understand-visual-content-ai-0609)

### William (Will) Gaviria Rojas -- Co-Founder & Field CTO

- **Education**: PhD in Materials Science, Northwestern University; BS in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science, MIT (2013).
- **Prior experience**: Data Scientist at eBay; visiting researcher roles; academic work spanning IoT electronics to deep learning in neuromorphic systems.
- **Personal background**: Colombian refugee who became an MIT graduate and startup founder. Moved to the Bay Area in 2020 to work at eBay.
- **Founding story**: While helping Gaviria Rojas move a couch in 2020, he and Coleman realized they both saw an explosion happening around data and AI -- the conversation on that car ride led to Coactive AI's founding.

Sources: [Frontlines Podcast](https://www.frontlines.io/podcasts/william-gaviria-rojas/), [MIT News](https://news.mit.edu/2025/coactive-helps-machines-understand-visual-content-ai-0609)

## Product & Technology

### Platform

Coactive provides a **multimodal AI platform** for enterprise visual content intelligence. Core capabilities:

1. **Semantic Search**: Unified search layer across all visual/audio assets using natural language queries -- no pre-existing metadata or tags required. Single consistent API.
2. **Automated Metadata Generation**: Converts visual and audio content into structured metadata at scale. Uses natural language prompts with frame- to video-level precision. Zero-shot capability; refinable with examples (no model training needed).
3. **Content Analytics**: Extract insights, trends, and patterns from visual content libraries.
4. **Content Moderation / Trust & Safety**: Detect and classify problematic content (gore, sexualized imagery, brand safety violations).

### Technical Differentiators

- Can ingest up to **2,000 video hours per hour**, with 30-60% cost reduction vs. traditional pipelines (per customer benchmarks).
- Available on **AWS Marketplace** and **Azure**, signaling enterprise-grade deployment and cloud-native architecture.
- Designed as infrastructure ("the multimodal AI stack you'd build yourself") -- customers build applications on top of it rather than using a point solution.

Sources: [Coactive Platform](https://www.coactive.ai/product/platform), [Coactive Product Overview](https://www.coactive.ai/product/overview), [AWS Marketplace](https://aws.amazon.com/marketplace/pp/prodview-3f6cw5exiwtoo)

## What Makes Them Interesting

1. **Deep academic pedigree with practical chops**: A Stanford PhD (under Matei Zaharia) and an MIT/Northwestern PhD, both with real industry experience at top tech companies. Angel backing from Fei-Fei Li and Jure Leskovec is a strong signal.

2. **Tackling the "dark matter" of enterprise data**: The vast majority of enterprise data is unstructured (images, video), yet most AI tooling focuses on text and structured data. Coactive is building the operating system for visual content -- a genuinely underserved space relative to its scale.

3. **Infrastructure play, not a point solution**: By positioning as the multimodal AI stack rather than a single-use tool, they create platform lock-in and expand their addressable market across verticals.

4. **Benchmarking DNA**: Coleman literally built the benchmarks (DAWNBench, MLPerf) that the industry uses to measure ML system performance. That background translates into an obsession with throughput and efficiency -- evidenced by the 2,000 video hours/hour ingestion rate.

5. **Founder story and diversity**: Two MIT classmates -- one a Colombian refugee, the other a Black Stanford PhD -- founding a company after a couch-moving road trip. The origin story is authentic and the team's emphasis on inclusive culture (documented on their blog) is notable.

6. **Strong investor syndicate**: a16z, Bessemer, Emerson Collective, Greycroft, and Cherryrock at a $200M valuation -- serious backers for a company with ~66 employees, suggesting high conviction.

7. **Timing**: As generative AI matures, the bottleneck shifts from model capability to data infrastructure. Enterprises sitting on petabytes of visual content need a way to make it queryable and actionable. Coactive is well-positioned for this wave.
