# Air

## Overview

Air was founded in 2017 by Shane Hegde and Tyler Strand. The company is the first comprehensive system of record built specifically for creative work, helping over 2,500 brands manage 200M+ images and videos. Air automates the collection, approval, and distribution of visual content using AI-powered auto-tagging, smart search, and natural language asset editing.

## Funding

Air has raised approximately $77M across 7 funding rounds, including a $35M Series B led by Avenir Growth Capital in January 2025.

## Key Details

- Over 100,000 creatives across 2,100+ businesses use the platform
- AI auto-generates tags, summaries, and chapters without manual input
- Natural language search and bulk editing capabilities
