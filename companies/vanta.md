# Vanta

## Overview
Vanta is an AI-powered security compliance automation platform that helps companies achieve and maintain SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR, and other compliance certifications. The platform uses AI to continuously monitor a company's security posture, automating the traditionally months-long manual compliance process. Serves 9,000+ companies. Founded in 2018 and YC-backed, Vanta reached a $4.15B valuation with its Series D in July 2025.
