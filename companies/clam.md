# Clam

## Overview

Clam provides enterprise-grade security for broad-access AI agents. As organizations deploy AI agents that interact with sensitive data, APIs, and internal systems, Clam solves the fundamental questions of who can act, what they can touch, and how you prove it happened correctly. The platform provides identity management, access control, and audit trails specifically designed for autonomous AI agents operating in enterprise environments. This is part of the critical agent infrastructure layer that 41.5% of YC W26 companies are building.
