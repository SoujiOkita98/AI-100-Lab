# XtalPi

## Overview

XtalPi is an AI-powered drug discovery company that combines quantum physics, artificial intelligence, and robotic automation to accelerate pharmaceutical R&D. Founded in 2014 by three Chinese quantum physicists who were postdoctoral researchers at MIT, XtalPi has built a platform that uses quantum algorithms to calculate molecular structures, with resulting datasets used to train AI models that screen billions of molecules for potential drugs.

XtalPi has dual operations: Cambridge, Massachusetts (US R&D) and Shenzhen, China (global headquarters). The company went public on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange in December 2025.

## Chinese Founder Context

XtalPi represents the MIT-to-China AI pipeline -- three mainland Chinese physicists who did their postdoctoral work at MIT and then built a company that bridges US and Chinese pharmaceutical R&D ecosystems. Their decision to maintain significant US operations (Cambridge, MA) while headquartering in China positions them uniquely in the US-China AI landscape.

## Technology

- **Quantum-AI Platform:** Uses quantum mechanical algorithms to calculate molecular crystal structures with high accuracy, then feeds that data into AI/ML models to predict drug properties.
- **AI Large Language Models:** Developed proprietary AI LLMs trained on molecular data for drug screening and design.
- **Robotic Automation:** Integrates automated lab systems for high-throughput experimentation.
- **COVID-19 Contribution:** Helped accelerate the development of Pfizer's Paxlovid, the first US-approved oral COVID treatment.

## Business Scale

- Serves nearly 4 out of 5 major global pharmaceutical companies
- Partnerships with Pfizer, and multiple other big pharma companies
- Dual operations in Cambridge, MA and Shenzhen, China

## IPO

XtalPi listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange on December 30, 2025 -- the first AI-driven biotech company to go public on HKEX's Main Board under Chapter 8.05 listing rules. The IPO raised HKD 2.277 billion, making it the largest biotech IPO in Hong Kong in 2025.

## What Makes XtalPi Interesting

1. **MIT quantum physics pedigree.** Three Chinese postdocs from MIT applying quantum physics to AI drug discovery.
2. **Pfizer validation.** Contributing to Paxlovid development provides powerful commercial proof.
3. **Public company.** Rare AI drug discovery company to reach IPO.
4. **US-China bridging.** Maintains significant US R&D operations while operating as a Chinese company.
5. **Cross-domain innovation.** Combining quantum physics, AI, and robotics in a single platform is genuinely novel.
