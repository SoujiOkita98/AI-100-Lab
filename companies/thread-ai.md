# Thread AI

## One-liner

Composable AI infrastructure platform helping enterprises design, deploy, and scale AI-powered workflows through its core platform Lemma, founded by former Palantir AI leaders.

## Founders & Leadership

### Angela McNeal -- Co-Founder & CEO
- Former Foundry Head of AI Product at Palantir.
- Built Palantir's first ML product suite, representing over $100M in projects.

### Mayada Gonimah -- Co-Founder & CTO
- Former Foundry Head of AI/ML Engineering at Palantir.
- Co-led Palantir's modeling and AI/ML products group alongside McNeal.

## Product & Business Model

Thread AI builds a composable infrastructure layer for enterprise AI, designed for the complexities of the business world. The core platform, Lemma, helps enterprises design, deploy, and scale AI-powered workflows. The company differentiates by focusing on enterprise-grade AI infrastructure rather than consumer or tech-only use cases.
