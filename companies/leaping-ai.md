# Leaping AI

## Overview

Leaping AI builds AI-powered voice agents designed for automating call center operations. The platform uses generative AI technology to conduct human-like conversations across multiple channels, handling tasks such as complex workflows, sales, customer support, and outbound campaigns. The founding team has backgrounds in autonomous robotics (self-driving cars, space-tech), and one co-founder previously led Germany's largest space-tech student organization's software division.
