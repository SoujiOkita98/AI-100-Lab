# LiveKit

## Overview

LiveKit is an open-source real-time communication platform and SDK for building applications that transmit real-time audio, video, and data. Founded in 2021 by Russ d'Sa and David Zhao, the platform powers voice AI and real-time media for thousands of developers and teams across industries. Most notably, LiveKit powers ChatGPT's Advanced Voice Mode, enabling low-latency streaming for OpenAI. The company achieved unicorn status at a $1B valuation following its $100M Series C led by Index Ventures in January 2026, with participation from Salesforce Ventures, Hanabi Capital, Altimeter Capital, and Redpoint Ventures.
