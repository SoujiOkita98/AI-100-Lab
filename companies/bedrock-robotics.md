# Bedrock Robotics

## Overview

Bedrock Robotics is an autonomous construction robotics company founded in 2024 by ex-Waymo engineers. CEO Boris Sofman previously led Waymo's self-driving trucks program; CTO Kevin Peterson and co-founders Ajay Gummalla and Tom Eliaz are also Waymo veterans. The company builds self-driving kits that retrofit onto existing construction and worksite heavy equipment, integrating adaptive machine intelligence, continuous performance learning, real-time progress tracking, and remote operational oversight. Bedrock emerged from stealth in July 2025 with $80M in Seed/Series A funding, then raised a massive $270M Series B in February 2026 at a $1.75B valuation (unicorn). Targeting first fully operator-less excavator deployments in 2026.
