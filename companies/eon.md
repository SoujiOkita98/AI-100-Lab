# Eon

## Overview
Eon is one of Israel's fastest-growing startups, reaching a $4 billion valuation less than two years after founding. Built by three AWS veterans who previously co-founded CloudEndure (acquired by Amazon), Eon's platform eliminates manual backup tasks by autonomously scanning, mapping, and classifying cloud resources. The platform provides full visibility and seamless access to enterprise backup data, enabling AI analytics on previously locked-away data. Eon's growth trajectory has been compared to Wiz, the Israeli cybersecurity company.
