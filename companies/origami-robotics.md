# Origami Robotics

## Overview

Origami Robotics is building a general-purpose robotic manipulation system -- the "Manipulate Anything Robot." The company applies AI to enable robots to handle diverse objects and tasks without task-specific programming, addressing one of the longest-standing challenges in robotics: dexterous manipulation of arbitrary objects in unstructured environments. This aligns with the broader robotics boom in early 2026, with over $1.2B flowing into AI robotics companies in a single week of March 2026.
