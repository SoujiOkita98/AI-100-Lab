# Datapeople

## Overview

Datapeople is a New York City-based recruiting analytics platform founded in 2015 that uses AI to help hiring teams optimize job descriptions for clarity, effectiveness, and regulatory compliance. The platform analyzes job postings using performance, operational, and language analytics to improve candidate quality and hiring outcomes. In September 2025, Datapeople was acquired by Payscale, a compensation data and software company.

## Founders

- **Amit Bhatia** - Co-Founder. Computer Engineering (NTU Singapore), MBA Finance (UCLA); Executive Director at Morgan Stanley in media investment banking; former Citigroup. Origin: India / Singapore.
- **Phillip Reyland** - Co-Founder. Founder/MD of The Byte Dept; co-founded TapRecruit; bootstrapped Datapeople analyzing 40K+ companies' job postings. Origin: United States.

## Product

Datapeople's platform helps compensation and talent professionals write and edit job descriptions that integrate with their applicant tracking systems. Key capabilities include:
- AI-powered job description scoring and optimization
- Language analytics to reduce bias and improve clarity
- Pay transparency compliance support (critical as states like California, New York, and Colorado mandate pay range disclosure)
- ATS integration for streamlined workflows

## Acquisition by Payscale

On September 16, 2025, Payscale acquired Datapeople for an undisclosed amount. The strategic rationale centers on bridging talent acquisition and compensation management, particularly as pay transparency legislation creates demand for integrated solutions. Some jurisdictions impose fines up to $300,000 for salary transparency violations.
