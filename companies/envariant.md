# Envariant

## Overview

Envariant builds interpretability and reasoning infrastructure for foundation models. As AI systems are deployed in high-stakes domains like healthcare, finance, and legal, the ability to understand why a model made a specific decision becomes critical for trust, compliance, and debugging. Envariant provides tools that make foundation model reasoning transparent and verifiable, addressing a key barrier to enterprise AI adoption. The company operates in the growing mechanistic interpretability space alongside research from Anthropic, OpenAI, and academic labs.
