# Music AI (Moises)

## One-liner

AI-powered music platform with 50M+ users worldwide offering stem separation, chord recognition, and music transcription tools, winning Apple iPad App of the Year 2024.

## Founders & Leadership

### Geraldo Ramos -- Co-Founder & CEO
- Originally from Northeast Brazil. Started his first tech company at 16, sold it at 26.
- Moved to New York in 2012 for business development before eventually settling in Utah.

### Eddie Hsu -- Co-Founder & COO

### Jardson Almeida -- Co-Founder & CDO

## Product & Business Model

Music AI's flagship product Moises is an AI-driven music platform offering stem separation, chord recognition, and music transcription. The platform has over 50 million users worldwide and won Apple's iPad App of the Year in 2024. Charlie Puth joined as Chief Music Officer.
