# Helm.ai

## One-liner

AI software platform for autonomous driving and ADAS using proprietary Deep Teaching unsupervised learning technology, serving automotive OEMs, Tier 1 suppliers, and robotics companies.

## Founders & Leadership

### Vladislav Voroninski -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Russian-American mathematician with BS/MA from UCLA (summa cum laude) and PhD from UC Berkeley.
- Former faculty member in the MIT Mathematics Department.
- Previously co-founded Sift Security (cybersecurity startup acquired by Netskope).

### Tudor Achim -- Co-Founder & CTO

- CS/Math background from Carnegie Mellon University.
- Former PhD candidate in CS at Stanford.
- Built and led the ML team at Quora.

## Product & Business Model

Helm.ai develops AI software for driver assistance systems, autonomous driving, and robotics using its proprietary "Deep Teaching" unsupervised learning approach. This eliminates the need for expensive manual data labeling. Honda Motor announced plans to integrate Helm.ai's Deep Teaching technology in its 2026 Zero Series Electric Vehicles for Level 3 partial automation.
