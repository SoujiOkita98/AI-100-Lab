# Arcline

## Overview

Arcline provides AI-native legal services for startups, combining elite lawyers with AI automation to deliver same-day turnaround on legal work. The platform's AI handles approximately 80% of the work, so startups pay elite lawyers only for the final 20% of review and complex judgment. This hybrid model aims to make top-tier legal services accessible to startups at a fraction of traditional BigLaw costs. Arcline was highlighted by Nordic 9 and featured in Artificial Lawyer's coverage of Y Combinator's legal-tech picks.

## What Makes Arcline Interesting

1. **80/20 AI-human model:** Clear articulation of where AI adds value (routine work) vs. where humans are needed (judgment calls).
2. **Startup-focused:** YC-backed law firm serving YC-backed startups creates a powerful distribution channel.
3. **Same-day turnaround:** Speed is a key differentiator; traditional legal processes take days to weeks.
