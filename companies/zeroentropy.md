# ZeroEntropy

## Overview

ZeroEntropy builds next-generation AI search and retrieval infrastructure. At its core is a proprietary re-ranker called ze-rank-1, which ensures that when an AI system searches a knowledge base, it retrieves the most relevant information first. The company is part of a growing wave of infrastructure companies using retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) to power search for AI agents.
