# Hume AI

## Overview

Hume AI is an empathic AI company founded in 2021 by Alan Cowen (PhD Psychology, UC Berkeley; formerly led Affective Computing at Google AI). The company builds AI models that understand nuances of human emotional expression across voice, face, body, and language, based on Cowen's semantic space theory. Their flagship product is the Empathic Voice Interface (EVI). Total funding is approximately $81M. In January 2026, Google made a reverse acqui-hire investment in the company.
