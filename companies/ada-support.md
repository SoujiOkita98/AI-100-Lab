# Ada

## Overview
Ada is a Toronto-based AI customer service automation company that has raised $200M in total funding. The platform provides AI-powered agents that automate customer interactions for enterprises, replacing traditional rule-based chatbots with intelligent conversational AI. Ada is recognized as a prime example of Canadian AI innovation shaping the global customer experience market. The company serves enterprise clients across multiple industries and has been supported by the Government of Canada's AI investment initiatives.
