# Surf AI

## Overview
Surf AI is a Tel Aviv-based cybersecurity startup that emerged from stealth in March 2026 with $57M in funding led by Accel. Founded in September 2024 by a team of five experienced Israeli cybersecurity veterans, the company builds an agentic security operations platform. The platform aggregates signals from identity, cloud, security, data, HR, and IT tools to create a living context graph mapping assets, owners, permissions, and dependencies. Specialized AI agents prioritize risks by business impact and coordinate remediation workflows under human oversight. The company has 66 employees and is already working with Fortune 500 companies.
