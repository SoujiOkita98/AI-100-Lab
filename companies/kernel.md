# Kernel

## Overview

Kernel is a London-based AI startup founded in 2023 that builds custom AI agents for enterprise revenue operations. The company offers an AI-native alternative to Dun & Bradstreet, providing an entity database that serves as a digital representation of the B2B economy structured for AI to extract information. Revenue operations teams at companies like Remote, Navan, Zip, and Gong use Kernel's Data Management Platform to clean CRM data, remove duplicates, build corporate family trees, and perform custom enrichment. The company has raised $14M in total funding.

## Founders

- **Anders Krohn** - Co-Founder & CEO. BA Economics & Management (Oxford); previously co-founded Aula Education and Ublend. Origin: Denmark.
- **Marcus Henglein** - Co-Founder. Philosophy degree; co-founded Aula Education and Ublend with Krohn; former BCG consultant; co-founded Project Access. Origin: Norway.

## Product

Kernel's platform provides:
- **Entity Database:** A comprehensive digital representation of the B2B economy
- **AI Agents for RevOps:** Custom agents that research companies and sync findings
- **CRM Data Quality:** Account cleaning, deduplication, and hierarchy building
- **Custom Enrichment:** Extraction from unstructured data including website content and job descriptions
