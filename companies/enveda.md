# Enveda Biosciences

## One-liner

AI platform that decodes the chemistry of plants and natural compounds using mass spectrometry and machine learning to discover novel therapeutics.

## Founders & Leadership

### Viswa Colluru -- Founder & CEO

- PhD in immuno-oncology from University of Wisconsin-Madison; developed new therapeutic agents for prostate cancer.
- First Innovation Scientist and Product Manager at Recursion Pharmaceuticals, where he also established the early commercial team.
- Built Enveda from $55k in personal savings into a clinical-stage unicorn in six years.
- Named UBS Global Visionary in 2023; member of the World Economic Forum's Unicorn Innovator Community.

### Pieter Dorrestein -- Scientific Co-Founder

- Professor at UC San Diego and one of the world's leading experts in mass spectrometry and metabolomics.

## Product & Business Model

Enveda's AI platform identifies and decodes molecules inside plants and natural sources, then predicts which may become useful medicines. The company has a pipeline of 10+ development candidates and has progressed to Phase 1b/Phase 2a clinical trials in atopic dermatitis, with additional trials in asthma.
