# Cognition

## Overview
Cognition builds Devin, an autonomous AI software engineer that can plan, write, debug, and deploy code. Founded by three competitive programming champions who are all Chinese-American. The company grew from $1M to $73M ARR in 9 months.

## Windsurf Acquisition
In July 2025, Cognition acquired Windsurf (formerly Codeium) for ~$250M after Google acqui-hired Windsurf's CEO for $2.4B and OpenAI's $3B bid expired. Cognition got the IP, product, brand, ~210 employees, and $82M ARR business with 350+ enterprise customers. Combined ARR grew 30%+ within 7 weeks post-acquisition.

## Investors
Founders Fund (Peter Thiel), 8VC (Joe Lonsdale), Lux Capital, Khosla Ventures, Pear VC, Neo, Elad Gil, Definition Capital, Swish VC, Bain Capital Ventures, D1 Capital.
