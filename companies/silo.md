# Silo AI

## One-liner

Europe's largest private AI lab, building enterprise AI solutions and custom LLMs, acquired by AMD for $665M in the largest AI acquisition in European history.

## Founders & Leadership

### Peter Sarlin -- Co-Founder & CEO

- PhD in Machine Learning. One of Finland's most prominent AI researchers.
- Built Silo AI from a bootstrapped consultancy into Europe's largest private AI lab with 300+ AI researchers.
- Continues to lead the Silo AI team within AMD's Artificial Intelligence Group post-acquisition.

### Tero Ojanpera -- Co-Founder

- Former Nokia executive and EVP of Services, bringing deep telecom and enterprise technology experience.

### Additional Co-Founders

Johan Kronberg, Juha Hulkko, Kaj-Mikael Bjork, and Ville Hulkko -- a team of six Finnish tech veterans.

## Founding Story

Founded in Helsinki in 2017 by six co-founders, Silo AI was bootstrapped for its first five years, growing organically to over 200 AI researchers. The company operated as a private AI lab helping enterprises integrate AI into their products and operations. After raising modest strategic capital in 2022 to transition from lab to product company, Silo AI caught AMD's attention as the chipmaker sought to build its enterprise AI software capabilities to compete with NVIDIA's ecosystem.

## Product & Business Model

### What Silo AI Did

1. **Enterprise AI solutions** -- Helped major corporations (including Allianz, Philips, Rolls-Royce) integrate AI into products and operations.
2. **Custom LLMs** -- Built the Viking family of open-source multilingual LLMs optimized for European languages.
3. **AI platform** -- SiloGen platform for enterprise AI development and deployment.

### Strategic Value to AMD

AMD acquired Silo AI to expand its enterprise AI solutions capabilities globally, complementing AMD's hardware (MI300X GPUs) with software and services expertise.

## What Makes Silo AI Interesting

1. **Bootstrapped to $665M exit.** Built on only ~$11M of external capital -- extraordinary capital efficiency.
2. **European AI champion.** Largest private AI lab in Europe, proving world-class AI companies can be built outside Silicon Valley.
3. **Strategic acquisition logic.** AMD needed AI software expertise to compete with NVIDIA's CUDA ecosystem; Silo AI provided a 300-person AI team.
4. **Largest AI exit in Europe.** Set the benchmark for European AI startup exits.

## Risks & Open Questions

- Post-acquisition integration with AMD's organization and product roadmap.
- Whether Silo AI's team retains autonomy and innovation speed within a large chipmaker.
- AMD's AI software strategy competing against NVIDIA's entrenched ecosystem.
