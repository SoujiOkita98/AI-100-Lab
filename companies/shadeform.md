# Shadeform

## Overview

Shadeform is a GPU cloud marketplace that aggregates GPU supply from more than 20 cloud providers -- including Lambda, Nebius, and Crusoe -- offering a unified API and console for smooth provisioning. Founded in 2023 in response to the GPU shortage driven by the generative AI boom, Shadeform helps AI startups and developers find, compare, and deploy GPU compute resources across providers without vendor lock-in.

## Founders & Key People

### Ed Goode -- Co-Founder
- **Background:** Built and operated critical cloud infrastructure services at AWS; experienced the pain of GPU procurement firsthand as a customer at Robinhood

### Ronald Ding -- Co-Founder
- **Background:** Cloud infrastructure experience at Azure; experienced compute challenges as a customer at Capital One

## Products & Technology

1. **GPU Marketplace:** Aggregated inventory from 20+ cloud providers with real-time pricing and availability
2. **Unified API:** Single API for provisioning GPU instances across any supported provider
3. **Console:** Web-based dashboard for browsing, comparing, and deploying GPU resources
4. **Provider-Agnostic:** Seamless switching between providers without changing code or configurations

## Business Model

- **Marketplace commission:** Takes a percentage on GPU provisioning facilitated through the platform
- **Target customers:** AI startups, ML teams, and researchers needing GPU compute
- **Value proposition:** Find the cheapest available GPUs across all providers in one place, avoiding the GPU shortage bottleneck

## What Makes Shadeform Interesting

1. **Solving the GPU scarcity problem.** During the AI boom, GPU availability became a critical bottleneck. Shadeform addresses this by aggregating supply, making it the "Kayak for GPUs."

2. **Founding team from both sides.** Having founders who both built (AWS, Azure) and consumed (Robinhood, Capital One) cloud infrastructure gives unique insight into both provider and customer pain points.

3. **Network effects potential.** As more providers join and more customers use the platform, the marketplace becomes increasingly valuable to both sides -- a classic marketplace flywheel.

4. **Infrastructure picks-and-shovels play.** Rather than building AI applications, Shadeform provides the compute layer that every AI company needs, positioning it as a horizontal enabler of the entire AI ecosystem.
