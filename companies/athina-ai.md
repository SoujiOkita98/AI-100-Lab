# Athina AI

## Overview

Athina AI is an LLM evaluation and monitoring platform used by AI teams to prototype, experiment, evaluate, and monitor production-grade AI pipelines. The platform provides 50+ preset evaluation metrics and supports custom evaluations without code. Athina is used by AI teams at notable companies including Perplexity, Doximity, You.com, Meesho, and PhysicsWallah, among hundreds of others.

## Products & Technology

Athina's platform covers the full LLM development lifecycle:

1. **Prototyping:** IDE for building and testing AI pipelines with rapid iteration
2. **Experimentation:** A/B testing and comparison tools for models, prompts, and configurations
3. **Evaluation:** 50+ preset evaluation metrics (accuracy, hallucination detection, relevance, etc.) plus custom evaluation creation with no-code tools
4. **Monitoring:** Production monitoring for detecting quality degradation, latency spikes, and cost anomalies in deployed AI features

## Business Model

- **SaaS platform:** Subscription-based pricing for teams
- **Target customers:** AI/ML product teams building production LLM features
- **Free tier:** Available for individual developers

## Competitive Landscape

Athina competes in the LLM evaluation space alongside Langfuse, Braintrust, Arize AI, and LangSmith. Its differentiation lies in the breadth of preset evaluation metrics and the ease of creating custom evaluations without code.

## What Makes Athina AI Interesting

1. **High-profile customer base.** Being used by Perplexity and You.com (both leading AI search companies) validates the platform for the most demanding AI workloads.

2. **Evaluation-first approach.** While many observability platforms focus on logging and tracing, Athina leads with evaluation -- the step that most directly impacts AI product quality.

3. **No-code custom evaluations.** Allowing non-technical team members to create custom evaluation criteria broadens the platform's appeal beyond just engineering teams.
