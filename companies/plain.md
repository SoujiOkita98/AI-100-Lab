# Plain

## One-liner

API-first collaborative B2B customer support platform that aggregates cross-channel customer communications into a single real-time platform, built by ex-Deliveroo leaders.

## Founders & Leadership

### Simon Rohrbach -- Co-Founder
- Former Deliveroo, where he led a 40-person content, research, and design team from the early days through scale-up.

### Matt Vagni -- Co-Founder
- Former Deliveroo, where he worked on design systems and internal tooling from the early days.

## Product & Business Model

Plain powers the shift in B2B customer support from emails and ticketing to real-time, cross-channel communication and collaboration. The platform takes an API-first approach, enabling deep integrations with existing tools and workflows. Originally launched as Resolve before rebranding to Plain.
