# Mainframe

## Overview

Mainframe was founded in 2024 by Jordan Singer, a design engineer known for founding Diagram (one of the earliest AI-native design tools, acquired by Figma) and his work at Cash App. The company's debut product, Cobot, is a generative AI assistant designed to proactively anticipate user needs and streamline daily tasks.

## Funding

Mainframe raised a $5.5M seed round co-led by Peter Boyce II of Stellation Capital and Lachy Groom, with participation from Akshay Kothari (co-founder of Notion), Weekend Fund, and others. Total funding stands at $6.75M including a pre-seed round.

## Key Details

- Founded by Diagram creator Jordan Singer (Diagram was acquired by Figma)
- Building Cobot, an AI assistant that anticipates needs rather than waiting for prompts
- Notable angel investors from the design and productivity tool ecosystem
