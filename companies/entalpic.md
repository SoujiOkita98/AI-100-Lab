# Entalpic

> French AI startup using generative models to discover optimized catalytic materials for decarbonizing industrial chemistry, founded by Mila and Owkin alumni.

## Company Overview

Entalpic is a Paris-based AI startup building a generative AI platform to accelerate the discovery of new materials for industrial chemistry, with a particular focus on decarbonization. Founded in 2023 by a team combining deep ML research credentials (from Mila, Quebec's world-renowned AI institute) with industrial AI commercialization experience (from Owkin, the AI drug discovery unicorn), Entalpic tackles one of the most consequential applications of AI: reducing CO2 emissions through optimized catalytic materials.

The chemical industry accounts for approximately 5% of global CO2 emissions, and Entalpic's thesis is that AI-driven materials discovery can identify catalysts that make industrial chemical processes dramatically more efficient and less carbon-intensive.

## Founders

### Mathieu Galtier -- CEO
Former Chief Data & Platform Officer at Owkin, the billion-dollar startup applying AI to drug discovery. At Owkin, Galtier developed the company's data partnership network with major pharmaceutical and industrial companies -- experience directly applicable to building industrial partnerships for materials discovery.

### Victor Schmidt -- CTO
Researcher at Mila, the Quebec AI Institute founded by Turing Award winner Yoshua Bengio. Co-authored a series of papers on predictive and generative models for materials science. Brings deep expertise in applying modern ML architectures to scientific problems.

### Alexandre Duval -- CSO
Also from Mila, where he co-authored foundational work on AI for materials discovery with Victor Schmidt. Provides the scientific rigor and chemistry domain expertise for the company's research direction.

## Business Model

Entalpic partners with industrial companies to discover and optimize catalytic materials. The platform uses generative AI to propose novel molecular and material structures, which are then validated experimentally. Revenue model likely includes R&D partnerships, licensing, and milestone-based payments.

## What Makes Entalpic Interesting

1. **Mila pedigree:** Founded by researchers from one of the world's top AI research institutes (founded by Yoshua Bengio), bringing academic rigor to a commercial application.
2. **Climate impact thesis:** Directly targeting CO2 reduction in industrial chemistry -- one of the hardest-to-abate sectors for decarbonization.
3. **CuspAI competitor/peer:** Part of a growing cohort of AI-for-materials-discovery startups (alongside CuspAI, Orbital Materials) that are attracting serious venture capital.
4. **Owkin commercialization playbook:** CEO Galtier's experience building Owkin's industrial partnership network provides a proven template for commercializing scientific AI platforms.
5. **European AI ecosystem:** One of the more promising deep-tech AI startups emerging from the French/European ecosystem.
