# Deepnote

## Overview

Deepnote is a collaborative data science notebook platform founded in 2019 by Jakub Jurovych, Filip Stollar, Jan Matas, and Jirka Lhotka, originally from Prague, Czech Republic (Y Combinator alumni). The platform combines code notebooks, SQL, visualization, and AI assistance in a collaborative workspace for data teams. Deepnote has raised ~$24M from Accel and Index Ventures, and hit $3.9M in revenue by mid-2025. Notable angel investors include Naval Ravikant.
