# Osmo

## Overview

Osmo is an AI company digitizing the sense of smell, founded in 2022 by Alex Wiltschko, who previously created Alphabet's olfaction group at Google Brain. Wiltschko holds a PhD in Neurobiology from Harvard and previously founded two AI companies (one acquired by a biotech firm, one by Twitter). Osmo's Olfactory Intelligence platform enables AI-powered scent design and has achieved Scent Teleportation (full digitization of scent with no human intervention). The company launched "Generation," the world's first AI-powered fragrance house. Total funding is ~$135M.
