# Kai Security

## Overview
Kai Security is a San Jose-based cybersecurity startup that emerged from stealth in March 2026 with $125M in funding. The company has built an agentic AI platform designed to operate autonomously at machine speed, bridging IT and OT (operational technology) security. Founded by Galina Antova (ex-Claroty co-founder) and Damiano Bolzoni (ex-SecurityMatters co-founder), the platform uses intelligent agents capable of reasoning, acting, and adapting across threat intelligence, exposure management, detection, and incident response. Within just 10 months of writing its first line of code, Kai signed multiple large enterprise customers across energy, pharmaceuticals, automotive, and hospitality sectors.
