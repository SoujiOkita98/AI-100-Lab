# Nozomio

## Overview

Nozomio's flagship product Nia is an API layer that provides AI coding agents with up-to-date, continuously monitored context from libraries, research papers, and technical documentation. Founded by 18-year-old Arlan Rakhmetzhanov, who dropped out of high school in Kazakhstan after getting accepted into Y Combinator (S25).
