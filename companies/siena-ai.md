# Siena AI

## Overview

Siena AI is an autonomous AI customer service platform founded in 2022 by Romanian entrepreneurs Andrei Negrau (CEO) and Lisa Popovici (CMO). The company builds empathic AI agents for e-commerce and consumer brands, delivering human-like customer support experiences. Siena hit $5.9M in revenue by 2025 with a 54-person team. The company has raised approximately $11M across a pre-seed and two seed rounds.
