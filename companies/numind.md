# NuMind

## Overview

NuMind enables companies to create custom machine learning models for NLP tasks without in-house ML expertise. By leveraging large language models like GPT-4 under the hood, NuMind allows customers to complete NLP projects at least 10x faster than traditional approaches. The platform supports a variety of NLP tasks including sentiment analysis, content moderation, chatbot creation, topic detection, and entity extraction.

## Founders & Key People

### Etienne Bernard -- Co-Founder & CEO
- **Background:** Former Head of Machine Learning at Wolfram Research, where he led the development of ML capabilities for Mathematica and the Wolfram Language. Deep expertise in making ML accessible to non-technical users.
- **Origin:** French, based in San Francisco
- **Insight:** At Wolfram, Bernard saw firsthand how powerful ML could be when made accessible. With LLMs, he recognized an opportunity to democratize custom NLP model creation.

### Samuel Bernard -- Co-Founder
- **Background:** Co-founded Make.org, a civic technology platform focused on citizen engagement. Background in technology and social impact.
- **Origin:** French

## Products & Technology

1. **Custom Model Builder:** No-code interface for creating task-specific NLP models using LLMs as the foundation
2. **Supported Tasks:** Sentiment analysis, content moderation, chatbot creation, topic detection, entity extraction, text classification
3. **LLM-Powered:** Uses large language models (GPT-4 class) to enable rapid model creation with minimal training data
4. **Deployment:** Models can be deployed as APIs for production use

## Business Model

- **SaaS platform:** Subscription-based access to the model building and deployment platform
- **Target customers:** Companies that need custom NLP solutions but lack in-house ML teams
- **Value proposition:** 10x faster NLP project completion compared to traditional custom model development

## What Makes NuMind Interesting

1. **Wolfram Research pedigree.** The CEO's experience leading ML at Wolfram -- a company synonymous with making complex computation accessible -- directly informs NuMind's mission of democratizing custom ML.

2. **Pre-ChatGPT insight.** Founded in 2022 (S22 batch), NuMind recognized the potential of LLMs for custom model creation before the ChatGPT explosion made this obvious to the broader market.

3. **Custom models over general-purpose.** While many companies focus on using LLMs directly, NuMind uses them to create smaller, task-specific models -- potentially offering better accuracy, lower cost, and faster inference for specific use cases.

4. **Franco-American bridge.** French founders with investors in France and the US position NuMind to serve both European and American markets.
