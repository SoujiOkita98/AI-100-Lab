# K-Scale Labs

## Overview

K-Scale Labs is building the world's first open-source general-purpose humanoid robot. Their robot, Stompy, is a 4-foot humanoid featuring claw grippers, designed so that each part fits on a standard 3D printer bed, with a total bill of materials under $10,000. The company's vision is to enable anyone with a few 3D printers to start a robot factory.

## Founders

- **Benjamin Bolte** — Previously worked on robotics at Tesla and Meta
- **Matthew Freed** — Developed advanced robotics for Lockheed Martin and General Dynamics
- **Pawel Budzianowski** — ML researcher with PhD from University of Cambridge
