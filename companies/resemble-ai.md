# Resemble AI

## Overview

Resemble AI is an enterprise AI platform for voice generation and real-time deepfake detection. Founded in 2019 by Zohaib Ahmed and Saqib Muhammad, the company provides voice cloning, text-to-speech, and its DETECT-3B Omni deepfake detection model with 98% accuracy across 38+ languages. The company raised a $13M Series B in December 2025 backed by Google's AI Futures Fund, Okta Ventures, Sony Innovation Fund, and others. Saudi Aramco's Wa'ed Ventures also made a strategic investment in 2026 to expand deepfake detection capabilities in the Middle East.
