# Apriora

## Overview

Apriora (now also known as Alex AI) conducts live AI interviews using an AI interviewer named "Alex." Alex engages in real-time video conversations with job candidates, using either pre-set questions or AI-recommended ones based on the job description. During interviews, the AI asks personalized follow-up questions based on candidate responses.
