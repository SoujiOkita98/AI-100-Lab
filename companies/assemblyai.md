# AssemblyAI

## Overview

AssemblyAI is an AI company focused on speech recognition and audio intelligence. Founded in 2017 by Dylan Fox, the company provides research-grade AI models via API for automatic speech recognition, speaker diarization, sentiment analysis, topic detection, and content moderation of audio and video data. The company has raised $115M across 4 rounds, with its most recent being a $50M Series C led by Accel in December 2023.
