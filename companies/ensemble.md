# Ensemble

## Overview

Ensemble is a San Francisco-based AI startup founded in 2023 that addresses data quality challenges in enterprise AI deployments. The company has developed a proprietary technology called "Dark Matter" that enhances machine learning model performance by creating richer representations of data without requiring vast amounts of additional data or complex model architectures. Ensemble raised $3.3M in seed funding led by Salesforce Ventures in September 2024.

## Founders

- **Alex Reneau** - Co-Founder & CEO. PhD research at Northwestern on programmable feature engineering for time series; published foundational papers in the field. Origin: United States.
- **Zach Albertson** - Co-Founder & COO. Northwestern University; AI/ML consulting background with hands-on enterprise implementation experience. Origin: United States.

The founders are pioneering a novel approach to data representation using an "objective function" that automatically generates features for tabular data, enabling better model performance and decision-making.

## Product

Ensemble's core technology, Dark Matter, supports data quality by:
- Automatically generating features for tabular data
- Creating richer data representations for predictive tasks
- Improving ML model performance without requiring additional raw data
- Enabling better production deployment of AI models

The company is working with customers in biotechnology and advertising technology, with early results showing promise.
