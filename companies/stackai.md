# Stack AI

## Overview

Stack AI is a no-code platform for building and deploying enterprise AI agents, founded in 2021 by MIT PhDs Antoni Rosinol and Bernardo Aceituno. The company was created to solve the problem of enterprises being unable to integrate their proprietary data with emerging LLMs.

## Funding

Stack AI raised a $3M seed round backed by Gradient Ventures and Y Combinator, followed by a $16M Series A with investors including Lobby VC, LifeX Ventures, Guillermo Rauch (CEO of Vercel), and Bob Van Luijt (CEO of Weaviate), bringing total funding to $19M.

## Key Details

- Y Combinator alumnus
- Enables enterprises to build AI agents without writing code
- Used for deploying AI workflows across departments at scale
