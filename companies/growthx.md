# GrowthX AI

## One-liner

AI-powered growth engine that combines AI workflows with expert human oversight to transform brands into publishers and leaders into influencers through scalable content creation.

## Founders & Leadership

### Marcel Santilli -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Growth marketing and entrepreneurship background.
- Led GrowthX from concept to $7M annual run rate in under a year.

### Daniel Lopes -- Co-Founder & CTO

- Former engineer at IFTTT.
- Technical leader with expertise in automation and AI workflow infrastructure.

## Product & Business Model

GrowthX positions itself as a "service-as-software" company, combining AI-powered content workflows with human expert oversight. The platform generates finished content that drives measurable business outcomes, sitting between traditional agencies and pure software products. Clients include Reddit, Webflow, Ramp, Superhuman, and Strapi (40+ customers).
