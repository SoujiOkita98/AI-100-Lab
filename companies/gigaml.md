# GigaML

## One-liner

AI operations platform for enterprises that builds AI support agents for customer service and operations automation, enabling on-premises LLM deployment with data privacy.

## Founders & Leadership

### Varun Vummadi -- Co-Founder & CEO
- Y Combinator alumni leading GigaML's growth in enterprise AI.

### Esha Manideep -- Co-Founder
- Y Combinator alumni and technical co-founder.

## Product & Business Model

GigaML builds AI support agents for enterprises, particularly focused on customer service and operations automation. The company originally focused on helping enterprises deploy LLMs on-premises while maintaining data privacy, and has evolved into a comprehensive enterprise AI operations platform.
