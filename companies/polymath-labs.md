# Polymath Labs

## Overview

Polymath Labs is a research lab focused on long-horizon reinforcement learning -- building AI agents capable of planning and executing strategies over extended time periods rather than making single-step decisions. Long-horizon RL is considered one of the most important unsolved problems in AI, as it enables agents to tackle complex, multi-step tasks that require sustained planning, such as software engineering projects, scientific research, and real-world logistics. The company's work has direct implications for the next generation of autonomous AI agents.
