# Octolane AI

## Overview

Octolane AI builds a self-driving AI CRM that automates sales actions and manual CRM updates. The platform uses AI to provide data-driven sales intelligence, challenging incumbents like Salesforce with a fundamentally AI-native approach to customer relationship management.
