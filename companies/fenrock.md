# Fenrock AI

## Overview

Fenrock AI builds the most powerful AI agents for financial crime compliance, specifically tailored for banks and regulated fintechs. The platform enables compliance teams to process 10x their typical workload per analyst while maintaining bullet-proof audit logs for regulators. Fenrock addresses one of the most labor-intensive and regulated areas of financial services -- anti-money laundering (AML), know-your-customer (KYC), and sanctions screening -- by deploying AI agents that can handle the bulk of compliance review work.

## What Makes Fenrock Interesting

1. **Massive TAM in compliance:** Financial institutions spend billions annually on compliance staffing; 10x productivity per analyst is a compelling value proposition.
2. **Regulatory moat:** Deep domain expertise in financial crime compliance creates barriers to entry for generalist AI companies.
3. **Audit-first design:** Built-in audit logs for regulators addresses the key objection to AI in regulated industries.
