# Atlan

## Overview

Atlan is a data and AI governance platform that serves as a "control plane" for enterprise data teams -- providing automated metadata management, data cataloging, lineage tracking, and governance capabilities that enable organizations to discover, trust, and govern their data assets. Atlan has been recognized as a Leader in both the 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Metadata Management Solutions and the 2026 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Data & Analytics Governance.

Founded in 2018 by Prukalpa Sankar and Varun Banka, who previously ran data infrastructure for the Indian Prime Minister's office, Atlan has raised $206 million and was valued at $750 million as of May 2024. The company's revenues grew over 7x in two years, with 400% enterprise sales growth in Q1 2024.

Sources: [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2024/05/08/atlan-scores-105m-for-data-control-plane-just-as-llms-increase-importance-of-data/); [SiliconANGLE](https://siliconangle.com/2024/05/08/atlan-raises-105m-750m-valuation-data-ai-governance-platform/)

## Founding Story & Team

Atlan's origin story is distinctive. Co-founders **Prukalpa Sankar** and **Varun Banka** met at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore in 2012 and co-founded **SocialCops**, a "data for social good" platform that built data infrastructure for the Indian Prime Minister's Office, the United Nations, and NITI Aayog (India's premier policy think tank). Over seven years, they completed approximately 200 data projects at the national scale -- working with messy, multi-source government data across India.

The breaking point came when a key analyst quit days before a major government presentation, taking all institutional knowledge about the project's data with them. Sankar and Banka realized the fundamental problem: data teams did not need just better tools -- they needed a better way to collaborate on and govern their data. The internal tool they built to solve this problem at SocialCops worked so well that they spun it out as Atlan in 2018.

Both co-founders serve as Co-CEOs, an unusual dual-CEO structure. Sankar has been recognized as a Forbes 30 Under 30 honoree, Fortune 40 Under 40, and TED speaker. The company is now headquartered in New York, with a significant engineering presence in India.

## Business Model

Atlan operates a **SaaS subscription model** with pricing based on the number of users and data assets governed. The platform integrates with the modern data stack, sitting as a metadata and governance layer across cloud warehouses, BI tools, ETL pipelines, and ML platforms.

### Core Products & Capabilities

- **Data Catalog**: AI-powered automated discovery and cataloging of data assets across the entire data estate
- **Data Lineage**: Column-level lineage tracking from source systems through transformations to dashboards and ML models
- **AI Governance**: Governance controls specifically designed for AI/ML models and LLM-powered applications -- tracking what data trains which models, managing access controls, and ensuring compliance
- **Data Quality Monitoring**: Integrated data quality checks and anomaly detection
- **Business Glossary**: Collaborative definitions and documentation of business metrics and terminology
- **Persona-Based Access**: Role-based access controls and data masking for compliance (GDPR, HIPAA, etc.)
- **Integrations**: Native connectors for Snowflake, Databricks, BigQuery, dbt, Fivetran, Airflow, Looker, Tableau, Power BI, and 50+ other tools

## What Makes Atlan Notable

1. **Government-scale origin story**: Few enterprise data startups can claim that their product was forged in the fires of managing data infrastructure for a billion-person country. The SocialCops experience gave the founders deep intuition about data governance challenges at extreme scale and messiness.

2. **Gartner Magic Quadrant Leader**: Atlan achieved Leader status in both the Metadata Management and Data & Analytics Governance Gartner Magic Quadrants within a few years of entering the enterprise market -- a remarkable pace for a startup competing against legacy vendors like Informatica, Collibra, and Alation.

3. **AI governance timing**: As enterprises race to deploy LLMs and AI agents, the need for AI governance -- tracking which data trains which models, managing access controls, and ensuring compliance -- has become urgent. Atlan is well-positioned as one of the few modern platforms addressing this need.

4. **7x revenue growth**: Growing revenue 7x in two years demonstrates strong product-market fit and enterprise demand for modern data governance.

5. **Indian founder success story**: Atlan represents the growing pipeline of Indian-founded enterprise SaaS companies that build for global markets from Day 1. The SocialCops-to-Atlan pivot is a textbook example of finding a massive commercial opportunity inside a social impact project.

## Competitive Landscape

- **Collibra**: Incumbent data governance platform; larger but legacy architecture
- **Alation**: Data catalog and governance; strong in data discovery
- **Informatica**: Legacy data management giant with governance capabilities
- **Select Star**: Automated data discovery and lineage
- **Secoda**: AI-powered data catalog for modern data teams
- **Unity Catalog (Databricks)**: Open-source governance catalog tightly integrated with Databricks

## Outlook

Atlan is riding two converging tailwinds: the maturation of the modern data stack (requiring governance layers) and the explosion of AI (requiring AI governance). The company's rapid revenue growth and Gartner recognition position it well for continued expansion. A future funding round could push Atlan into unicorn territory.
