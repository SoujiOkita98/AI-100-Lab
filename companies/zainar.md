# ZaiNar

## Overview

ZaiNar emerged from nine years of stealth on February 19, 2026 with a patented technology platform that turns any wireless network (5G, WiFi) into a spatial sensing system. The company achieves sub-nanosecond time synchronization across wireless networks, enabling every connected device to know where it is relative to other objects with sub-meter accuracy, in real time -- without GPS, cameras, or dedicated sensors.

## Funding Story

The company has accumulated over $100M in investment and reached a $1B+ valuation. The investor list reads like a who's who of Silicon Valley: Steve Jurvetson (SpaceX board), Jerry Yang (Yahoo! co-founder), Tom Gruber (Siri co-founder), Jaan Tallinn (Skype).

## What Makes Them Interesting

1. **Nine years in stealth.** This is an unusually long incubation period, suggesting deep technology development rather than a quick market play.
2. **$450M+ in contracts and MOUs.** Substantial commercial validation before even publicly launching.
3. **GPS alternative.** Indoor and dense-urban positioning where GPS fails is a massive unsolved problem for robotics, logistics, and autonomous systems.
4. **Infrastructure-light approach.** Leverages existing 5G and WiFi networks rather than requiring new hardware installation.
5. **Physical AI enabler.** As robots and autonomous systems proliferate, knowing precise location without line-of-sight sensors becomes critical infrastructure.
