# Notion

## Overview
All-in-one workspace that nearly died — Zhao and Last retreated to Kyoto, Japan in 2015 to rebuild the product from scratch. Relaunched to explosive organic growth starting 2018. Launched Notion AI in early 2023 (LLM integration for writing, summarization, Q&A). Notably capital-efficient — achieved massive scale with a lean team. Ivan Zhao is Chinese-born (Xinjiang), raised in Canada.

## Investors
Coatue, Sequoia Capital, Index Ventures.
