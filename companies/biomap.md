# BioMap

## Overview
BioMap is a Beijing-based AI biotech company co-founded by Baidu founder Robin Li and former Baidu Ventures CEO Wei Liu. The company's flagship product is xTrimo V4, a 268-billion-parameter foundation model designed to decode the underlying principles of biological systems through AI. BioMap challenges Google's AlphaFold in AI-powered protein design and drug discovery. The company has partnered with Sanofi in a deal worth up to $1 billion in milestone payments and serves nearly 1,000 clients. BioMap has operations in Beijing and Hong Kong and is reportedly preparing for a Hong Kong IPO in 2026.
