# Axiomatic AI

## Overview
Axiomatic AI is a Boston-based startup founded in 2024 that builds intelligence infrastructure for verified science and engineering. The company integrates frontier AI models with formal mathematical and physics-based verification to deliver interpretable, provable reasoning for critical engineering applications. Founded by a team of world-class academics from MIT, the University of Toronto, and the Institute of Photonic Sciences, and led by CEO Jake Taylor, Axiomatic AI focuses on semiconductors, photonics, and advanced manufacturing -- domains where AI must operate reliably within physical law. The company raised $25M total, with an $18M seed round led by Engine Ventures in March 2026.
