# Landbase

## One-liner

Agentic AI platform for go-to-market that uses autonomous AI agents to automate the full customer acquisition workflow -- from identifying ideal customer profiles to executing personalized outreach -- built by the former co-CEO of AppDirect (a SaaS unicorn) and seasoned data science and product leaders.

## Founders & Leadership

### Daniel Saks -- Co-founder & CEO

- Co-founder and former **co-CEO of AppDirect**, a SaaS marketplace unicorn valued at over $1B.
- Deep experience scaling enterprise B2B companies from startup to IPO readiness.
- Brings CEO-level understanding of GTM challenges that Landbase aims to solve.

### Emily Zhang -- Co-founder & CPO

- Founding **Chief Product Officer at OysterHR**, a unicorn HR platform.
- Product strategy expertise in building platforms that scale globally.

### Hua Gao -- Co-founder & Chief Data Scientist

- Founding **Chief Data Scientist at EverString** (acquired by ZoomInfo), a Sequoia and Lightspeed-backed B2B data company.
- Deep expertise in B2B data science, predictive analytics, and machine learning applied to sales intelligence.

## Founding Story

Saks, Zhang, and Gao founded Landbase in 2023 after recognizing that despite billions spent on sales and marketing technology, most B2B companies still relied on fragmented, manual processes to find and convert customers. Saks experienced this firsthand while scaling AppDirect; Zhang saw it from the product side at OysterHR; and Gao had built the data infrastructure at EverString (later ZoomInfo) that powered sales intelligence. Together, they set out to build an "agentic AI" platform that could autonomously handle the entire customer acquisition workflow.

## Product & Business Model

### What Landbase Does

Landbase is an **agentic AI GTM platform** that:

1. **Identifies ideal customers** using AI agents that analyze market data, company signals, and behavioral patterns to find high-fit prospects.
2. **Researches and enriches accounts** by pulling data from multiple sources to build comprehensive prospect profiles.
3. **Generates personalized outreach** using AI that crafts contextually relevant messages across email, phone, and other channels.
4. **Executes and optimizes campaigns** with autonomous agents that manage timing, follow-ups, and A/B testing without human intervention.
5. **Provides revenue intelligence** with analytics and insights on campaign performance and pipeline generation.

### Pricing / Monetization

Enterprise SaaS model. Specific pricing undisclosed but targets mid-market and enterprise B2B companies looking to consolidate their GTM technology stack.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning |
|-----------|------------|
| **Clay** | Data enrichment and GTM workflow automation; more manual/builder-oriented |
| **11x** | AI SDR digital workers; focused on outbound execution |
| **Apollo.io** | Sales intelligence + sequences; legacy approach with AI additions |
| **Outreach / Salesloft** | Sales engagement platforms; incumbents adding AI |

Landbase differentiates by offering an **end-to-end agentic platform** that handles the full GTM workflow autonomously, rather than focusing on a single step (like AI SDRs) or requiring users to build their own workflows (like Clay).

## What Makes Landbase Interesting

1. **Unicorn founder credibility.** Daniel Saks co-founded and scaled AppDirect to unicorn status, giving him rare firsthand experience with the GTM challenges Landbase solves. Investors bet on founders who have done it before.
2. **Full-stack founding team.** A CEO (Saks), CPO (Zhang), and Chief Data Scientist (Gao) with directly relevant backgrounds is an unusually well-constructed founding team for this market.
3. **ZoomInfo DNA.** Hua Gao built the data science behind EverString/ZoomInfo; Landbase inherits that expertise in B2B data quality and predictive modeling.
4. **825% growth signal.** Near-10x revenue growth in less than a year is extraordinary, even by AI startup standards, and suggests the product is hitting a nerve.
5. **Agentic AI framing.** Landbase is positioned at the intersection of two hot themes -- agentic AI and GTM automation -- which attracts both investor and customer interest.

## Risks & Open Questions

- **Revenue base unclear.** 825% growth sounds impressive, but if the base was small (e.g., $100K), the absolute numbers may be modest. 150 customers is early.
- **Crowded GTM AI market.** The space is becoming saturated with well-funded competitors attacking various parts of the workflow.
- **Platform vs. point solution tension.** Full-stack platforms are harder to build and sell than focused tools; Landbase needs to avoid being "good at everything, great at nothing."
- **Sound Ventures as lead.** While a capable fund, Sound Ventures is less of a traditional enterprise software investor compared to the investors backing competitors (Sequoia, a16z, Benchmark).
