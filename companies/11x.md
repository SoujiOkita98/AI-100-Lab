# 11x

## One-liner

AI digital worker platform that replaces traditional SDR headcount with autonomous agents (Alice for outbound sales, Mike for phone calls) to automate go-to-market workflows at a fraction of the cost.

## Founding Story

Founded in London in 2022, 11x set out to build autonomous AI agents that function as digital employees rather than software tools. The company quickly attracted attention from top-tier investors and relocated its headquarters to San Francisco to be at the center of the AI industry. The core thesis is that the future of work is not about better software but about digital workers that can independently execute complex tasks like sales prospecting.

## Product & Business Model

### What 11x Does

11x builds AI-powered "digital workers" for go-to-market teams:

1. **Alice** -- AI Sales Development Representative that automates prospecting, multichannel outreach, and personalized engagement at scale. Alice researches leads, crafts personalized messages, and manages follow-ups autonomously.
2. **Mike** -- AI Phone Representative that handles both inbound lead qualification and consented outbound calling 24/7, scaling instantly without human limitations.

### Pricing / Monetization

11x operates on a SaaS subscription model where companies pay for digital workers rather than hiring human SDRs. Pricing is typically a fraction of a fully loaded SDR salary ($60K-$100K+/year), making the ROI case straightforward for sales leaders.

## What Makes 11x Interesting

1. **Digital worker framing.** Instead of selling software, 11x sells outcomes -- a fully autonomous employee replacement. This reframes the budget conversation from "software spend" to "headcount savings."
2. **Speed of fundraising.** Raised Series A and Series B within two months of each other (Sep and Nov 2024), reflecting intense investor demand for the AI SDR category.
3. **Revenue velocity.** Growing from ~$10M to ~$25M ARR in a single quarter is exceptional, even by AI startup standards.
4. **Multi-modal expansion.** Moving from text (Alice) to voice (Mike) signals a platform play across all GTM communication channels.

## Risks & Open Questions

- **Deliverability and spam concerns.** Mass AI-generated outbound emails risk domain reputation issues and regulatory scrutiny.
- **Customer retention.** AI SDR tools face churn risk if response rates decline as recipients learn to identify AI-generated outreach.
- **Valuation premium.** At ~$350M on ~$25M ARR (~14x), the valuation assumes continued hypergrowth.
- **Competitive crowding.** The AI SDR space is becoming densely populated with well-funded competitors.
