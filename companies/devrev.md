# DevRev -- AI-Native CRM & Developer Platform

## Company Overview

DevRev is an AI-native platform that unifies customer support and product development workflows. The company's thesis is that traditional CRM and ticketing systems create artificial boundaries between customer-facing teams and engineering teams. DevRev uses AI agents to bridge this gap, automatically routing customer feedback to relevant product teams, triaging support tickets, and connecting customer conversations to engineering work items.

Founded by Nutanix co-founder Dheeraj Pandey, DevRev represents a second-act startup from one of India's most prominent enterprise technology founders.

## Founders and Team

### Dheeraj Pandey -- Co-Founder & CEO
- **Education:** B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Kanpur (1997).
- **Origin:** Born in Bihar, India. Came to the US with reportedly $900 in his pocket.
- **Prior experience:** Co-founded **Nutanix** in 2009 with Ajeet Singh and Mohit Aron. Served as CEO from founding through IPO (2016, NASDAQ: NTNX) and until 2020. Nutanix reached a market cap exceeding $18B. Before Nutanix, worked at Oracle.

### Manoj Agarwal -- Co-Founder & CTO
- **Origin:** India. Former SVP of Engineering at Nutanix, where he led large-scale distributed systems engineering.

**Founder Pattern:** Dheeraj Pandey is a textbook "Indian-American serial founder" -- IIT education, immigration to the US, building a first company to massive scale (Nutanix: $18B+ market cap), then starting again. Notably, his Nutanix co-founder Ajeet Singh also founded ThoughtSpot (already in database) and Mohit Aron founded Cohesity. This "founder cluster" effect -- where one successful company's founding team spawns multiple unicorns -- is a key pattern in the Indian AI founder ecosystem.

## What Makes DevRev Interesting

1. **Serial Indian founder with proven track record:** Pandey took Nutanix from founding to IPO and $18B+ market cap. The $50M seed round reflects investor confidence in his execution ability.
2. **$900 to $18B (twice?):** Pandey's origin story -- arriving in the US from Bihar with $900 and building a publicly traded company -- is one of the most compelling Indian-American founder narratives.
3. **Unicorn on Series A:** Achieving $1.15B valuation on a Series A is extremely rare and almost entirely attributable to founder reputation.
4. **Nutanix-ThoughtSpot founder cluster:** All three Nutanix co-founders went on to found unicorn-scale companies -- an extraordinary cluster.
5. **India-first R&D model:** Unlike Nutanix, DevRev started with India-based R&D from day one.
