# Momenta

## Overview

Momenta is a Beijing-based autonomous driving company that develops AI software for self-driving vehicles, spanning both L2+ driver-assistance systems (ADAS) and full L4 autonomous driving. Founded in 2016, Momenta has raised $1.42 billion and is backed by strategic investors including General Motors, Mercedes-Benz, Tencent, and SAIC Motor.

The company filed for a Hong Kong IPO in March 2026, seeking to raise approximately $1 billion at a valuation around $6 billion. Momenta is distinguished from peers by its partnerships with major global automakers and its plan to launch Uber-powered robotaxi services in Europe in 2026. [Caproasia](https://www.caproasia.com/2026/03/08/china-autonomous-driving-ai-company-beijing-momenta-technology-files-for-hong-kong-ipo-to-raise-1-billion-fundraising-at-6-billion-valuation-in-2025-founded-in-2016-by-ex-microsoft-ai-engineers-le/)

## Founder: Cao Xudong

Cao Xudong founded Momenta in 2016 after working as a **researcher at Microsoft Research in Beijing** and at **SenseTime**. His background combines deep learning research (Microsoft) with computer vision applications (SenseTime), positioning him at the intersection of AI research and real-world deployment.

Momenta's founding team consisted of ex-Microsoft AI engineers, giving the company strong technical foundations in perception, planning, and machine learning. [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2025-09-26/self-driving-firm-momenta-seeks-funding-at-5-billion-plus-value)

## Key Partnerships and Business Model

Momenta's business model is centered on licensing its autonomous driving software to automakers and ride-hailing platforms:

### Uber Partnership (2025-2026)
In September 2025, Uber and Momenta announced a partnership to deploy Momenta-powered autonomous vehicles on Uber's European platform. The initial launch was planned for Munich, Germany, in early 2026, with human safety operators on board. This represents one of the first Chinese autonomous driving companies to deploy in Europe. [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2025/09/07/uber-and-momenta-to-test-autonomous-vehicles-in-germany-in-2026/)

## What Makes Momenta Interesting (GSB Research Perspective)

1. **Chinese AV tech going global via Uber.** Most Chinese autonomous driving companies operate primarily domestically. Momenta's Uber partnership represents a new model for Chinese AV technology reaching Western markets through established platform partners.

2. **"Two-leg" strategy.** Momenta simultaneously pursues L2+ ADAS (for near-term OEM revenue) and L4 full autonomy (for long-term robotaxi deployment), sharing data and models across both tracks. This mirrors Mobileye's strategy and provides revenue while building toward full autonomy.

3. **Microsoft Research + SenseTime founder DNA.** Cao Xudong's path from Microsoft Research to SenseTime to founding Momenta illustrates the Chinese AI talent pipeline: Western research labs -> Chinese AI companies -> specialized startups.

4. **Automaker-as-investor model.** Having GM, Mercedes-Benz, and SAIC as both investors and customers creates strong commercial alignment but also potential conflicts as these OEMs develop their own AV capabilities.

## Key Risks

- Autonomous driving commercialization timeline remains uncertain globally
- Intense competition from Pony.ai, WeRide, Huawei ADS, and Tesla FSD
- Regulatory complexity of deploying in multiple countries
- Revenue model dependent on OEM adoption cycles
