# Iris.ai

## Overview

Iris.ai is a Norwegian AI company founded in 2015 that builds AI-powered science assistants for R&D departments. The company helps researchers and scientists navigate, search, and understand scientific text content at scale. Iris.ai combines its own proprietary scientific language models -- developed in-house over eight years -- with generative AI modules to provide smart search, reading list analysis, auto-generated summaries, autonomous extraction, and data systematization. The company has raised $21.6M in total funding across 6 rounds.

## Product

**RSpace** is Iris.ai's core product offering:
- Smart search with advanced filtering across scientific literature
- Reading list analysis and auto-generated summaries
- Autonomous data extraction and systematization
- Integration of proprietary scientific NLP models with generative AI

The company was selected by the prestigious EIC Accelerator (European Innovation Council) to use AI for accelerated scientific text understanding.
