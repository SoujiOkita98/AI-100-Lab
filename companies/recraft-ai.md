# Recraft

## Overview

Recraft is an AI image generation company founded in 2022 by Anna Veronika Dorogush, a machine learning scientist. The company's model topped the Artificial Analysis text-to-image ELO benchmark, beating DALL-E and Midjourney. Recraft focuses on professional design use cases with brand control, vector graphics generation, and high-quality image outputs. The company has over 4 million users and hit $8.4M in revenue by September 2025.
