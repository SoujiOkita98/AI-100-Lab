# Third Dimension AI

## Overview

Third Dimension AI was founded in 2024 by Tolga Kart, Piotr Sokolski, and Soner Ozgun Pelvan. The company creates large-scale, usable 3D environments — whether digital twins of real places or entirely virtual worlds — using generative AI. Their technology eliminates the need for complex 3D modeling software.

## Funding

Third Dimension raised a $7M seed round led by Felicis Ventures, with participation from Abstract Ventures, MVP, Soma Capital, and Solari Capital.

## Key Details

- Founded by ex-video game and autonomous vehicle experts
- Serves industries ranging from gaming and film to autonomous vehicles and defense
- Generates quality 3D environments from text prompts and images
