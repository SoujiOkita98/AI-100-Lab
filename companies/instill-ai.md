# Instill AI

## Overview

Instill AI is an open-source AI infrastructure company founded in 2020 by Ping-Lin Chang (PhD, Imperial College London; previously co-founded Umbo Computer Vision) and Xiaofei Du. Based in London with offices in Barcelona and Taipei, the company builds no/low-code AI and data pipelines that enable users to extract insights from unstructured data types including text, images, audio, and video. Total funding is approximately $3.7M from a seed round led by RTP Global.
