# WeRide

## Overview
WeRide is a Guangzhou-based autonomous driving company and the first publicly listed universal autonomous driving and robotaxi company. Founded in Silicon Valley in 2017 by Tony Han (former Baidu chief scientist), WeRide operates robotaxis, robobuses, robosweepers, and robovans across multiple cities. The company dual-listed on Nasdaq (2024) and Hong Kong Stock Exchange (2025), raising over $700M from public markets. WeRide is backed by Temasek, Tencent, Nissan, Renault, and has NVIDIA as a shareholder. The company has R&D centers in Guangzhou, Beijing, Shanghai, San Jose, Abu Dhabi, and Singapore.
