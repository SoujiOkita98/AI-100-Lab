# Lepton AI

## Overview

Lepton AI was a GPU cloud infrastructure startup founded by Yangqing Jia, one of the most influential Chinese-born AI researchers in the world. The company focused on renting NVIDIA GPU servers and developing cloud management software to help AI startups build, manage, and optimize AI applications. NVIDIA acquired Lepton AI in 2025 for several hundred million dollars.

## Founder

### Yangqing Jia (Jia Yangqing) -- Co-Founder & CEO

- **Origin:** Mainland Chinese. Undergraduate at Tsinghua University (one of China's most elite institutions). PhD in EECS from UC Berkeley.
- **Creator of Caffe:** Jia created the Caffe deep learning framework during his PhD at Berkeley -- one of the foundational deep learning frameworks that helped launch the modern AI era. Caffe was widely used before PyTorch and TensorFlow became dominant.
- **Co-creator of ONNX and PyTorch 1.0:** Contributed to building the open-source AI framework ecosystem at Facebook (now Meta).
- **Google Brain:** Conducted research at Google Brain before joining Facebook AI.
- **Facebook AI Infrastructure:** Led AI infrastructure efforts at Facebook/Meta.
- **Alibaba VP of Technology:** Served as Alibaba's Vice President of Technology from March 2019, leading Alibaba Cloud's AI strategy, before leaving in March 2023 to found Lepton AI.

### Bai Junjie -- Co-Founder

- **Origin:** Mainland Chinese.
- Both co-founders joined NVIDIA after the acquisition.

## Chinese Founder Context

Yangqing Jia is arguably one of the most technically influential Chinese-born AI engineers in the world. His creation of the Caffe framework was a catalytic moment in the deep learning revolution. His career arc -- from Tsinghua to Berkeley to Google Brain to Facebook AI to Alibaba VP to founding Lepton AI to joining NVIDIA -- represents the quintessential path of elite Chinese AI talent moving between China and the US.

## Technology & Business

Lepton AI provided GPU-as-a-service infrastructure specifically optimized for AI workloads. The company made it simple for startups and researchers to access NVIDIA GPU compute through a clean API and cloud management layer.

## Acquisition by NVIDIA

NVIDIA acquired Lepton AI in April 2025 for several hundred million dollars, with approximately 20 employees joining NVIDIA. The acquisition reflected NVIDIA's strategy to vertically integrate cloud infrastructure capabilities and absorb top AI talent.

## What Makes Lepton AI Interesting

1. **Legendary founder.** Yangqing Jia's creation of Caffe was a foundational contribution to modern AI. His decision to leave an Alibaba VP role to build a startup in 2023 signaled conviction about the AI infrastructure opportunity.
2. **Tsinghua-to-Berkeley pipeline.** Jia represents the most elite version of the Chinese-to-US AI talent pipeline.
3. **NVIDIA acquisition validates thesis.** The rapid acquisition by NVIDIA at a significant multiple on $11M raised demonstrates the strategic value of AI infrastructure talent.
4. **China-US bridge figure.** Having led AI at both Facebook and Alibaba, Jia had unmatched cross-Pacific AI expertise.
