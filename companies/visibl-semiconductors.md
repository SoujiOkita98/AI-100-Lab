# Visibl Semiconductors

## Overview

Visibl Semiconductors is building the AI IDE for chip design, applying the same AI-native development paradigm that transformed software engineering (via tools like Cursor and Windsurf) to the semiconductor industry. The platform aims to dramatically accelerate chip design workflows by integrating AI assistants directly into the hardware design environment. This addresses a critical bottleneck in the semiconductor industry, where chip design cycles can take years and require highly specialized engineers.

## What Makes Visibl Interesting

2. **Massive market:** The EDA (electronic design automation) market is $15B+ and ripe for AI-native disruption.
3. **Strategic timing:** As demand for custom AI chips surges, tools that accelerate chip design become increasingly critical.
