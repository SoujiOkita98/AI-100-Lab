# Klarity

## One-liner

AI-powered document review platform that automates contract analysis and revenue operations workflows, achieving 85%+ pass-through rates on document processing.

## Founders & Leadership

### Andrew Antos -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Harvard Law School graduate with Biglaw experience.
- Met Nischal Nadhamuni in MIT class 15.390 (New Enterprises) in 2017, where they formed the team that became Klarity.

### Nischal Nadhamuni -- Co-Founder & CTO

- MIT Computer Science graduate with AI specialization.
- Named to Forbes 30 Under 30 list.

### Logan Ford -- Co-Founder

- Technical co-founder.

## Product & Business Model

Klarity uses AI to process data in contracts and internal records, eliminating manual document review. The platform automates revenue-critical document workflows for enterprise customers, including order forms, contracts, and compliance documents. The company is a Y Combinator alum.
