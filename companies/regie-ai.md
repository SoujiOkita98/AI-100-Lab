# Regie.ai

## One-liner

AI-native sales engagement platform that combines AI-generated content, autonomous prospecting agents, and human-driven outreach into a unified system (RegieOne) -- designed to help enterprise B2B sales teams expand their addressable market while reducing technology costs.

## Founders & Leadership

### Dr. Srinath Sridhar -- Co-founder & CTO

- AI scientist with a PhD background in machine learning.
- Previously developed enterprise-scale ML systems at **Google** and **Meta (Facebook)**.
- Co-founded **Onera** before starting Regie.ai.
- Brings deep technical expertise in NLP and content generation.

### Matt Millen -- Co-founder & CEO

- Veteran sales leader who was **Head of Revenue at Outreach.io**, one of the largest sales engagement platforms.
- Experienced firsthand the limitations of existing sales engagement tools, which inspired Regie.ai's founding.
- Deep network in enterprise sales and revenue operations.

## Founding Story

Millen and Sridhar teamed up in June 2021 (company incorporated 2020) after recognizing a convergence: Millen saw from his time at Outreach that sales reps spent enormous amounts of time writing emails and researching prospects, while Sridhar saw that AI had reached the point where it could generate high-quality sales content at scale. They founded Regie.ai to bridge AI-powered content generation with sales engagement execution, creating a platform that could both write and send the right message to the right prospect at the right time.

## Product & Business Model

### What Regie.ai Does

Regie.ai has evolved from an AI content generation tool into a full **AI-native sales engagement platform** called **RegieOne**:

1. **AI Content Generation.** Creates personalized sales emails, sequences, and messaging at scale using AI trained on successful sales interactions.
2. **Autonomous Prospecting Agents.** AI agents that identify target accounts, research prospects, and execute outreach with minimal human intervention.
3. **Human-in-the-Loop Orchestration.** Unlike fully autonomous AI SDR tools (11x, Artisan), Regie.ai keeps human reps in the loop for high-value interactions, using AI to handle research and initial outreach.
4. **Unified Platform (RegieOne).** Consolidates prospecting, sequencing, content, and analytics into one platform, reducing the need for multiple point solutions.

### Pricing / Monetization

Enterprise SaaS model with tiered pricing based on team size and usage. Positioned to replace multiple tools in the sales stack (outreach, content, prospecting) with a single platform.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning |
|-----------|------------|
| **Outreach** | Incumbent sales engagement platform; co-founder Matt Millen's former employer |
| **Salesloft** (merged with Clari) | Sales engagement + revenue intelligence; recently merged |
| **11x (Alice)** | Fully autonomous AI SDR; no human-in-the-loop |
| **Artisan (Ava)** | Fully autonomous AI BDR |
| **Apollo.io** | Sales intelligence + sequences + AI features |
| **Copy.ai** | AI content generation; more marketing-focused |

Regie.ai differentiates through its **human-in-the-loop philosophy** (AI handles volume, humans handle quality) and its **platform consolidation play** (replacing Outreach + content tools + prospecting tools with one system).

## What Makes Regie.ai Interesting

1. **"Outreach insider" disruption.** The CEO built Outreach's revenue engine and is now building the AI-native replacement. This is a classic pattern of an insider seeing the next wave before the incumbent.
2. **Human-in-the-loop differentiation.** While 11x and Artisan bet on full automation, Regie.ai bets that enterprise sales requires human judgment for high-stakes deals. Both could be right for different market segments.
3. **Platform consolidation thesis.** RegieOne aims to replace 3-5 tools in the sales stack, which could drive strong expansion revenue and reduce churn.
4. **AI scientist + sales leader founding team.** The Sridhar-Millen combination (deep ML expertise + enterprise sales leadership) is an unusually well-matched founding pair for this market.

## Risks & Open Questions

- **Revenue scale.** At ~$3.8M ARR estimated, Regie.ai is smaller than some competitors; the 300% growth rate needs to be sustained.
- **Human-in-the-loop limitation.** If fully autonomous SDRs (11x, Artisan) prove effective enough, the human-in-the-loop approach may be seen as a half-measure.
- **Enterprise sales cycle.** Selling to large B2B companies is slow and expensive; customer acquisition costs could be high.
- **Outreach/Salesloft-Clari response.** Incumbents are adding AI features and consolidating (Salesloft merged with Clari in Dec 2025), which could squeeze Regie.ai's positioning.
