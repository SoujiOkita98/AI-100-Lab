# Multiverse Computing

## Overview
Multiverse Computing is a Spanish quantum-inspired AI company that developed CompactifAI, a breakthrough technology capable of compressing large language models by up to 95% with only 2-3% precision loss. Founded in 2019, the company holds over 160 patents and serves 100+ customers globally including Iberdrola, Bosch, and the Bank of Canada. The Series B round at a 5x valuation jump positions Multiverse as a key player in making AI deployment more efficient and cost-effective across industries. The company has operations in Spain, Canada, France, and Germany.
