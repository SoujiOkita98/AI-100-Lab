# Coveo

## Overview
Coveo Solutions (TSX: CVO) is a Montreal-based AI enterprise search and relevance company that uses generative AI and agentic capabilities to optimize digital experiences. Listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange, Coveo was named a Leader in the 2025 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Search and Product Discovery. In December 2025, the Government of Canada signed an MOU with Coveo to advance AI adoption in government. The company also announced a strategic alliance with Deloitte. Coveo serves enterprise clients across commerce, customer service, and workplace search.
