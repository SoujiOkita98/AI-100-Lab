# Jarvis ML (Aidaptive)

## Overview

Jarvis ML, now operating commercially as Aidaptive, is a Cupertino, California-based AI personalization startup founded in 2021 by Rakesh Yadav. The company provides an end-to-end machine learning platform that enables e-commerce and hospitality businesses to deploy AI-powered recommendation and personalization engines within minutes. In November 2022, the company launched Aidaptive as its commercial brand while Jarvis ML continued as the underlying ML technology platform. The company raised $16M in seed funding in April 2022.

## Founder

**Rakesh Yadav** - Founder & CEO. Spent 14 years at Google where he led the development of the Machine Learning Operations platform behind Google Ads and Google Payments as a Senior Staff Engineer. The Aidaptive team has 50+ years of combined machine learning experience.

## Product

Aidaptive, powered by Jarvis ML, provides:
- SaaS end-to-end ML platform for personalization
- AI-powered recommendation engines deployable in minutes
- Personalized customer experiences across online and offline touchpoints
- Enterprise-grade ML capabilities accessible to mid-market businesses

**Target markets:** E-commerce and hospitality, with the platform designed to help sellers and service providers personalize customer experiences through all brand touchpoints.
