# GroupUltra

## Overview
Originally founded as Beijing Jiqun Technology (极群科技), operating as GroupUltra Limited, with products including Intent (AI Messenger), Moobius (open-source multi-agent SDK used by Tsinghua University), and Riffo (AI file organization). The company blends real-time messaging, multimodal context, and intelligent agents.

## Notes
- Originally founded in Beijing (2022), expanded to Sunnyvale, CA in 2024
- Miracle Plus (formerly YC China) backed
- Both founders are Tsinghua University graduates
