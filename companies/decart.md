# Decart

## One-liner

Real-time world model and generative video platform enabling interactive AI-generated experiences, built on GPU-optimized livestream diffusion models.

## Founders & Leadership

### Dean Leitersdorf -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Completed PhD in Computer Science at age 23; awarded the ACM PODC Dissertation Award.
- Began undergraduate studies at 17 while waiting for IDF draft into Unit 8200.
- Postdoctoral researcher at the National University of Singapore before pivoting to AI after ChatGPT launched.

### Moshe Shalev -- Co-Founder & CPO

- Grew up in a Haredi (ultra-Orthodox) household in Bnei Brak, Israel.
- Enlisted in IDF at 23 via the Bina Beyarok program and served 13+ years in Unit 8200, eventually running AI operations.
- Self-taught in computer science during military service.

## Founding Story

Leitersdorf and Shalev met during their service in Israel's elite Unit 8200 intelligence unit. Despite vastly different backgrounds, they co-founded Decart in September 2023 with a focus on building real-time generative AI models. The company emerged from stealth in late 2024.

## Product & Business Model

Decart builds real-time generative video technology with extreme GPU optimization. Its flagship model, Oasis (launched November 2024), is a "livestream diffusion model" that reached over 1 million users within three days of release. The company licenses its optimization technology to cloud providers and AI laboratories through multimillion-dollar contracts. Despite raising $153M, Decart has spent less than $10M of investor capital.
