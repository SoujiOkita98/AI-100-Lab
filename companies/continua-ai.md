# Continua AI

## One-liner

Social AI platform that brings always-on AI agents into group chats on SMS, iMessage, and Discord to enhance collaboration, set reminders, launch polls, and manage plans.

## Founders & Leadership

### David Petrou -- Founder & CEO
- Former Google Distinguished Engineer with deep expertise in computer vision and AI.
- Helped start Google Goggles, one of Google's early computer vision products.

## Product & Business Model

Continua develops an always-on personal helper designed to enhance collaboration and productivity within group chats. As groups discuss projects and common plans, Continua can automatically set reminders, launch polls, add calendar invitations, or generate documents with checklists and to-do lists.
