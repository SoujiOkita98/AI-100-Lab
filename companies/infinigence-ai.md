# Infinigence AI

## Overview
Infinigence AI (also known by its Chinese name Wuwen Xinqiong) is a Shanghai-based AI infrastructure provider founded in May 2023. The company focuses on heterogeneous AI computing solutions, aggregating computing resources across 53 data centers in 26 Chinese cities. Its platform optimizes large model energy efficiency and provides MaaS (Model-as-a-Service) capabilities. Clients include Lenovo, Baichuan Intelligence, and China Mobile. Infinigence has raised approximately 1.5 billion yuan to date and was named one of four Chinese AI startups to watch beyond DeepSeek by MIT Technology Review.
