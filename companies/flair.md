# Flair AI

## Overview

Flair AI is a San Francisco-based generative AI startup that has built a design platform for digitizing product photoshoots from end-to-end. Founded in 2022 by Mickey Friedman and Antonio Cao, the platform allows users to create professional-quality product photography using AI, eliminating the need for costly physical photoshoots. The company has grown to over 2 million users and raised $5M in seed funding.

## Founders

- **Mickey Friedman** - Co-Founder & CEO. University of Chicago graduate who co-launched Flair.ai in 2022. Active in the AI community, organizing San Francisco's AI Tinkerers events.

- **Antonio Cao** - Co-Founder & President. Yale University graduate with degrees in Physics, Mathematics, and Computer Science. Previously worked with AI teams at companies including Tesla and Adobe.

The founders met through one of the earliest generative art communities.

## Product

Flair AI provides a comprehensive AI-powered product photography platform:
- Drag-and-drop scene builder with images, props, and 3D assets
- Adjustable lighting and camera angles
- AI-generated model try-on for clothing
- Brand-consistent visual content generation at scale

The platform targets e-commerce brands, marketers, and designers who need to rapidly produce high-quality product imagery.
