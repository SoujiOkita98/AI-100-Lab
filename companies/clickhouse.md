# ClickHouse

## Overview
Fastest-growing open-source real-time analytics database. Originally created by Alexey Milovidov at Yandex ~2009, open-sourced 2016. Company incorporated 2021. ClickHouse Cloud launched late 2022 with explosive growth. Customers include Anthropic, Tesla, Meta, Sony, Lyft, Instacart. Acquired Langfuse (LLM observability) alongside Series D.

## Investors
Dragoneer, Benchmark, Index Ventures, Khosla Ventures, Coatue, Altimeter, Lightspeed, GIC, FirstMark, Nebius, T. Rowe Price.
