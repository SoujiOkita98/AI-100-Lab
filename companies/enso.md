# Enso

## Overview

Enso is a Tel Aviv-based startup that emerged from stealth in July 2024 with $6M in seed funding, aiming to make AI agents accessible to small and medium businesses (SMBs). Founded by Mickey Haslavsky, co-founder of API platform RapidAPI, Enso takes an industry-specific approach by offering over 1,000 preprogrammed AI agents across approximately 70 different verticals.

## Founder

**Mickey Haslavsky** is the founder and CEO. He previously co-founded RapidAPI, one of the world's largest API development platforms, giving him deep experience in building developer platforms and marketplace businesses at scale.

## Product

Enso's "Guided AI Agents" platform delivers consistent, predictable results through targeted, industry-specific, and predefined libraries of templates, components, and software integrations. Use cases include:
- SEO management
- Instagram follower engagement
- Competitor tracking
- Newsletter writing
- Invoice management
- Amazon store optimization

Pricing follows an a la carte model at $29-$79 per agent per month.
