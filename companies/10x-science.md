# 10x Science

## Overview

10x Science develops frontier AI models with deep memory to redefine how scientists understand and engineer biology, starting with next-generation protein characterization. The platform aims to compress the cycle of biological discovery by applying AI to understand protein structure, function, and interactions at a level that traditional experimental methods cannot achieve alone. The company sits at the intersection of two major trends: AI for science and computational biology.
