# Elicit

## One-liner

AI research assistant that automates scientific literature review and evidence-based reasoning, spun out from the Ought nonprofit focused on scalable AI alignment.

## Founders & Leadership

### Andreas Stuhlmuller -- Co-Founder & CEO

- PhD in Cognitive Science from MIT; postdoctoral researcher in Computation and Cognition at Stanford.
- Co-created WebPPL, a probabilistic programming language for machine learning.
- Started thinking about automating reasoning as a teenager; published first paper on generative learning processes in 2010.
- Founded Ought in 2017, an AI research nonprofit exploring factored cognition and process supervision. Spun out Elicit as a public benefit corporation.

## Product & Business Model

Elicit helps researchers and professionals automate literature review, extract structured data from papers, and conduct evidence-based reasoning. The platform uses LLMs to search, summarize, and synthesize scientific literature at scale. Originally focused on academia, the company is expanding into broader industries that require evidence-based decision-making.
