# Archetype AI

## Overview

Archetype AI is a physical AI company founded by former Google ATAP scientists, designers, and engineers. Led by CEO Ivan Poupyrev (20+ years at Google ATAP, Walt Disney Imagineering, Sony), the company builds foundation models that interpret real-world sensor data to power autonomous physical agents. Their technology enables AI systems to understand and act in the physical world using data from cameras, LiDAR, radar, and other sensors. Key investors include Bezos Expeditions, Venrock, Amazon Industrial Innovation Fund, Samsung Ventures, and Hitachi Ventures.
