# MAIHEM

## Overview

MAIHEM builds AI agents specialized in testing and quality-assuring other AI products, particularly conversational AI applications like chatbots. The company automates AI quality assurance, enabling continuous testing of AI-driven applications. Founded by two PhDs with deep AI and NLP expertise from Oxford, Imperial College, and MIT.

## Founders

- **Max Ahrens** (CEO) — PhD and Postdoc in NLP at University of Oxford; previously McKinsey consultant
- **Eduardo Candela** (CTO) — PhD in AI Safety for Autonomous Vehicles at Imperial College London; MSc in Operations Research from MIT; previously Technical Program Manager at Tesla
