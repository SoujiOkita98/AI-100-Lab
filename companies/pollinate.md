# Pollinate

## Overview

Pollinate builds AI agents for the supply chain, automating procurement, logistics, and vendor management workflows. Supply chain management is one of the most complex and data-intensive business functions, involving coordination across multiple parties, geographies, and systems. Pollinate's AI agents aim to replace manual workflows in areas like purchase order processing, shipment tracking, demand forecasting, and supplier communication. The global supply chain management market exceeds $20B and remains heavily dependent on manual processes and legacy software.
