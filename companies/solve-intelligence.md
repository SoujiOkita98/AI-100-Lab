# Solve Intelligence

## One-liner

AI platform specialized for patents, used by 400+ IP teams across six continents for patent drafting, prosecution, harvesting, office actions, and global filings coordination.

## Founders & Leadership

### Dr. Chris Parsonson -- Co-Founder & CEO
- Co-founded Solve Intelligence with his brother Angus. Y Combinator alumni.

### Angus Parsonson -- Co-Founder & CTO
- Brother of Chris. Technical co-founder. Y Combinator alumni.

### Dr. Sanj Ahilan -- Co-Founder & Chief Research Officer
- Leads research and development at Solve Intelligence.

## Product & Business Model

Solve Intelligence builds AI software specialized for patents. Customers use the platform for collecting ideas from inventors, drafting patent applications, responding to office actions, handling continuations and divisionals, and coordinating global patent filings. The platform serves 400+ IP teams across six continents. The company is profitable and has generated more cash than it has raised since inception.
