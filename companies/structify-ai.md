# Structify

## One-liner

AI-powered platform that automates the gathering, cleaning, and structuring of unstructured web data into enterprise-ready datasets using a proprietary visual language model called DoRa.

## Founders & Leadership

### Alex Reichenbach -- Co-Founder & CEO
- Top CS student in his class. Received his first patent at 17.
- Deep experience at Matician in SLAM and translating data into action.

### Alex Goldstein -- Co-Founder & CTO
- Experience at Matician in SLAM and data-to-action workflows.

### Ronak Gandhi -- Co-Founder & COO
- Met Reichenbach on their first day of college. Diverse academic background spanning CS and humanities.

## Product & Business Model

Structify uses a proprietary visual language model called DoRa to automate the gathering, cleaning, and structuring of data -- a process that typically consumes up to 80% of data scientists' time. Finance teams use it for pitch deck extraction, construction companies turn geotechnical documents into readable tables, and sales teams gather real-time organizational charts.
