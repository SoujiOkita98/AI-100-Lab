# Nscale

## Overview

Nscale is a London-based AI infrastructure company that designs, builds, and operates GPU data centers for AI workloads. Founded in 2024, it has become Europe's most valuable AI infrastructure startup after raising the largest Series C in European history. The company offers sovereign AI compute infrastructure and is a key player in Europe's push for AI compute independence from US hyperscalers.

## Founding Story and Team Origins

Nscale was founded in 2024 by Josh Payne, a British-born serial entrepreneur with an unconventional background. Payne started working in coal mines, where he filled downtime reading entrepreneurship books. He went on to build companies across recruitment, capital markets, and supercomputing infrastructure. Before Nscale, Payne ran Arkon Energy, a Bitcoin mining company focused on renewably powered infrastructure. Nscale was spun off from Arkon Energy to pivot the data center expertise toward AI compute.

Payne has led the design and construction of 54 data centres, including some of Europe's largest, and has contributed to projects like the London Stock Exchange's data infrastructure.

The board includes high-profile appointments: Sheryl Sandberg (former Meta COO), Susan Decker (former Yahoo President), and Nick Clegg (former UK Deputy PM and Meta President of Global Affairs).

## Business Model

- **GPU cloud infrastructure:** Provides access to NVIDIA GPU clusters for AI training and inference
- **Sovereign AI compute:** Positions as a European-first alternative for organizations with data sovereignty requirements
- **Data center development:** Builds and operates large-scale facilities across Europe and expanding to the Nordics (Norway)
- **Full-stack platform:** Hardware + software stack optimized for AI workloads
