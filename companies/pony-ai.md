# Pony.ai

## Overview
Pony.ai is a Chinese autonomous vehicle technology company that has dual-listed on Nasdaq and the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. Founded in 2016 by former Baidu executives, the company operates fully driverless Gen-7 commercial robotaxi services across Guangzhou, Shenzhen, and Beijing. The Hong Kong IPO raised HKD 6.45 billion ($830M), far exceeding its Nasdaq raise. Pony.ai has R&D centers in Guangzhou, Beijing, Shanghai, and Silicon Valley. Note: While originally founded in Silicon Valley, the company's primary operations and headquarters are in China, with the majority of its robotaxi fleet deployed in Chinese cities.
