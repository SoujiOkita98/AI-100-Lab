# Onyx

## One-liner

Open-source AI research agent for the workplace that searches across 40+ enterprise tools like a human would, iteratively exploring parallel pathways to find the most complete answers.

## Founders & Leadership

### Yuhong Sun -- Co-Founder

- Technical co-founder with strong AI/ML research background.
- Architected Onyx's deep research agent that explores parallel pathways and iteratively digs into promising directions.

### Chris Weaver -- Co-Founder

- Leads product and enterprise go-to-market strategy.

## Founding Story

Founded in 2023 in San Francisco, Onyx (formerly DanswerAI) started as an open-source project to solve the problem of enterprise knowledge fragmentation. Teams waste hours searching across disconnected tools -- Salesforce, SharePoint, GitHub, Gong, and dozens more. The founders built an AI research agent that searches the way a human would: exploring multiple paths, digging deeper into promising leads, and synthesizing comprehensive answers. The seed round was oversubscribed at $10M, more than 3x their original $3M target.

## Product & Business Model

### What Onyx Does

1. **AI deep research agent** -- Explores parallel pathways and iteratively investigates promising directions to retrieve complete context.
2. **40+ integrations** -- Indexes knowledge across Salesforce, SharePoint, GitHub, Gong, Slack, and more.
3. **Open source** -- Core platform is open source, enabling on-premises deployment for security-conscious organizations.
4. **Enterprise-ready** -- Used by Netflix, Thales Group, Bitwarden, and other security-conscious enterprises.

### Pricing / Monetization

Open-source core with enterprise features (SSO, audit logs, advanced security, managed hosting) as paid tiers.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning |
|-----------|------------|
| **Glean** | Enterprise AI search (closed-source, $4.6B valuation) |
| **Perplexity** | Consumer/prosumer AI search |
| **Guru** | Enterprise knowledge management |
| **Coveo** | Enterprise search and recommendations |
| **Hebbia** | AI research platform for knowledge work |

Onyx differentiates with its open-source model (enabling on-premises deployment for sensitive environments) and its "deep research" approach that goes beyond simple RAG to iterative, multi-step investigation.

## What Makes Onyx Interesting

1. **Open-source moat.** Enables deployment in defense and security-conscious organizations where cloud-only solutions are non-starters.
2. **Deep research vs. simple search.** Iterative, multi-pathway exploration produces more complete answers than basic RAG.
3. **Oversubscribed round.** 3x oversubscription from $3M target to $10M signals strong investor conviction.
4. **Enterprise traction.** Netflix, Thales Group, and defense organizations already using the platform.

## Risks & Open Questions

- Open-source monetization is challenging; must convert free users to paid enterprise tiers.
- Glean is the dominant enterprise AI search player with massive funding lead ($4.6B valuation).
- Early stage with modest funding relative to well-capitalized competitors.
