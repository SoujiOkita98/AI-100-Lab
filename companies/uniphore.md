# Uniphore -- Enterprise Conversational AI & Business Automation

## Company Overview

Uniphore is a business AI company that provides conversational service automation, combining conversational AI, computer vision, emotion AI, and workflow automation for enterprise customer and employee experiences. Originally founded in 2008 as a speech-recognition platform for multilingual Indian markets, Uniphore has evolved into a full-stack enterprise AI platform serving Fortune 500 companies globally.

The company's platform analyzes and automates conversations across voice, video, text, and email channels. Use cases span contact center automation, sales intelligence, after-call summarization, compliance monitoring, and knowledge management.

## Founders and Team

### Umesh Sachdev -- Co-Founder & CEO
- **Education:** Incubated Uniphore at IIT Madras; received initial grant of $100,000 from the National Research Development Corporation.
- **Recognition:** Named to TIME's list of "10 Millennials Who Are Changing the World" in 2016.
- **Background:** Serial entrepreneur focused on speech and language technology. Built Uniphore from an India-first speech recognition company into a global enterprise AI platform.

### Ravi Saraogi -- Co-Founder & President (APAC)
- **Origin:** India. Co-founded Uniphore with Sachdev and leads the Asia-Pacific business.

**Founder Pattern:** Both founders are Indian. The company's origin at IIT Madras is central to its identity. Uniphore started solving a distinctly Indian problem (multilingual speech recognition for a linguistically diverse country) and scaled that expertise into a global enterprise AI platform.
