# One Robot

## Overview

One Robot builds world models for Vision-Language-Action (VLA) evaluations and training, providing critical infrastructure for the next generation of AI-powered robots. VLA models represent the cutting edge of robotics AI, enabling robots to understand visual scenes, process natural language instructions, and execute physical actions. One Robot's world models provide the simulation and evaluation environments needed to train and benchmark these systems at scale, addressing a key bottleneck in robotics development.
