# Coval

## Overview

Coval is a San Francisco-based AI testing startup that brings proven evaluation methodologies from the self-driving car industry to assess and improve the performance of AI voice and chat agents. The company was part of Y Combinator's Summer 2024 batch and launched publicly in October 2024. By January 2025, it had raised a $3.3M seed round.

## Founder

**Brooke Hopkins** is the sole founder and CEO. Before starting Coval, she was a tech lead at Waymo, where she led the evaluation job infrastructure team. At Waymo, she coded the first versions of dataset storage and other foundational simulation systems and built all of the developer tools for launching and running evaluations. She saw a direct parallel between testing autonomous vehicles and testing conversational AI agents.

## Product

Coval simulates thousands of scenario variations to identify performance gaps in AI agents. The platform enables engineering teams to rigorously evaluate and monitor AI agent performance at scale. Key capabilities include:
- Simulation-based testing for voice and chat AI agents
- Scenario variation generation to identify edge cases
- Performance monitoring and regression detection
- Plans to extend evaluation to web-based agents
