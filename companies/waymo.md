# Waymo

> Alphabet's autonomous driving subsidiary, now valued at $126 billion after a $16 billion raise -- the most valuable autonomous vehicle company in the world.

## Company Overview

Waymo is the autonomous driving technology company spun out of Google's Self-Driving Car Project (started in 2009, became Waymo in 2016). Operating the world's first and largest commercial autonomous ride-hailing service, Waymo completed over 15 million rides in 2025 alone (tripling year-over-year) and surpassed 20 million lifetime rides. The company operates in multiple U.S. cities and is expanding to 20+ additional cities globally in 2026, including Tokyo and London.

In February 2026, Waymo raised $16 billion at a $126 billion valuation -- the largest autonomous vehicle investment ever -- with Alphabet providing approximately $13 billion and external investors including Sequoia, DST Global, and Dragoneer contributing the rest.

## Leadership

- **Tekedra Mawakana** -- Co-CEO
- **Dmitri Dolgov** -- Co-CEO

### Historical Founders

- **Sebastian Thrun** -- Founded the original Google Self-Driving Car Project in 2009 while a Stanford professor. Later founded Google X. Now involved with Cresta AI.
- **Chris Urmson** -- Early CTO of the self-driving car project who departed in 2016 to found Aurora Innovation.

## Business Model

- **Ride-hailing:** Waymo One robotaxi service, operating commercially in Phoenix, San Francisco, Los Angeles, Austin, and Atlanta, with global expansion planned.
- **Technology licensing:** Waymo Driver technology platform for potential partnerships with automakers and fleet operators.
- **Logistics/delivery:** Waymo Via for autonomous trucking and delivery (less emphasized recently).

## Key Metrics (2025)

- 15 million+ rides completed in 2025 (3x year-over-year)
- 20 million+ lifetime rides
- Operational in 5+ U.S. cities
- Expanding to 20+ additional cities in 2026 (including Tokyo, London)
- Valuation nearly tripled in 15 months ($45B to $126B)

## What Makes Waymo Interesting

1. **Largest AV investment ever:** $16B at $126B valuation makes Waymo the most valuable autonomous vehicle company and one of the most valuable private AI companies globally.
2. **External investor validation:** Sequoia, DST, and Dragoneer choosing to invest at these levels signals confidence that autonomous driving is reaching commercial scale.
3. **Ride volume inflection:** Tripling from ~5M to 15M rides in a single year suggests the classic S-curve adoption pattern is underway.
4. **Global expansion:** Moving from U.S.-only to Tokyo and London in 2026 represents a major strategic inflection point.
5. **Longest-running autonomous driving program:** Starting in 2009, Waymo has more real-world autonomous driving data and experience than any competitor.
