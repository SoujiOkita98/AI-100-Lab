# Candid Health

## Overview

Candid Health is a San Francisco-based healthcare technology company founded in 2019 by Nick Perry (former Palantir healthcare lead), Adam Reis (former Lyft engineer), and Doug Proctor. The company provides an AI-powered revenue cycle management (RCM) platform that automates medical billing, claims processing, and reimbursement for healthcare providers.

Candid Health has raised $99.5M in total funding, with its most recent $52.5M Series C led by Oak HC/FT in February 2025. The platform serves over 200 provider organizations across dozens of medical specialties.

## Funding story

**March 2020 -- Seed (~$18M).** Y Combinator backed, with participation from First Round Capital and BoxGroup. Initial capital to build the RCM automation platform.

**September 2024 -- Series B ($29M).** Led by 8VC with First Round Capital, BoxGroup, and Y Combinator. Brought total to $47M.

**February 2025 -- Series C ($52.5M).** Led by Oak HC/FT, a specialist healthcare investor. All existing investors participated. Just six months after the Series B, reflecting rapid growth. Brought total to $99.5M.

## What makes them interesting

1. **Massive, broken market.** Healthcare RCM is a $200B+ market plagued by manual processes, coding errors, and denial management headaches. AI automation can meaningfully reduce administrative waste.

2. **Rapid scaling.** Nearly tripled claims volume and processed 5x more charges YoY in 2025, with 200+ provider organizations onboard.

3. **Founder-market fit.** Nick Perry's Palantir healthcare background gives him deep understanding of healthcare data infrastructure. Stanford biomedical informatics training adds domain credibility.
