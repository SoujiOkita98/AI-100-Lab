# Agentio

## Overview

Agentio is a New York-based AI-native advertising platform founded in 2023 by Arthur Leopold (former Cameo president) and Jonathan Meyers (former Spotify growth engineer). The company automates the process of matching brands with social media creators for sponsored content, operating as a programmatic advertising platform for the creator economy.

Agentio has raised $56M in total funding across three rounds, reaching a $340M valuation at its November 2025 Series B led by Forerunner Ventures. The company grew 5x year-over-year and serves 100+ enterprise brands including Uber, DoorDash, and CashApp.

## Funding story

**November 2023 -- Seed ($8M).** AlleyCorp led, with Antler, Starting Line, and Craft Ventures participating. Initial capital to build the AI matching platform and launch on YouTube.

**November 2024 -- Series A ($8M).** Benchmark led. A smaller round focused on product development and early scaling.

**November 2025 -- Series B ($40M at $340M valuation).** Forerunner Ventures led, with all existing investors (Benchmark, Craft, AlleyCorp, Antler, Starting Line) participating. The round followed 5x YoY growth and expansion beyond YouTube to other platforms.

## What makes them interesting

1. **Programmatic creator advertising.** Agentio is essentially building "Google Ads for influencer marketing" -- making creator-led advertising as easy to buy as search ads. This unlocks a massive shift of digital ad spend toward creators.

2. **Founder-market fit.** Leopold scaled Cameo from 10 to 52,000+ creators and $500M in sales. He deeply understands the creator economy. Meyers built programmatic content systems at Spotify. Together they cover both sides of the marketplace.

3. **Market size.** RockWater estimates Agentio could help unlock $800B in advertising budget shift toward creator-led formats. Even capturing a small fraction represents a massive opportunity.
