# Doctronic

## Overview

Doctronic is an AI-powered healthcare platform that uses a chatbot to triage patients, discuss symptoms and health concerns, then transfer them to a clinician over telehealth for diagnosis and treatment, all for a $39 fee.

In January 2026, the state of Utah announced a partnership allowing Doctronic's autonomous AI system to legally renew prescriptions for patients with chronic conditions -- making it the first AI platform authorized to prescribe routine refills in the U.S. This is a landmark regulatory milestone for autonomous medicine.

## Funding Story

Three rounds in under a year: Series A ($20M) from Union Square Ventures, a $5M bridge, and a $40M Series B co-led by Abstract and Lightspeed, bringing total raised to $65M.

## What Makes Them Interesting

1. **First AI to legally prescribe.** The Utah partnership is a regulatory first that could open the door to autonomous prescription management nationwide.
2. **Speed of fundraising.** Three rounds in under a year signals strong investor conviction and rapid commercial traction.
3. **$39 price point.** Dramatically lower than traditional telehealth visits, potentially disrupting the primary care cost structure.
4. **Lightspeed backing.** Having a top-tier VC lead the Series B at this pace validates the clinical and regulatory progress.
