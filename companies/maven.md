# Maven AGI

## One-liner

Enterprise-grade AI agents for customer support that unify systems, sync functions, and orchestrate real-time action across the full customer journey.

## Founders & Leadership

### Jonathan Corbin -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Former HubSpot executive with deep enterprise SaaS experience.
- Leading Maven AGI's mission to build "Business AGI" for customer experience.

### Sami Shalabi -- Co-Founder & CTO

- Former Google engineer with expertise in AI systems and enterprise infrastructure.

### Eugene Mann -- Co-Founder & CPO

- Former Stripe executive with product leadership across enterprise platforms.

## Founding Story

Founded in 2023 by executives from HubSpot, Google, and Stripe, Maven AGI was born from the observation that enterprise customer support remained fragmented across dozens of disconnected systems. The founders set out to build an AI-native platform that could serve as a connected intelligence layer across the entire enterprise, not just a chatbot but a system that orchestrates action across CRM, ticketing, knowledge bases, and other enterprise tools.

## Product & Business Model

### What Maven AGI Does

1. **AI customer support agents** -- Autonomous agents that handle complex, high-friction customer interactions across channels.
2. **Enterprise system integration** -- Connects and unifies data from CRM, ticketing, knowledge bases, and internal tools into a single intelligence layer.
3. **Real-time orchestration** -- Goes beyond answering questions to taking action across enterprise systems on behalf of customers and support teams.

### Pricing / Monetization

Enterprise SaaS model targeting large companies with complex customer support operations.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning |
|-----------|------------|
| **Ada** | AI-powered customer service automation |
| **Forethought** | AI agent for customer support |
| **Intercom Fin** | AI chatbot for customer support |
| **Zendesk AI** | AI features layered on legacy ticketing |
| **Sierra AI** | Conversational AI for customer experience |

Maven AGI differentiates by functioning as an enterprise operating layer rather than a standalone chatbot, unifying disparate systems into coherent AI-driven workflows.

## What Makes Maven AGI Interesting

1. **Enterprise-grade from day one.** Built for complex, high-friction environments rather than simple FAQ bots.
2. **Strategic investors.** Dell Technologies Capital and Cisco Investments signal enterprise distribution partnerships.
3. **System orchestration.** Goes beyond conversational AI to actually execute actions across enterprise systems.

## Risks & Open Questions

- Crowded AI customer support market with well-funded competitors (Sierra, Ada, Intercom).
- Enterprise sales cycles are long and require deep system integrations.
- Must prove ROI over existing support stack investments.
