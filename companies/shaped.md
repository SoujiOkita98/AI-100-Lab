# Shaped

## Overview

Shaped is a real-time retrieval and recommendation engine that powers search, feeds, and AI agents for companies that need personalized user experiences. The platform enables companies to deploy production-grade recommendation and search systems without building complex ML infrastructure from scratch. Shaped launched a self-serve cloud solution alongside its Series A, making enterprise-grade recommendations accessible to smaller teams.

## Founders & Key People

### Tullie Murrell -- Co-Founder & CEO
- **Background:** Previously at Meta/Facebook AI Research (FAIR), where he created PyTorchVideo -- a deep learning library for video understanding -- and worked on other video-centric AI projects. Deep expertise in recommendation systems and real-time retrieval.
- **Origin:** Australian, based in New York

### Daniel Camilleri -- Co-Founder & CPO
- **Background:** Previously at Uber (marketplace engineering) and Afterpay (fintech); product and engineering experience building real-time systems at scale
- **Origin:** Australian

*Note: Shai Bruhis was a third co-founder who has since moved on to another venture.*

## Products & Technology

1. **Recommendation Engine:** Real-time, personalized recommendations for feeds, product listings, and content
2. **Search Engine:** ML-powered search with personalization and semantic understanding
3. **AI Agent Retrieval:** Retrieval layer for AI agents that need to surface relevant information
4. **Self-Serve Platform:** Cloud-based platform for teams to deploy recommendation systems without ML expertise

## Business Model

- **SaaS platform:** Usage-based pricing for API calls to recommendation and search endpoints
- **Self-serve tier:** Launched alongside Series A to reach smaller teams
- **Target customers:** Companies building consumer products, marketplaces, and content platforms that need personalized recommendations

## What Makes Shaped Interesting

1. **Meta FAIR pedigree.** The CEO's background creating PyTorchVideo at Facebook AI Research provides deep technical credibility in ML-powered retrieval and recommendation systems.

2. **Self-serve launch.** Moving from enterprise-only to self-serve dramatically expands the addressable market and enables product-led growth.

3. **AI agent retrieval.** As AI agents proliferate, they need efficient retrieval systems to surface relevant information -- positioning Shaped as infrastructure for the agentic AI wave.

4. **Strong investor signal.** Madrona leading the Series A (known for early-stage AI infrastructure bets) and angel investors from ClickHouse, Rippling, and StitchFix signal confidence from people who deeply understand recommendation systems.
