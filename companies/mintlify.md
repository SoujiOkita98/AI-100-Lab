# Mintlify

## Overview

Mintlify is an AI-powered documentation platform that makes building world-class developer documentation easy. The platform converts Markdown and MDX files into branded documentation sites with built-in search, custom domains, and interactive components. Two years post-launch, Mintlify powers developer documentation for over 5,000 companies, reaching more than 2 million unique monthly visitors. Its customers include many of the fastest-growing AI companies: Anthropic, Scale AI, Cursor, and Pinecone.

## Products & Technology

1. **Docs-as-Code Platform:** Git-connected documentation that auto-syncs every commit, runs CI checks, and generates preview URLs
2. **AI-Powered Search:** Intelligent search across documentation with natural language understanding
3. **Auto-Generation:** AI-powered content generation and code documentation from source code
4. **Custom Branding:** White-label documentation sites with custom domains, themes, and interactive components
5. **Analytics:** Usage analytics to understand what users are searching for and reading

## Recent Acquisitions

- **Trieve (Jul 2025):** Acquired RAG infrastructure company to enhance AI search capabilities
- **Helicone (Mar 2026):** Acquired open-source LLM observability platform, expanding into AI knowledge infrastructure

## Business Model

- **SaaS subscription:** Tiered pricing from free developer tier through enterprise
- **Target customers:** Developer-facing companies that need documentation (API companies, developer tools, open-source projects)
- **Expansion strategy:** Growing from pure documentation into a broader "intelligent knowledge platform" through acquisitions

## What Makes Mintlify Interesting

1. **AI-native documentation leader.** With 5,000+ companies and 2M+ monthly visitors, Mintlify has established itself as the default documentation platform for modern developer-facing companies.

2. **Marquee AI customers.** Having Anthropic, Scale AI, Cursor, and Pinecone as customers provides both revenue and powerful social proof -- the companies building AI are using Mintlify to document it.

3. **Aggressive acquisition strategy.** Acquiring Trieve (RAG) and Helicone (LLM observability) signals ambition beyond documentation toward becoming a comprehensive AI knowledge infrastructure platform.

4. **a16z backing.** Andreessen Horowitz leading the Series A provides access to their extensive portfolio of developer tools companies and AI startups.

5. **Early YC W22 vintage.** Founded before the AI boom, Mintlify has had time to build a strong foundation and customer base, now expanding into AI-powered features from a position of strength.
