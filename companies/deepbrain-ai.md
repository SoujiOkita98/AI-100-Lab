# DeepBrain AI

## Overview
DeepBrain AI (formerly Moneybrain) is a Seoul-based AI company specializing in photorealistic AI avatars and synthetic human technology. The company provides AI-powered video generation, text-to-speech, dubbing, and deepfake detection tools. Its "synthetic humans" are human-like avatars that respond to natural language, deployed for customer service, corporate training, and content creation. With approximately 100 employees, DeepBrain AI serves customers globally with a growing focus on US expansion. The company's AI Studios platform enables video creation using AI avatars and voiceovers in multiple languages.
