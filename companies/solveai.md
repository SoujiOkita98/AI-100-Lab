# SolveAI

## Overview
SolveAI is a London-based startup founded in July 2025 by former Palantir engineer Steve Basher. The company emerged from stealth in February 2026 with $50M in total funding. Unlike AI coding tools like Lovable and Claude Code that focus on code acceleration or prototypes, SolveAI delivers fully production-ready enterprise applications with built-in governance, security, and integration capabilities. Its model orchestration and forward-deployed engineering approach enables non-technical employees to create sophisticated tools meeting enterprise compliance and scalability requirements. The 12-person team, based in Chancery Lane, London, includes senior hires from Palantir, ElevenLabs, and Meta, with plans to grow 4x in 2026.
