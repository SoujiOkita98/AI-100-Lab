# Nebius Group (NBIS)

## Overview

Nebius is a full-stack AI cloud infrastructure provider offering GPU-as-a-Service (GPUaaS) to AI developers and enterprises. The company emerged from the international (non-Russian) operations of Yandex N.V. after a multi-year corporate restructuring driven by geopolitical events. Nebius provides high-speed, low-latency access to NVIDIA's most advanced GPUs (H100, B200, and upcoming Rubin architecture) and has rapidly become a leading European "neocloud" player.

## Founding Story and Team Origins

Nebius traces its roots to Yandex, Russia's dominant search and technology company, co-founded by Arkady Volozh and the late Ilya Segalovich. Following Russia's invasion of Ukraine, Yandex N.V. sold its Russian assets for $5.4 billion to a domestic consortium in July 2024. The remaining international entity -- encompassing AI/ML R&D, data center infrastructure, and cloud services built over two decades -- rebranded as Nebius Group N.V. in August 2024 and resumed trading on NASDAQ in October 2024.

CEO Arkady Volozh, whose EU sanctions were lifted in early 2024, leads the company. He has renounced his Russian citizenship to solidify standing with Western regulators. The team inherits deep engineering talent from Yandex's AI research labs and infrastructure operations.

## Business Model

- **GPU-as-a-Service (GPUaaS):** Primary revenue driver, providing cloud access to NVIDIA's latest GPU clusters for AI training and inference
- **Full-stack AI platform:** Software tools, orchestration, and managed services on top of bare-metal GPU compute
- **Data center operations:** Operates facilities across Europe and is expanding globally
- **Major enterprise contracts:** Signed a $27B infrastructure commitment from Meta (March 2026) and a $17B commitment from Microsoft
