# Interloom

## Overview

Interloom is a Munich-based enterprise operations platform that captures expert knowledge -- the kind that never gets written down -- and transforms it into permanent memory for AI agents. The company's core insight: 70% of operational decisions are never documented, which creates a massive barrier to AI agent deployment in enterprise settings.

The platform ingests millions of operational records (support emails, service tickets, call transcripts, work orders) and uses them to build a "context graph" -- a continuously updated map of how problems actually get resolved within a given organization.

## Funding Story

After a $3M initial seed in 2024 from Air Street Capital, Interloom raised a EUR 14.2M ($16.5M) extension in March 2026 led by DN Capital, one of Europe's most active enterprise software investors.

## What Makes Them Interesting

1. **Tacit knowledge problem.** The insight that 70% of operational decisions are undocumented is a genuine barrier to AI agent adoption that most agent startups ignore.
2. **Context graph approach.** Building a continuously updated map of organizational problem-solving is architecturally different from RAG or fine-tuning.
3. **Founder track record.** Fabian Jakobi previously built and sold Boxplot to Hyperscience, demonstrating ability to build and exit enterprise AI companies.
4. **European enterprise focus.** European enterprises have strong compliance requirements that make the "documented decision-making" value proposition even more compelling.
