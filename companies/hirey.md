# Hirey

## Overview
AI-first recruiting platform specializing in high-volume hiring automation. AI agent "Rey" handles candidate sourcing, screening, and interview scheduling via SMS/chat. Primarily serves home care agencies, caregiver franchises, and essential local businesses. Partnered with 200+ caregiver franchises in Houston.

## Investors
DST Global, Celtic House Asia Partners, MindWorks Ventures, Ying Fund, StartupAZ Collective.

## Notes
- Total raised is $29M per Crunchbase, though only the $14M Series A from DST Global is publicly detailed
- Presence in Palo Alto, CA and Houston, TX in addition to HQ in Tempe, AZ
- Founder previously built OnTime Job and Hirect App in the same recruiting space
