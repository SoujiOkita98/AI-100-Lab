# Autonomous A2Z

## Overview
Autonomous A2Z is South Korea's leading autonomous driving mobility startup, founded in 2018 by four former Hyundai Motor Group engineers. The company develops and operates autonomous driving services using its self-developed autonomous mobility platform, with over 400,000 km of accumulated autonomous driving experience. Autonomous A2Z was the first Korean company to register a VSSA (Voluntary Safety Self-Assessment) with the U.S. Department of Transportation. The company operates autonomous shuttle buses in Singapore in cooperation with Grab, runs Level-4 autonomous taxi demonstrations in Japan, and has received government approval to deploy its "ROii" autonomous vehicle in the UAE. With total funding of 122.5 billion won (the largest raised by a Korean self-driving startup), the company is preparing for a Kosdaq IPO in 2026.
