# Eightfold AI

## One-liner

AI-powered talent intelligence platform that uses deep learning to help enterprises recruit, retain, and develop talent across 110+ countries.

## Founders & Leadership

### Ashutosh Garg -- Co-Founder & Co-CEO

- BTech from IIT-Delhi, PhD from UIUC.
- Former CTO and Co-Founder of BloomReach (e-commerce AI).
- Worked at Google on search and ML infrastructure and at IBM Research on machine learning and information retrieval.
- Prolific inventor with 50+ patents and 30+ published papers.

### Varun Kacholia -- Co-Founder & CTO

- Ranked #2 at the IIT entrance exam in 2000; Masters from UC Berkeley.
- Led the Facebook News Feed ranking team and the YouTube Search & Recommendations team at Google.

## Product & Business Model

Eightfold's Talent Intelligence Platform applies deep learning to talent data across the entire employee lifecycle: talent acquisition, talent management, workforce planning, and diversity. The platform analyzes over 1.5 billion talent profiles to match people to opportunities. Customers include Fortune 500 companies such as Bayer, Capital One, AirAsia, and Micron. The company operates on an enterprise SaaS subscription model.
