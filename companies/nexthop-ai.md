# Nexthop AI

## Overview

Nexthop AI builds custom networking hardware and software optimized for AI data centers. The company addresses a critical bottleneck in AI infrastructure: the networking layer that connects GPU clusters. As AI training and inference workloads scale to tens of thousands of GPUs, traditional Ethernet and InfiniBand networking becomes a major performance constraint. Nexthop designs bespoke networking solutions tailored to each hyperscaler customer's specifications.

## Founding Story and Team Origins

Nexthop AI was founded in 2024 by Anshul Sadana, who spent 17 years as Chief Operating Officer at Arista Networks, where he helped scale the company from its earliest days through an IPO and into a $100B+ market cap networking leader. Prior to Arista, Sadana spent eight years at Cisco Systems.

The team is composed of Arista Networks alumni engineers and FAANG supply chain experts from Meta, Google, and Microsoft. The company has offices in Santa Clara, Seattle, Vancouver, and Bangalore.

## Business Model

- **Custom networking hardware:** Builds switches and networking infrastructure designed to each hyperscaler customer's exact specifications
- **Network operating system:** Provides a hardened NOS of the customer's choice, optimized by Nexthop AI
- **Pre-tested interconnects:** Delivers pre-tested optical and electrical interconnects from the customer's diverse supply chain
- **Direct hyperscaler relationships:** Works alongside the world's largest cloud providers as a design and manufacturing partner

Nexthop focuses on high-performance, low-latency, lossless connectivity for scale-out, scale-up, scale-across, and front-end AI networks.
