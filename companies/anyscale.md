# Anyscale

## Overview

Anyscale is the company behind the Ray open-source framework for scaling AI and Python workloads. Founded in 2019 by UC Berkeley researchers Robert Nishihara, Philipp Moritz, Ion Stoica, and Michael I. Jordan, Anyscale provides a managed platform that simplifies distributed computing for AI/ML applications. The company achieved unicorn status at a $1B valuation following its Series C in December 2021. Ray is used by major companies including OpenAI, Uber, Spotify, and Netflix for distributed training, serving, and data processing.
