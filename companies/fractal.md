# Fractal Analytics

## One-liner

Global enterprise AI and analytics company powering 100+ Fortune 500 companies with data-driven decision intelligence across healthcare, retail, and financial services.

## Founders & Leadership

### Srikanth Velamakanni -- Co-Founder, Group Chief Executive & Executive Vice-Chairman

- Background in engineering and finance.
- Worked at ICICI Bank's structured products group, where he co-pioneered India's first CBO in 1999 alongside Pranay Agrawal.

### Pranay Agrawal -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Educated at the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad (IIM-A).
- Appointed CEO in 2016, taking over from Velamakanni.

Additional co-founders include Nirmal Palaparthi, Pradeep Suryanarayan, and Ramakrishna Reddy, who have since departed.

## Founding Story

Founded in March 2000 in Mumbai, Fractal started as a modest analytics firm serving enterprises. Over 25 years, the company grew into a global AI powerhouse serving Fortune 500 clients. In January 2022, a $360M investment from TPG Capital gave Fractal unicorn status at a $1B valuation.

## Product & Business Model

Fractal provides AI-driven enterprise solutions across analytics, data engineering, and decision intelligence. The company serves clients in healthcare, retail, financial services, and consumer goods. Revenue demonstrated 503% PAT growth from FY24 to FY25, swinging from a loss to a profit of approximately 220 crore.

## IPO Plans

Fractal received SEBI approval on November 18, 2025 for a landmark 4,900 crore (~$588M) IPO expected to value the company above $3.5B.
