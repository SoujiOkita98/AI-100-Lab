# Quotr

## Overview
Quotr automates construction estimating workflows using AI. Claims 90% faster takeoffs and 95% estimation accuracy. Also provides access to factory-direct materials at 40-50% below retail pricing via an 800,000-item database. Targets subcontractors, general contractors, and real estate developers (primarily 2-10 seat teams).

## Founders
Both co-founders met at UC Berkeley. Hanyang Liu brings architecture and construction industry experience from firms in China and the US. Junzhe Shi brings ML research expertise from Berkeley's Civil Engineering department.
