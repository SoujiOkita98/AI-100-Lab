# Servo7

## Overview

Servo7 builds autonomous warehouse robots designed for logistics and fulfillment operations. The company applies AI-driven autonomy to warehouse robotics, enabling robots to navigate, pick, and manage inventory without constant human supervision. The warehouse automation market is one of the largest and most proven use cases for robotics, with incumbents like Amazon Robotics (Kiva) and newer players competing for a market expected to exceed $30B by 2028.
