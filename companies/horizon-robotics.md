# Horizon Robotics

## Overview
Horizon Robotics is a Chinese AI chip company that designs processors for autonomous driving and advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS). Founded in 2015 by former Baidu AI leader Yu Kai, the company controls approximately 49% of the Chinese self-driving chip market. Its Journey series chips have shipped over 10 million units, powering one in three intelligent vehicles in China. Horizon went public on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange in October 2024, raising $696M in the most significant tech IPO in Hong Kong that year. Key partners and investors include Volkswagen, BYD, Chery, CATL, Intel, and Hillhouse Investment.
