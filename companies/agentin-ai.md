# Agentin AI

## Overview

Agentin AI develops reinforcement learning algorithms so AI agents can learn and adapt from failures when automating enterprise software processes. Founded by ex-Google Research Scientist Sankeerth Rao Karingula, the company uses RL-based approaches to make AI agents more robust and self-improving in real-world enterprise environments.
