# Run:ai

## Overview
Founded by two TAU researchers who identified enterprises were wasting 50-75% of GPU capacity. Kubernetes-native platform virtualizes GPU resources for dynamic allocation. Nvidia announced acquisition for ~$700M in April 2024, completed December 30, 2024 after EU/DOJ approval. Nvidia immediately open-sourced the software, making it compatible with non-Nvidia hardware.

## Investors
TLV Partners, S Capital, Insight Partners, Tiger Global.
