# PolyAI

## Overview
PolyAI is a London-based enterprise conversational AI company that builds human-like voice agents for customer service. Founded in 2017 by three researchers from Cambridge University's Machine Intelligence Lab, the company specializes in voice-based virtual agents capable of understanding intent, managing multi-step workflows (authentication, bookings, payments), and integrating with enterprise systems. The Series D round included a GBP 15M investment from the British Business Bank and participation from NVIDIA's venture arm NVentures. PolyAI has offices in London and New York.
