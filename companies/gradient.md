# Gradient AI

## One-liner

AI-powered risk analytics and claims automation platform for the insurance industry, helping carriers and MGAs improve underwriting accuracy and operational efficiency.

## Founders & Leadership

### Stan Smith -- Founder & CEO

- Nearly 30 years growing AI and technology organizations.
- BS from Dartmouth College.
- Previously VP at MatrixOne, EVP & GM at Agile Software, and CEO & Founder of OpenRatings.
- As a predictive analytics practice leader at Milliman (actuarial consulting firm), witnessed firsthand the insurance industry's data inefficiencies. Acquired the Gradient AI business from Milliman in 2018 to build it into an independent SaaS company.

## Product & Business Model

Gradient AI provides AI-based underwriting, claims automation, and auditing solutions for the insurance industry. The platform helps insurance carriers and managing general agents (MGAs) make more accurate risk assessments and automate claims processing. Customers include MassMutual and other major insurance carriers.
