# Seeing Systems

## Overview

Seeing Systems builds AI-powered drone systems for defence applications, enabling autonomous aerial surveillance, reconnaissance, and situational awareness. The company leverages advances in computer vision, edge AI, and autonomous navigation to create drone systems that can operate with minimal human intervention. Defense AI and autonomous systems continue to attract significant venture capital, with companies like Anduril, Shield AI, and Skydio leading the way.
