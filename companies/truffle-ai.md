# Truffle AI

## Overview

Truffle AI provides an AI agent development platform that enables businesses to build, customize, and deploy AI agents to automate and unify workflows. Their open-source product Saiki is a general-purpose AI agent designed for integration into any software application via simple APIs. Saiki automates complex workflows by connecting to diverse tools (Slack, email, Notion, GitHub) and can navigate browsers, resolve ambiguities, and unify tasks.
