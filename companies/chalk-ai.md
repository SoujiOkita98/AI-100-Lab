# Chalk

## One-liner

Real-time data platform for AI inference that provides the infrastructure needed to operationalize machine learning and AI at scale across industries including fintech, healthcare, and e-commerce.

## Founders & Leadership

### Marc Freed-Finnegan -- Co-Founder & CEO
- Spent many years at Google where he helped launch the first version of Google Wallet.
- Founded Index, which Stripe acquired as its in-store payment solution (now Stripe Terminal).

### Elliot Marx -- Co-Founder

### Andrew Moreland -- Co-Founder

## Product & Business Model

Chalk is a data platform for AI inference. It helps companies build real-time AI features by providing the infrastructure to operationalize machine learning models. The platform serves industries including fintech, healthcare, and e-commerce.
