# 7AI

> Autonomous AI-powered cybersecurity agents that automate security investigations, founded by the co-founders of Cybereason.

## Company Overview

7AI was founded in 2024 by Lior Div and Yonatan Striem-Amit, the co-founders of Cybereason (a cybersecurity company that raised over $700M and reached a $3.5B+ valuation). The company builds a platform of autonomous AI-powered agents that automate investigative security tasks — processing alerts, running investigations, and eliminating false positives — freeing human security analysts to focus on strategic work.

7AI emerged from stealth in February 2025 and within 10 months had processed more than 2.5 million alerts, completed over 650,000 security investigations, and cut investigation times from hours to minutes while eliminating 95-99% of false positives.

## Product: 7AI Platform

The 7AI Platform deploys autonomous AI agents that:

- Process and triage security alerts at machine speed
- Conduct full security investigations autonomously
- Eliminate 95-99% of false positives
- Reduce investigation times from hours to minutes

**Deployment models:**
- **Self-service:** Customers deploy the 7AI Platform independently
- **PLAID (People-Led, AI-Driven):** White-glove customization and expert guidance from 7AI's Boston-based team

The platform was showcased at Black Hat 2025 as the "industry's first dynamic agentic security platform with end-to-end autonomous operations."

## Business Model

7AI sells its agentic security platform to enterprises through a combination of:

1. **Platform licensing:** Direct enterprise sales of the 7AI autonomous investigation platform
2. **Managed services (PLAID):** Premium tier with expert human guidance layered on top of AI agents
3. **Channel partnerships:** Working with security resellers like GuidePoint for distribution

## What Makes 7AI Interesting

1. **Serial founder credibility:** Lior Div and Yonatan Striem-Amit previously built Cybereason to a $3.5B+ valuation — rare to see repeat founders in cybersecurity with this level of success.

2. **Record-breaking Series A:** $130M is the largest cybersecurity Series A ever, achieved just 10 months after launch, signaling extreme investor conviction.

3. **Unit 8200 pedigree:** Both founders are veterans of Israel's elite intelligence unit, which has produced founders of CrowdStrike, Check Point, Palo Alto Networks, and many other security companies.

4. **Immediate traction:** 2.5M+ alerts processed and 650K+ investigations completed within 10 months of launch demonstrates rapid enterprise adoption.

5. **Agentic security positioning:** 7AI sits at the intersection of two major trends — agentic AI and cybersecurity — addressing the chronic shortage of human security analysts with autonomous agents.
