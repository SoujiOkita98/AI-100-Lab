# Automat (fka Lasso)

## Overview

Automat (formerly known as Lasso) is an AI-enabled robotic process automation (RPA) platform that translates video recordings or text descriptions of workflows into automated software processes. The platform breaks down recorded workflows into step-by-step instructions and uses AI to interpret and replicate those actions -- performing tasks on behalf of users across any application. Originally pitched as "Zapier for any application," Automat integrates with tools like HubSpot and Salesforce and can parse documents, manage inventory, and respond to communications.

## Products & Technology

1. **Video-to-Automation:** Record a workflow on screen and the AI converts it into a replicable automation
2. **Natural Language Instructions:** Describe a workflow in text and the AI builds the automation
3. **Computer Vision:** AI visually interprets screen elements to navigate and interact with any application
4. **Cross-Application Support:** Works across HubSpot, Salesforce, and other enterprise applications
5. **Document Parsing:** AI-powered extraction and processing of data from documents

## Business Model

- **SaaS platform:** Subscription-based access to the automation platform
- **Target customers:** Operations teams, sales teams, and anyone with repetitive software workflows
- **Differentiation from Zapier:** While Zapier relies on API integrations, Automat uses AI and computer vision to automate workflows in any application -- even those without APIs

## What Makes Automat Interesting

1. **Khosla Ventures at pre-seed.** Having Khosla Ventures and Initialized Capital invest at the earliest stage signals strong conviction in the founding team and approach.

2. **AI-native RPA.** Traditional RPA tools (UiPath, Automation Anywhere) rely on brittle screen-scraping. Automat uses modern AI (ChatGPT + computer vision) to create more robust, adaptable automations.

3. **Video-to-workflow paradigm.** The concept of "show me the task and I'll automate it" is intuitive and dramatically lowers the barrier to automation -- no coding, no API configuration, just a screen recording.

4. **Universal application support.** Unlike API-dependent tools, Automat can work with any software that has a visual interface, dramatically expanding the addressable automation market.
