# Hightouch

## Overview

Hightouch is an AI-powered composable customer data platform (CDP) and marketing decisioning platform. Founded in 2019 by Tejas Manohar, Kashish Gupta (both ex-Segment), and Josh Curl, the company helps organizations sync customer data across business tools and uses AI agents to automate personalized marketing campaigns. Hightouch achieved unicorn status with a $1.2B valuation following its $80M Series C led by Sapphire Ventures in February 2025. The company has raised $172M in total from investors including ICONIQ Growth, Amplify Partners, Bain Capital Ventures, and Y Combinator.
