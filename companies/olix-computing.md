# Olix Computing

## Overview
Olix Computing is a London-based AI chip startup developing the Optical Tensor Processing Unit (OTPU), a novel AI accelerator designed specifically for inference workloads. The chip integrates SRAM memory architecture with photonics to achieve higher throughput per megawatt and lower total cost of ownership compared to traditional HBM-based designs. The company was originally founded as Flux Computing in March 2024 by 25-year-old James Dacombe and rebranded to Olix Computing in January 2026. With $250M in total funding and a $1B+ valuation, Olix aims to challenge Nvidia in the AI inference hardware market, with first customer deliveries targeted for 2027.
