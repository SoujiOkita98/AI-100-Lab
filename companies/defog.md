# Defog.ai

## One-liner

Open-source LLM platform that converts natural language questions into SQL queries for enterprise data analysis, with on-premise deployment and fine-tuning capabilities.

## Founders & Leadership

### Medha Basu -- Co-Founder

- Former journalist and editor who transitioned to content marketing.
- Built an enterprise content marketing firm from $0 to 7-figure annual revenue.
- Previously built a digital media startup for Asian governments focused on tech in public service delivery.

### Rishabh Srivastava -- Co-Founder

- Technical co-founder with ML expertise.
- Led development of SQLCoder, Defog's open-source text-to-SQL model.

## Product & Business Model

Defog builds open-source LLMs specialized for data analysis, most notably SQLCoder -- the world's best models for converting natural language into SQL queries. SQLCoder models have garnered 50,000+ downloads on Hugging Face and 1,800+ GitHub stars within 3 months of release. The models can be deployed and fine-tuned on-premise, outperforming GPT-4 for data analysis tasks. Y Combinator W23 batch.
