# Fyxer AI

## One-liner

AI executive assistant that organizes inboxes, drafts emails, and creates meeting notes, growing from $1M to $17M ARR in just eight months.

## Product & Business Model

Fyxer AI is an AI executive assistant that organizes inboxes, drafts emails, and creates meeting notes. The platform serves over 180,000 users and has demonstrated exceptional product-market fit with revenue growing from $1M to $17M ARR in eight months.
