# Vapi

## Overview

Vapi is a voice AI developer platform that provides the infrastructure for building, testing, and deploying AI-powered voice agents. Founded in 2020 by Jordan Dearsley and Nikhil Gupta (originally as Superpowered, Y Combinator W21), the platform enables enterprises to create conversational voice AI experiences. Vapi raised a $20M Series A led by Bessemer Venture Partners at a $130M valuation in December 2024, with participation from Abstract Ventures, AI Grant, Y Combinator, Saga Ventures, and Michael Ovitz. The company serves thousands of developers building voice-first AI applications.
