# Tera AI

## Overview

Tera AI was founded in 2023 by Tony Zhang, who previously led machine learning at Google X. The company emerged from stealth in March 2025 with a $7.8M seed round. Tera builds a software-only spatial reasoning system that enables any robot to navigate new environments using just its onboard camera, similar to how humans navigate unfamiliar spaces without prior maps.

## Funding

Tera raised a $7.8M seed round co-led by Felicis Ventures and Inovia Capital, with participation from Caltech + Wilson Hill and Naval Ravikant.

## Key Details

- Team includes AI and simulation researchers from Google AI, Caltech, MIT, and the European Space Agency
- Platform-agnostic: works on any robot with a camera
- No prior setup, external sensors, or infrastructure required
