# KREA

## Overview

KREA is an AI creative tools platform founded in 2022 by Diego Rodriguez and Victor Perez, who notably turned down postgraduate grants from the King of Spain to build the company. KREA provides a unified platform for generative AI creation including image generation, video generation, and real-time design tools. The company has experienced 10x growth over two years, reaching over 4 million users and $5.8M in revenue by 2025. Valued at $500M after its Series B led by Bain Capital Ventures.
