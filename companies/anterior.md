# Anterior

## Overview

Anterior is a clinician-led AI company automating administrative workflows for health plans, with a focus on prior authorization. Founded in 2022 by Dr. Abdel Mahmoud (a physician and former Google Product Manager) and Zahid Mahmood, the company uses large language models to streamline clinical decision-making processes. Over 40% of the team consists of nurses and physicians. The company has raised $64M across 3 rounds, with its most recent being a $40M round in February 2026 backed by NEA, Sequoia Capital, FPV, and Kinnevik.
