# Krutrim

## Overview
Krutrim is an Indian AI company founded by Ola founder Bhavish Aggarwal that became India's fastest company to achieve unicorn status in January 2024. The company builds multilingual large language models optimized for Indian languages, with Krutrim-2 (12B parameters) showing strong performance in processing Indian languages. Aggarwal invested $230M from his family office and announced plans to build India's largest supercomputer in partnership with NVIDIA. The company has open-sourced its AI models. Note: Krutrim has faced challenges in external fundraising, with reports of muted investor interest in later 2025.
