# Espresso AI

## One-liner

AI-powered SQL query optimization platform that reduces cloud data warehouse costs by up to 80% using generative AI, starting with Snowflake workloads.

## Founders & Leadership

### Nima Badizadegan -- Co-Founder

## Product & Business Model

Espresso AI uses advanced language models and machine learning to automatically optimize SQL queries and reduce cloud compute costs by up to 80%. The initial product focuses on streamlining queries for Snowflake, the popular cloud data warehousing platform. The company has seen strong early traction with multiple enterprise customers.
