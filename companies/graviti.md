# Graviti

## Overview

Graviti is a New York-based AI infrastructure startup that builds a modern data management platform designed specifically for machine learning applications. The company launched its first product, Graviti Data Platform, in January 2022 after emerging from stealth. Graviti addresses one of the costliest challenges in AI development: managing, curating, and versioning large volumes of unstructured data (images, video, point clouds, etc.) that power AI models.

## Founder

**Edward Cui** founded Graviti in 2019. He previously worked as a machine learning expert within Uber's Advanced Technologies Group, the company's autonomous driving division, where he gained firsthand experience with the data management challenges that plague AI development teams.

## Product

Graviti Data Platform provides:
- **Git-like data version control** for unstructured datasets
- **Workflow automation** for data curation and labeling pipelines
- **Data visualization** tools for inspecting and understanding AI training data
- **Collaboration features** for distributed AI development teams

The platform also includes TensorBay, a professional data management tool, and an Open Dataset platform for community use.
