# Hex

## Overview

Hex is an AI-powered collaborative data science and analytics workspace that enables teams to analyze data, build models, and drive insights from a unified platform. Founded in 2019 by Barry McCardel, Caitlin Colgrove, and Glen Takahashi (all ex-Palantir), Hex is trusted by leading organizations including Reddit, StubHub, HubSpot, Cisco, Figma, Anthropic, Rivian, and the NBA. The company raised a $70M Series C led by Avra in May 2025, bringing total funding to $172M. Other investors include a16z, Sequoia, Redpoint, Amplify, and Snowflake Ventures.
