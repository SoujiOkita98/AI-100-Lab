# Jasper AI

## Overview
One of the first generative AI unicorns. Rode the GPT-3 wave to explosive growth in 2021-2022. ChatGPT's launch in Nov 2022 disrupted the market — Jasper revised forecasts down 30%, conducted layoffs, and cut internal valuation to ~$1.2B. Founder Dave Rogenmoser stepped down Sep 2023, replaced by former Dropbox president Timothy Young. Now pivoted to enterprise AI marketing platform with 100+ specialized agents. Acquired Clipdrop (15M users) in Feb 2024.

## Investors
Insight Partners, Coatue, Bessemer Venture Partners, IVP, Foundation Capital, HubSpot Ventures, Y Combinator.
