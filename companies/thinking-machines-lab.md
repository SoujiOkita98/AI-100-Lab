# Thinking Machines Lab

> Public benefit corporation building customizable frontier AI models, founded by former OpenAI CTO **Mira Murati** and five former OpenAI researchers.

## Company Overview

Thinking Machines Lab was launched in February 2025 by Mira Murati, who served as CTO of OpenAI during the development of GPT-4, DALL-E, and ChatGPT. The company's stated mission is to "make AI systems more widely understood, customizable, and generally capable." Structured as a public benefit corporation, the company grants Murati a deciding vote on board matters.

The founding team of six all previously worked at OpenAI and other leading AI labs (Meta, Mistral). In addition to the co-founders, the company hired approximately 30 leading researchers and engineers from Meta, Mistral, and OpenAI.

## Founders and Team

### Mira Murati — CEO

- **Born:** 1988, Vlore, Albania; immigrated to Canada at age 16, then to the US
- **Education:** Dartmouth College, BS in Mechanical Engineering
- **Prior experience:** CTO of OpenAI (2018-2024); briefly served as interim CEO of OpenAI (Nov 2023); VP of Product at Leap Motion; engineer at Tesla (Model X development)
- Sources: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mira_Murati), [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2025/07/15/openai-mira-murati-thinking-machines-lab.html)

### John Schulman — Co-founder

- Co-creator of Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) and founding member of OpenAI
- Led OpenAI's RLHF and alignment teams

### Other Co-founders

## Product: Tinker

On October 1, 2025, Thinking Machines Lab announced **Tinker**, an API for fine-tuning language models. Users submit fine-tuning jobs through the API for one of several supported open-weight models, and the Lab runs the jobs on its internal GPU clusters and training infrastructure.

## Business Model

Thinking Machines Lab operates as an API-based model customization platform. Revenue comes from compute fees charged for fine-tuning jobs submitted through the Tinker API. The company is positioning itself as a model customization layer — enabling enterprises and developers to adapt open-weight models to their specific needs without managing their own GPU infrastructure.

## What Makes Thinking Machines Lab Interesting

1. **Founder pedigree:** Mira Murati was CTO of OpenAI during its most transformative period, and five co-founders bring deep expertise from OpenAI's core research and engineering teams.

2. **Record-setting seed round:** $2B at $12B valuation — one of the largest seed rounds ever — signals extraordinary investor conviction.

3. **Public benefit corporation:** Unusual for a frontier AI lab, this structure signals a mission-driven approach and gives Murati governance control.

4. **Potential $50B valuation:** If the November 2025 funding talks close, Thinking Machines would rival Anthropic and xAI in valuation within its first year.

5. **Differentiated strategy:** Rather than competing head-on with OpenAI or Anthropic on chat/API, the company is focused on model customization — helping users fine-tune and adapt models to their own needs.

## Uncertainties and Open Questions

- **Revenue and traction:** No public revenue figures as of early 2026. Tinker was only announced in October 2025.
- **$50B round status:** Whether the reported November 2025 fundraise at ~$50B valuation has closed.
- **Competitive positioning:** How Tinker differentiates from existing fine-tuning services (Together AI, Fireworks AI, AWS Bedrock) remains to be fully demonstrated.
