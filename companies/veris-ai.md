# Veris AI

## Overview

Veris AI was founded in 2025 by Mehdi Jamei (PhD, UC Berkeley) and emerged from stealth in June 2025. The company builds a platform that lets enterprises safely train and test AI agents through novel high-fidelity simulated experiences, removing key roadblocks to enterprise AI agent adoption.

## Funding

Veris AI raised an $8.5M seed round co-led by Decibel Ventures and Acrew Capital, with participation from Ian Livingstone, The House Fund, Rocket Money's Idris Mokhtarzada, and Dorothy Chang.

## Key Details

- Emerged from stealth in June 2025
- Addresses the gap between AI agent development and production deployment
- Simulation-based approach enables safe testing before real-world deployment
