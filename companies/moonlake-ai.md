# Moonlake AI

## Overview

Moonlake AI was founded in 2025 by Fan-Yun Sun and Sharon Lee, both Stanford AI Lab and NVIDIA researchers. The company emerged from stealth with a $28M seed round and is building AI models for creating real-time interactive content including simulations and games, enabling anyone to "vibe code" interactive worlds in minutes.

## Funding

Moonlake AI raised a $28M seed round led by AIX Ventures, Threshold, and NVIDIA Ventures, with participation from more than a dozen prominent industry figures including Jeff Dean (Chief Scientist at Google Research), Steve Chen (Founder of YouTube), Naval Ravikant (Founder of AngelList), Ian Goodfellow (inventor of GANs), and Guillermo Rauch (Founder of Vercel).

## Key Details

- Founded by Stanford AI Lab and NVIDIA researchers
- $28M seed is one of the largest seed rounds in the AI simulation space
- Passed 10,000 users on its waitlist and opened beta
