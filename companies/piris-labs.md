# Piris Labs

## Overview

Piris Labs is building next-generation AI inference infrastructure focused on achieving ultra-low latency -- "inference at light speed." As AI agents become more autonomous and need to make real-time decisions, the speed of AI inference becomes a critical bottleneck. Piris Labs addresses this with novel approaches to inference optimization, potentially including custom hardware, software optimization, or novel serving architectures. The company competes in the rapidly growing AI inference market alongside companies like Groq, Cerebras, and SambaNova.
