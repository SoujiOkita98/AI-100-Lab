# Docugami

## One-liner

AI document engineering platform that transforms unstructured business documents into structured, analyzable data using NLP, image recognition, and declarative markup.

## Founders & Leadership

### Jean Paoli -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Co-creator of the XML 1.0 standard for the W3C (alongside Tim Bray and C. Michael Sperberg-McQueen).
- Created Microsoft InfoPath and modern Microsoft Office file formats.
- Former President of Microsoft Open Technologies, Microsoft's open-source subsidiary.
- Spent 10 years developing startups at INRIA, the French institute for research in computer science, before joining Microsoft.

### Additional Co-Founders

Mike Palmer, Andrew Begun, Taqi Jaffri, and Martin Sawicki -- all senior engineering leaders from Microsoft.

## Product & Business Model

Docugami uses AI to help users create and analyze business documents (contracts, reports, etc.) that can then be queried in aggregate as if the contents were stored in a structured database. The platform combines natural language processing, image recognition, and declarative markup techniques.
