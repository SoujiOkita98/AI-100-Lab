# E2B

## One-liner

Open-source cloud platform providing secure sandboxed environments for AI agents to execute code, starting virtual machines in under 200ms at enterprise scale.

## Founders & Leadership

### Vasek Mlejnsky -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Studied at the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics in the Czech Republic.
- Czech entrepreneur who co-founded E2B with longtime friend Tomas Valenta.

### Tomas Valenta -- Co-Founder & CTO

- Also studied at the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics in the Czech Republic.
- Technical co-founder with deep systems and infrastructure engineering expertise.

## Product & Business Model

E2B provides sandboxed cloud environments (virtual machines that spin up in under 200ms) where AI-generated code can execute safely. The platform is open-source and used by 88% of the Fortune 100, with hundreds of millions of cloud sandboxes initiated across more than half of Fortune 500 companies.
