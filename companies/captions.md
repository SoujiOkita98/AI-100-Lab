# Captions

## Overview

Captions (legal name Mirage) is an AI-powered creative studio for video creation and editing. Founded in 2021 by Gaurav Misra (ex-Microsoft) and Dwight Churchill, the platform uses generative AI to automate video editing, captioning, dubbing, and social media management. Backed by top-tier investors including Sequoia, a16z, Kleiner Perkins, and Index Ventures, the company was valued at $500M following its $60M Series C in July 2024. The platform serves millions of content creators and businesses.
