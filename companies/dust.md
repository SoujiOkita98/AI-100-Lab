# Dust

## One-liner

Enterprise AI operating system that enables companies to build and deploy custom data-augmented AI agents without coding, connecting to internal knowledge bases and tools.

## Founders & Leadership

### Stanislas Polu -- Co-Founder & CTO

- Spent 3 years as a Research Engineer at OpenAI studying mathematical reasoning in language models.
- Previously software engineer at Stripe.
- Co-founded Totems with Gabriel Hubert (acquired by Stripe in 2015).

### Gabriel Hubert -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Ecole Polytechnique + Stanford education.
- Former CPO at Alan, one of Europe's fastest-growing insurtech companies.
- Previously at Stripe driving geographical expansion and product.

## Founding Story

Polu and Hubert have known each other for more than a decade. Their first startup, Totems, was acquired by Stripe in 2015. After Polu's stint at OpenAI, they reunited in February 2023 to build Dust, betting that foundation models would fundamentally change how companies work.

## Product & Business Model

Dust provides a no-code platform for enterprises to build custom AI agents that connect to internal data sources (Slack, Notion, Google Drive, etc.) and take actions on behalf of employees. The platform supports multiple LLM providers. As of mid-2025, Dust hit $7.3M ARR with a team of ~66 people.
