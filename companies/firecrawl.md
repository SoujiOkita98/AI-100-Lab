# Firecrawl

## Overview

Firecrawl is a developer-first web data platform that makes the web's constantly changing information accessible and usable for AI agents and LLM applications. Founded in 2024 by Caleb Peffer, Eric Ciarla, and Nicolas Silberstein Camara (previously behind Mendable), the platform is used by 350,000+ developers and has nearly 50,000 GitHub stars. Customers include Shopify, Replit, and Zapier. The company is already profitable and raised a $14.5M Series A led by Nexus Venture Partners with participation from Y Combinator and Shopify CEO Tobias Lutke.
