# Preferred Networks

## Overview
Preferred Networks (PFN) is Japan's most prominent AI company, developing the full AI technology stack from custom AI processors (MN-Core series) to generative AI foundation models (PLaMo) to industrial solutions. Founded in 2014, PFN has applied deep learning across manufacturing (Toyota partnership), transportation, healthcare, entertainment, and education. Valued at approximately $1 billion, PFN is expected to contribute roughly 100 engineers to a new public-private AI initiative backed by SoftBank Group and others to develop large-scale domestic foundation models. The company's MN-Core L1000 processor targets low-power generative AI inference.
