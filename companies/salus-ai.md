# Salus

## Overview

Salus provides guardrails that validate AI agent actions before they execute, acting as a pre-execution safety layer for autonomous AI systems. As AI agents gain the ability to take real-world actions -- sending emails, executing code, making purchases, modifying databases -- Salus ensures these actions are validated against defined policies and safety constraints before execution. This addresses a critical trust gap: organizations want autonomous AI agents but need assurance that agents won't take harmful or unintended actions.
