# Hyperbound

## One-liner

AI-powered sales coaching and role-play platform that enables scalable, realistic practice sessions for enterprise sales teams using conversational AI.

## Founders & Leadership

### Sriharsha Guduguntla -- Co-Founder

- Former Salesforce engineer who contributed to the conversational AI systems behind Salesforce Einstein.
- Longtime friends with co-founder Atul Raghunathan.

### Atul Raghunathan -- Co-Founder

- Former Meta Ads engineer, working on ML models that defined ideal market profiles for billions of ads daily.

## Founding Story

Launched in January 2024 after the founders went through Y Combinator, Hyperbound quickly gained traction in the AI sales coaching market. By September 2025, the company had hit its ambitious end-of-year projections early, adding more than $1M in new ARR for two consecutive months.

## Product & Business Model

Hyperbound's AI role-play platform lets sales teams practice realistic conversations with AI-generated buyer personas. The platform spans industries from SaaS and financial services to logistics and staffing, enabling enterprises to scale sales coaching without relying solely on human trainers.
