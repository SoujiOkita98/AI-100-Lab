# Pocket

## Overview

Pocket is a hardware AI company building what it calls "the world's best AI note-taking device and app." The device is designed to help professionals capture thoughts and conversations seamlessly. In its first five months, Pocket shipped over 30,000 units with 50% month-over-month growth -- numbers confirmed by Y Combinator, representing Series B-level traction from a batch-stage company. With $27M ARR, Pocket is one of the highest-traction companies in the entire YC W26 batch.
