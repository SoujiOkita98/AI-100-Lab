# Paradigm

## One-liner

Agentic AI spreadsheet platform that embeds autonomous AI agents into every cell, enabling teams to research, analyze, and produce deliverables at 10,000x the speed of manual work.

## Founders & Leadership

### Anna Monaco -- Founder & CEO

- Founded Paradigm at age 22 with the audacious vision of replacing Google Sheets and Excel with AI-native spreadsheets.
- Y Combinator Winter 2024 batch participant.

### June Lee & Michael Alfano -- Co-Founders

- Technical co-founders contributing to the core engineering of the agentic spreadsheet platform.

## Founding Story

Anna Monaco, at 22 years old, set out to reimagine the spreadsheet -- one of the most ubiquitous software categories -- by embedding "swarms of AI agents" directly into the grid. The company emerged from stealth in September 2024 with $2M from Y Combinator and angels, then raised a $5M seed from General Catalyst in August 2025 as the product went to general availability.

## Product & Business Model

### What Paradigm Does

1. **Agentic spreadsheet** -- Each cell can contain an autonomous AI agent that gathers, analyzes, and reports on data.
2. **Agent swarms** -- Deploys multiple agents in parallel to process up to 5,000 cells per minute (10,000x faster than manual work).
3. **Cross-functional use cases** -- Consulting, recruiting, finance, marketing, sales, and other data-intensive workflows.

### Pricing / Monetization

SaaS model with tiered pricing. The platform targets knowledge workers and teams who spend significant time on data collection and analysis.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning |
|-----------|------------|
| **Clay** | Data enrichment with spreadsheet UI for GTM |
| **Google Sheets + AI add-ons** | Incumbent with AI features being added |
| **Microsoft Excel Copilot** | AI assistant layered on legacy spreadsheet |
| **Rows.com** | Spreadsheet with built-in data integrations |
| **Airtable** | Database-spreadsheet hybrid |

Paradigm differentiates with the "agent in every cell" paradigm -- fundamentally reimagining the spreadsheet as an AI orchestration layer rather than adding AI features to a traditional grid.

## What Makes Paradigm Interesting

1. **Bold category bet.** Taking on Google Sheets and Excel with an AI-native approach.
2. **Speed of execution.** 5,000 cells per minute demonstrates tangible productivity gains.
3. **Strong angel network.** Backed by founders of major SaaS companies (Ramp, Dropbox, LangChain).
4. **Young founder energy.** 22-year-old CEO brings fresh perspective to a 40-year-old software category.

## Risks & Open Questions

- Competing against Google and Microsoft in the spreadsheet category is existential-level competition.
- Early stage with modest funding relative to the ambition.
- Spreadsheet workflows are deeply entrenched; switching costs may be higher than expected.
