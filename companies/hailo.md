# Hailo

## Overview
Hailo is an Israeli AI chip company that designs energy-efficient AI processors optimized for edge computing. Its chips enable real-time AI inference in resource-constrained environments including autonomous vehicles, security cameras, smart cities, and autonomous mobile robots. Valued at $1 billion (unicorn), Hailo competes with NVIDIA in the edge AI space and has offices in the US, Germany, China, Japan, Korea, and Taiwan. The company was reportedly preparing a new significant fundraising round as of September 2025.
