# Gong

## Overview

Gong is an AI-powered revenue intelligence platform that uses machine learning and NLP to analyze sales conversations, meetings, and emails to provide actionable insights for sales teams. Founded in 2015 by Amit Bendov and Eilon Reshef, the company has grown to serve over 4,000 customers and reached $332M in revenue in 2024. Gong achieved unicorn status in August 2020 and was valued at $7.25B following its $250M Series E in June 2021, led by Franklin Templeton. The company has raised $584M in total funding from investors including Coatue, Sequoia, Battery Ventures, and Norwest.
