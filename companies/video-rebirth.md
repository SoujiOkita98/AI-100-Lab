# Video Rebirth

## Overview

Video Rebirth is a Singapore-based AI video startup building what it calls the world's first industrial-grade AI video engine. The company's flagship product, the Bach series, uses novel architectures -- Dual DiT for prompt adherence and Physics Native Attention (PNA) for temporal consistency -- to generate coherent, controllable, commercial-ready video at native 30fps.

Founded by Dr. Wei Liu (ex-Tencent Distinguished Scientist, IEEE Fellow) and Dan Kong (former G42 AI investor), the company is positioned at the intersection of Asian AI talent and Southeast Asian startup ecosystem.

## Funding Story

Initial $50M round in November 2025, extended by $30M in March 2026, with AMD Ventures and Hyundai participating. Total $80M raised.

## What Makes Them Interesting

1. **Industrial-grade focus.** While competitors target consumer/creator markets, Video Rebirth emphasizes commercial and industrial video generation applications.
2. **Physics-native architecture.** The PNA (Physics Native Attention) approach to temporal consistency is technically differentiated from the dominant diffusion transformer approaches.
3. **AMD + Hyundai backing.** AMD Ventures participation suggests potential for AMD GPU optimization, while Hyundai signals automotive/industrial applications.
4. **Singapore hub.** Operating from Singapore provides access to both Chinese AI talent and global markets, with less geopolitical risk than a mainland China base.
