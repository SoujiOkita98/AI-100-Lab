# Rubric

## Overview

Rubric is a post-training research and product lab focused on the critical phase of AI model development that occurs after initial pre-training. Post-training -- including RLHF, DPO, constitutional AI, and other alignment techniques -- has become the primary differentiator between raw base models and production-ready AI systems. Rubric aims to build tools and methodologies that make post-training more efficient, reproducible, and effective, addressing a key bottleneck in the AI development pipeline.
