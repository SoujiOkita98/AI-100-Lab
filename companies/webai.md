# webAI

## Overview

webAI was founded in 2019 by David Stout, Tyler Mauer, and Ethan Baird. The company develops a distributed AI platform that enables enterprises to build "sovereign" AI models on localized infrastructure, offering a private alternative to cloud-hosted AI. Their systems are designed to run locally on users' devices.

## Funding

webAI raised $60M in a Series A in September 2024 at a $700M valuation. In January 2026, the company closed a Series A extension at a $2.5B pre-money valuation with participation from Marc Benioff's Time Ventures, Atreides Management, and Forerunner Ventures. The extension amount was described as "high double-digit" millions but was not precisely disclosed.

## Key Details

- Unicorn status achieved with $2.5B valuation in January 2026
- Emphasis on data sovereignty and privacy — AI runs on local infrastructure
- Positioned as alternative to cloud-dependent AI platforms
