# Human Native AI

## Overview

Human Native AI is a London-based startup founded in 2024 by James Smith and Jack Galilee, building a marketplace to broker licensing deals between companies training LLMs and rights holders willing to license their data. The platform facilitates secure and streamlined licensing of rights-cleared data for AI development through a single technical integration and standardized legal agreements. The company raised $3.6M in seed funding from LocalGlobe and Mercuri, and was subsequently acquired.
