# Generalist AI

## Overview

Generalist AI is a robotics AI company founded in 2024 by Andy Zeng, Pete Florence, and Andrew Barry, all former Google DeepMind researchers. Pete Florence co-authored RT-2 (Robotic Transformer 2) and PaLM-E, two landmark papers in embodied AI. The team also includes veterans from OpenAI and Boston Dynamics. The company's mission is to make general-purpose robots a reality through embodied intelligence scaling laws. Generalist raised a massive $140M seed round in March 2025, backed by Bezos Expeditions, NVentures (NVIDIA), Boldstart Ventures, and NFDG.
