# Basepilot

## Overview

Basepilot builds AI coworkers that collaborate with humans to automate repetitive browser-based work. Users can teach Basepilot new skills just by demonstrating tasks, and the AI learns to replicate them. The platform targets finance, recruitment, and sales teams, claiming to save 30% of time and resources per week on back-office tasks.

## Founders

- **Pascal Wieler** — Worked on robotics and self-driving at Carnegie Mellon and Mercedes-Benz Research
