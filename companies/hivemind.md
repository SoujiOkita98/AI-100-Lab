# Hivemind

## Overview

Hivemind is an early-stage AI hiring platform that uses AI recruiting software to screen candidates, automate hiring workflows, and help teams make faster hiring decisions. The company appears to have been bootstrapped for approximately two years and recently launched publicly, including on Product Hunt. Hivemind offers skill-based hiring on autopilot, positioning itself as AI recruiting software purpose-built for modern teams.

## Founder

- **Ali Elzeiny** - Founder. UC Berkeley, PhD Engineering from UC Irvine; ex-TikTok engineer; previously founded RocketDevs. Origin: United States (Egyptian heritage).

## Product

Hivemind provides:
- AI-powered candidate screening
- Automated hiring workflows
- Skill-based assessment and matching
- Recruiting process automation

**Pricing:** The company offers plans including a $199 lifetime license, suggesting an early product-market fit search with aggressive pricing.
