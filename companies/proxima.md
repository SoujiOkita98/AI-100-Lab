# Proxima

## Overview

Proxima (formerly VantAI) is an AI-driven drug discovery company focused on proximity-based therapeutics -- drugs that control how proteins interact with each other. The company uses machine learning to design molecules that bring target proteins together or push them apart, enabling a new class of medicines.

Spun out from Roivant Sciences in 2023, Proxima has already established partnerships with Johnson & Johnson, Bristol-Myers Squibb, and Blueprint Medicines (acquired by Sanofi). Multiple co-developed programs are advancing toward the clinic, with the first expected to enter clinical trials in 2026.

## Funding Story

The $80M seed round led by DCVC with Nvidia's NVentures participating is one of the largest seed rounds in biotech, reflecting the convergence of AI and drug discovery.

## What Makes Them Interesting

1. **Pharma partnerships validate the platform.** J&J, BMS, and Sanofi partnerships before a public funding round is unusual for a computational biotech startup.
2. **Proximity-based therapeutics.** A novel mechanism of action that could open up previously undruggable targets.
3. **Nvidia backing.** NVentures participation signals conviction that AI-driven drug design will be a major compute workload.
4. **Near-clinical programs.** Unlike many AI-bio startups, Proxima has programs approaching IND-enabling studies, reducing the technology risk.
