# Worktrace AI

## Overview

Worktrace AI is an AI workflow automation startup founded by former OpenAI product manager Angela Jiang and researcher Deepak Vasisht. The company builds AI agents that study a company's internal workflows and then help automate them, bridging the gap between AI capabilities and real business value.

## Founders

### Angela Jiang -- Co-Founder & CEO

- **Origin:** Mainland Chinese. Graduated from the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, with a double degree in economics and computer science.
- **OpenAI Career:** Jiang helped oversee the launch of GPT-3.5 (ChatGPT) and GPT-4 -- two of the most influential AI model releases in history. After the launches, she spent over a year meeting with policymakers and regulators on behalf of OpenAI.
- **Team Composition:** Approximately two-thirds of the Worktrace team (~30 people) are former OpenAI employees, including individuals who worked on ChatGPT deployment.

### Deepak Vasisht -- Co-Founder

- **Background:** Veteran researcher and academic with expertise in systems and networking.

## Chinese Founder Context

Angela Jiang is a mainland Chinese founder who occupied a critical product role at OpenAI during its most transformative period. Her departure to found Worktrace AI represents the pattern of Chinese talent leaving frontier AI labs to start their own companies, bringing operational knowledge of how the world's most advanced AI systems are built and deployed.

## Funding

Worktrace launched with $9.3M in seed funding led by Conviction and 8VC. OpenAI itself invested through its OpenAI Fund, and notable OpenAI alumni including former CTO Mira Murati and CSO Jason Kwon participated as angels. Reports suggest the actual seed round closed at over $20M.

## What Makes Worktrace Interesting

1. **OpenAI alumni pedigree.** Founded by the PM who launched ChatGPT and GPT-4, with 2/3 of the team from OpenAI.
2. **OpenAI as investor.** Rare case of OpenAI investing in a startup founded by its own alumni.
3. **Workflow automation focus.** Rather than building general-purpose agents, Worktrace studies specific company workflows and automates them -- a pragmatic approach to the agent problem.
4. **Chinese founder with unique AI policy experience.** Jiang's time meeting with policymakers gives her rare insight into AI governance, regulation, and enterprise adoption barriers.
