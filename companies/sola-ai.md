# Sola

## Overview

Sola is an agentic process automation platform founded in 2023 by Jessica Wu and Neil Deshmukh. The company enables businesses to automate workflows across any application without code, consultants, or replacing existing systems. Sola powers critical workflows for Fortune 100 enterprises, AmLaw 100 firms, and billion-dollar leaders in healthcare and logistics.

## Funding

Sola raised a $3.5M seed round led by Conviction (Sarah Guo), followed by a $17.5M Series A led by Andreessen Horowitz in August 2025, with participation from Conviction and Y Combinator, bringing total funding to $21M.

## Key Details

- Y Combinator alumnus
- Jessica Wu, the CEO, was previously a quant before founding Sola
- Platform-agnostic approach works across any enterprise application stack
