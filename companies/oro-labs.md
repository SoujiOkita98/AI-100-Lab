# ORO Labs

## Overview
ORO Labs is a Palo Alto-based startup founded in 2020 that provides an AI-native procurement orchestration platform. The platform coordinates people, processes, and systems to automate procurement front-end processes including new procurement requests, supplier onboarding, and compliance checks. ORO Labs' software is used by enterprises including Coca-Cola, Siemens Energy, and Novartis, with deployments across organizations operating in more than 100 countries. The company has raised $160M total and reported 300% revenue growth, demonstrating strong product-market fit in the enterprise procurement automation space.
