# Blue J

## Overview
Blue J is a Toronto-based legal technology company transforming tax research with generative AI. Founded in 2015 by University of Toronto law professors, the company relaunched its product in 2023 after integrating OpenAI's large language model technology, enabling users to receive answers to complex tax queries in seconds. Blue J serves more than 2,500 organizations (up from 200 in 2021) and more than doubled its revenue and customer base in the first half of 2025. The company is valued at over US$300 million.
