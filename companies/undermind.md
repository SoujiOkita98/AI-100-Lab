# Undermind

## Overview

Undermind is an AI-powered research assistant that reads and analyzes hundreds of academic papers to help scientists and researchers discover the most relevant literature for their work. Founded by two PhD graduates in quantum physics from MIT, the platform goes beyond keyword search to deeply understand scientific content.
