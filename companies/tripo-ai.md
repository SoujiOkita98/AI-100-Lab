# Tripo AI

## Overview

Tripo AI is an AI-powered platform for generating high-quality 3D models from text prompts or images in seconds. Developed by VAST, a Beijing-based technology company founded in March 2023, Tripo has rapidly become one of the leading AI 3D generation tools globally, serving over 3 million professional users with 90% of its user base outside China.

What makes Tripo notable is the pedigree of its co-founder Simon Song, who previously co-founded MiniMax -- one of China's "Six AI Tigers" that IPO'd on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange in January 2026. This gives Tripo unusual credibility and network effects in the Chinese AI ecosystem.

## Founders and Team Background

### Simon Song (Co-founder)

- **Origin:** Mainland China
- **Background:** Previously co-founded MiniMax, one of China's most prominent AI startups, which developed the Talkie consumer AI app and listed on the HKEX. Song's experience building a multi-billion-dollar AI company from scratch gives Tripo a significant advantage in fundraising, talent recruitment, and strategic partnerships.
- **Profile:** Serial AI entrepreneur, now applying foundation model expertise to 3D generation.

### Guoli Su (Co-founder)

- **Origin:** Mainland China
- **Background:** Research background affiliated with Tsinghua University, focused on 3D reconstruction and generative AI.

## Products and Technology

### Tripo 3D Generation Platform

Tripo's core product enables users to generate 3D models from:
- **Text prompts:** Describe an object and get a 3D model in seconds
- **Images:** Upload a 2D image and generate a corresponding 3D model
- **Multi-view generation:** Generate 3D assets from multiple reference images

### Tripo 2.0

Released in 2024-2025, Tripo 2.0 represented a major quality leap with more realistic textures and better geometric consistency. Originated from Tsinghua-affiliated research.

### Enterprise Integration

Tripo has signed over 40,000 studios and corporate partners, including major names:
- **Gaming:** Tencent, NetEase
- **Technology:** Microsoft, Sony, HTC

## Business Model

1. **SaaS subscriptions:** Credit-based pricing for individual users and studios
2. **Enterprise API:** Bulk 3D generation for gaming studios, e-commerce platforms, and AR/VR applications
3. **Platform partnerships:** Integration deals with major gaming and tech companies

**Revenue traction:** Tripo reached $12M ARR and over $1M monthly revenue as of late 2025, approximately 2.5 years after founding. With 3M+ professional users, this implies relatively low ARPU with room for expansion through enterprise upselling.

## What Makes Tripo Interesting

1. **MiniMax co-founder pedigree.** Simon Song co-founded one of China's most successful AI companies before starting Tripo. This is rare serial-founder quality in China's AI startup scene.

2. **Global-first from China.** With 90% of users outside China, Tripo defies the stereotype of Chinese AI companies being domestically focused. It competes directly with Western 3D generation tools on a global stage.

3. **Enterprise partnerships at scale.** 40,000+ studio and corporate partners -- including Tencent, NetEase, Microsoft, Sony, and Stability AI -- represent significant distribution and validation.

4. **3D generation is an underserved niche.** While text-to-image and text-to-video have attracted massive investment, AI 3D generation has fewer well-funded competitors but equally large market potential (gaming, e-commerce, AR/VR, industrial design).

5. **Hong Kong IPO aspirations.** The company's stated interest in USD fundraising and Hong Kong listing suggests ambitions to become a publicly traded 3D AI platform company.

## Uncertainties and Caveats

- $12M ARR, while demonstrating product-market fit, is still relatively early. The path to profitability in 3D generation is unproven.
- Competition from larger players (Microsoft's Rodin, Adobe's 3D tools) could intensify as 3D generation improves.
- The gaming industry, Tripo's primary market, is cyclical and faces regulatory headwinds in China specifically.
- Valuation is not publicly confirmed. The $50M Series A suggests a valuation in the $200-400M range but this is speculative.
