# Conduct

## One-liner

AI-powered platform that enables enterprises to engage directly with their legacy ERP environments, using AI agents to modernize and automate complex business operations.

## Founders & Leadership

### Jan Philipp Haas -- Co-Founder & CEO
- Former Palantir engineer who saw firsthand the challenges enterprises face with legacy ERP systems.

### Philipp Hoefer -- Co-Founder & CPO
- Former Palantir engineer with deep product expertise.

### Henry Thompson -- Co-Founder & CTO
- Former Palantir engineer with technical leadership background.

## Product & Business Model

Conduct's AI-powered platform allows enterprises to engage directly with their ERP environments, peeling back layers of hidden business logic and operational code. The platform uses AI agents to transform legacy IT systems. Customers include European enterprises such as Daimler Truck and Rittal.
