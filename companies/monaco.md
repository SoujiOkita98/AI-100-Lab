# Monaco

## Overview

Monaco is an AI-native sales platform that emerged from stealth on February 11, 2026. Co-founded by brothers Sam and Brian Blond (both ex-VCs with sales backgrounds), along with Abishek Viswanathan (ex-CPO at Apollo and Qualtrics) and Malay Desai (ex-SVP Engineering at Clari), the company is targeting seed and Series A startups with a product suite that includes an AI-native CRM and a built-from-scratch prospecting database.

## Funding Story

Founders Fund led both the $10M seed and $25M Series A, an unusual double-down that reflects the firm's conviction in the team. The angel investor list -- Stripe founders Patrick and John Collison, YC CEO Garry Tan, Greenoaks founder Neil Mehta -- adds significant signal.

## What Makes Them Interesting

1. **VC-to-founder pipeline.** Sam Blond's time at Founders Fund gave him a front-row seat to what early-stage startups actually need from sales tools.
2. **Anti-Salesforce positioning.** Building specifically for startups that find Salesforce too complex and expensive is a well-defined wedge.
3. **Four-founder team.** Combines sales expertise (Blonds), product leadership (Viswanathan from Apollo/Qualtrics), and engineering depth (Desai from Clari).
4. **Angel investor quality.** Stripe founders and YC CEO as angels provide distribution and credibility with the target customer base.
