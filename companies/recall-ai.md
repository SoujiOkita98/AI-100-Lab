# Recall.ai

## Overview

Recall.ai is a meeting bot API company founded in 2022 by David Gu (CEO) and Amanda Zhu (COO), both Y Combinator alumni. The platform provides a universal API for getting recordings, transcripts, and metadata from meetings across Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and other platforms. Recall.ai powers conversation intelligence for hundreds of AI products. The company reached ~$31M ARR by January 2026 (up from $8M ARR at end of 2024), and was valued at $250M after its $38M Series B led by Bessemer Venture Partners.
