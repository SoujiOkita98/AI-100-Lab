# Fasikl

## Overview
Fasikl is a University of Minnesota spinoff (2019) developing AI-powered wearable medical devices for neurological conditions. Their flagship product, the Felix NeuroAI Wristband, is the first and only AI-powered treatment for essential tremor, receiving FDA 510(k) clearance in 2025. The wristband uses a cloud-based AI platform to dynamically adjust neurostimulation in real time.

## Investors
Qiming Venture Partners, Google for Startups, Llama Venture, Tailwind Ventures, iSeed Ventures.
