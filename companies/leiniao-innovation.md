# Leiniao Innovation

## Overview
Leiniao Innovation (also known as Leiniao Technology) is a Chinese startup founded in October 2021 that designs and manufactures AI-powered augmented reality glasses. The company is affiliated with TCL Electronics Holdings. Its product portfolio includes the AI + AR glasses X series, the viewing glasses Air series, and the AI shooting glasses V series. In Q2 2025, Leiniao ranked first globally in AR glasses sales with a 39% market share according to Counterpoint Research. Products are available in more than 25 countries and regions. The company completed a Series C round approaching 1 billion yuan, the largest single-round financing in China's AI + AR glasses sector.
