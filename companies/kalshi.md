# Kalshi

> The first CFTC-regulated prediction market in the U.S., now valued at $22 billion, processing $2 billion in transactions per week.

## Company Overview

Kalshi is a web-based prediction market platform that allows users to trade contracts tied to real-world outcomes across weather, sports, pop culture, economics, and politics. Founded in 2018 by MIT classmates Tarek Mansour (Algerian-born) and Luana Lopes Lara (Brazilian-born), Kalshi launched in July 2021 and is the first exchange in the United States to be regulated by the CFTC (Commodity Futures Trading Commission) for event contracts.

The company has experienced explosive growth, reaching $2 billion in weekly transaction volume by early 2026. Its valuation doubled from $11 billion to $22 billion in just three months (December 2025 to March 2026).

## Founders

### Tarek Mansour -- Co-Founder / CEO
Algerian-born quantitative finance specialist. Met co-founder Luana Lopes Lara at MIT. Built Kalshi from the insight that prediction markets could serve as a better information mechanism than polls or expert opinions, if properly regulated.

### Luana Lopes Lara -- Co-Founder
Brazilian-born financial analyst. MIT classmate of Mansour. CNBC profiled her as taking "the biggest prediction markets risks."

## AI Relevance

While Kalshi is primarily a fintech/prediction market company, it is increasingly relevant to the AI landscape:
- Uses AI/ML models for market making, fraud detection, and risk management
- Prediction markets are emerging as a key mechanism for AI forecasting and alignment research
- Several AI labs and researchers use prediction markets as calibration tools
- The platform represents a novel application of information aggregation that competes with AI-based forecasting systems

## Business Model

Kalshi generates revenue through:
- **Transaction fees:** Per-contract fees on trades
- **Market making spread:** Bid-ask spreads on event contracts
- **Data licensing:** Potential future revenue from prediction market data as a signal

## Key Metrics

- $2 billion in weekly transaction volume
- CFTC-regulated (first U.S. event contract exchange)
- Valuation doubled from $11B to $22B in 3 months

## What Makes Kalshi Interesting

1. **Regulatory first-mover:** First and only CFTC-regulated prediction market in the U.S., creating a massive moat against competitors.
2. **Explosive growth trajectory:** From $5B to $22B valuation in under 6 months, driven by prediction markets becoming mainstream (especially during election cycles).
3. **Immigrant founder story:** Algerian and Brazilian founders who met at MIT, building a uniquely American financial product.
4. **Information market for AI era:** Prediction markets represent a complementary (or competing) paradigm to AI-based forecasting, making Kalshi relevant to the AI ecosystem.
5. **Top-tier investor roster:** Sequoia, a16z, Coatue, ARK Invest, CapitalG, Paradigm -- an unusually broad coalition of both traditional VC and crypto-native investors.
