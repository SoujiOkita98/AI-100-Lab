# LiveX AI

## Overview
LiveX AI deploys AI agents for customer support, retention, and engagement. Partners include Google Cloud, NVIDIA, HubSpot, AWS, and Zendesk. Claims 78%+ cost savings and 90%+ self-resolution rates for clients.

## Founders
All three co-founders have deep AI research backgrounds. Jia Li is one of the most prominent Chinese-American AI researchers — she was the founding head of Google Cloud AI R&D and is closely associated with Fei-Fei Li at Stanford.
