# Port

## Overview
Port is an Israeli startup building an Agentic Engineering Platform (AEP) that automates operational work across the entire software development lifecycle. The platform powers hundreds of customers worldwide including GitHub, British Telecom, Visa, Sonar, StubHub, and Nando's. Port emerged from the founders' experience building an internal developer portal for IDF Unit 8200 and has seen 300% revenue growth in the past year. Valued at $800 million with 200 employees across Israel and the US, Port competes with Spotify's Backstage in the developer platform space.
