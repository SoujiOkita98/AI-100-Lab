# Castari

## Overview

Castari lets developers deploy AI agents in secure, autoscaling sandboxes with a single command. The platform handles tool/MCP connectors, snapshots, and observability out of the box, abstracting away the infrastructure complexity of running autonomous AI agents in production. As AI agents move from demos to production workloads, Castari addresses the critical need for secure, isolated execution environments with built-in monitoring. The company is part of the massive agent infrastructure wave in YC's W26 batch.
