# Resolve AI

## One-liner

Autonomous AI SRE platform that investigates production incidents, identifies root causes, and executes remediations in minutes instead of hours.

## Founders & Leadership

### Spiros Xanthos -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Co-creator of OpenTelemetry, the industry-standard observability framework.
- Founded Omnition, acquired by Splunk in 2019. Subsequently served as GM & SVP of Splunk Observability.
- Met co-founder Mayank Agarwal in grad school at UIUC over 20 years ago.

### Mayank Agarwal -- Co-Founder & CTO

- Co-creator of OpenTelemetry. Former Chief Architect across all Splunk observability product lines.
- Deep technical background in distributed systems and production reliability.

## Founding Story

Founded in early 2024 by two longtime collaborators who spent their careers building the observability stack (OpenTelemetry, Omnition, Splunk Observability), Resolve AI was born from the insight that while monitoring tools generate massive amounts of telemetry data, the actual incident response process remains painfully manual. The founders set out to build AI agents that can autonomously investigate and resolve production incidents.

## Product & Business Model

### What Resolve AI Does

Resolve AI builds an autonomous SRE platform that uses agentic AI to:

1. **Investigate incidents** -- Automatically triages alerts, correlates signals across logs, metrics, and traces.
2. **Identify root causes** -- Uses deep reasoning to pinpoint the source of production issues.
3. **Execute remediations** -- Suggests or autonomously applies fixes, reducing MTTR from hours to minutes.

### Pricing / Monetization

Enterprise SaaS model targeting DevOps and SRE teams at mid-to-large technology companies.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning |
|-----------|------------|
| **PagerDuty** | Legacy incident management; adding AI features |
| **Shoreline.io** | Incident automation with runbooks |
| **Rootly** | Incident management and response coordination |
| **FireHydrant** | Incident management platform |
| **BigPanda** | AIOps event correlation |

Resolve AI differentiates with fully autonomous investigation and remediation, leveraging the founders' deep domain expertise in observability infrastructure.

## What Makes Resolve AI Interesting

1. **Founder-market fit.** Co-creators of OpenTelemetry with exits to Splunk -- arguably the most qualified team to build autonomous SRE.
2. **Massive Series A.** $125M at $1B valuation reflects strong conviction in the autonomous SRE category.
3. **From monitoring to action.** Bridges the gap between observability data collection and actual incident resolution.

## Risks & Open Questions

- Early product; must prove autonomous remediation is reliable enough for production use.
- Enterprise sales cycles in DevOps/SRE can be long.
- Incumbents (PagerDuty, Datadog) are adding AI features to existing platforms.
