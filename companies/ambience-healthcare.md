# Ambience Healthcare

> Ambient AI platform for health systems, automating clinical documentation, coding, and workflows -- backed by a16z and OpenAI, valued at $1.25 billion.

## Company Overview

Ambience Healthcare builds an ambient AI platform that automates clinical documentation, medical coding, and clinical workflow automation for health systems. Founded in 2020 by Mike Ng and Nikhil Buduma, the San Francisco-based company reached unicorn status in 2025 with a $1.25 billion valuation after raising $243 million in Series C funding. The company is part of the broader "AI scribe" wave in healthcare, but differentiates by offering a full-stack platform that extends beyond documentation into coding and operational workflows.

## Business Model

Enterprise SaaS for health systems. Ambience provides:
- **Ambient documentation:** AI-generated clinical notes from physician-patient conversations
- **Automated medical coding:** AI-powered CPT and ICD code suggestions
- **Workflow automation:** Referral management, prior authorization, and other administrative tasks

## What Makes Ambience Healthcare Interesting

1. **Full-stack healthcare AI platform:** Unlike point-solution AI scribes (e.g., Nuance DAX), Ambience extends into coding, referrals, and operational workflows -- a broader value proposition for health systems.
2. **OpenAI-backed:** One of the early OpenAI Startup Fund investments in healthcare, signaling alignment with frontier model capabilities.
3. **Unicorn status in competitive space:** Reached $1.25B valuation despite intense competition from Abridge, Nuance/Microsoft, and Nabla. The AI scribe category collectively raised nearly $1B in 2025 alone.
4. **a16z healthcare thesis:** Identified by a16z as a core example of their thesis that "healthcare is the new dev-tools" -- a sector where AI can dramatically improve productivity of highly skilled professionals.
