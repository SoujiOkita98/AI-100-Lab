# Harmonic

## Overview

Harmonic is an AI company building mathematical superintelligence, co-founded in 2023 by Tudor Achim (former CTO of Helm.ai, Stanford/CMU background) and Vlad Tenev (CEO of Robinhood Markets). The company's flagship product, Aristotle, specializes in formal mathematical reasoning using the Lean 4 proof assistant to translate natural-language math problems into formally verifiable proofs. Harmonic raised $295M across three rounds in just 14 months, reaching a $1.45B valuation (unicorn status) by November 2025.
