# Cascade

## Overview

Cascade is building safety and reliability infrastructure for autonomous AI agents. As AI agents gain more autonomy to execute real-world actions -- from writing code to managing finances to making legal decisions -- the need for robust safety guardrails becomes critical. Cascade addresses this by providing governance, monitoring, and control mechanisms that ensure AI agents operate within defined boundaries. The company is part of the AI agent security cluster in YC's W26 batch, alongside companies like Clam and Salus.
