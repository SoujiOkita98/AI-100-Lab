# Roboflow

## Overview

Roboflow is an end-to-end computer vision development platform that simplifies building, training, and deploying visual AI models. Founded in 2019 by Joseph Nelson and Brad Dwyer (both with backgrounds in AR and AI), the platform has been used by over one million developers for tasks ranging from dataset management and automated labeling to model training and deployment. The company raised a $40M Series B led by GV (Google Ventures) in November 2024, bringing total funding to $64M. Other investors include Craft Ventures, Y Combinator, and notable angels like Google's Jeff Dean and Replit's Amjad Masad.
