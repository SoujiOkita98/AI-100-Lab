# MosaicML

## One-liner

AI model training platform that made it dramatically easier and cheaper for enterprises to train custom large language models, acquired by Databricks for $1.3B.

## Founders & Leadership

### Naveen Rao -- Co-Founder & CEO

- Serial AI entrepreneur who previously founded Nervana Systems, a deep learning chip company acquired by Intel for $350M in 2016.
- Served as GM of Intel's AI Products Group post-acquisition.
- Indian-American entrepreneur recognized as one of the leading figures in AI infrastructure.

### Hanlin Tang -- Co-Founder & President

- AI researcher with a PhD in computational neuroscience.
- Expertise in efficient neural network training and optimization.

### Jonathan Frankle -- Founding Adviser & Chief Scientist

- Harvard professor and Kempner Institute researcher.
- Known for the Lottery Ticket Hypothesis, a foundational paper on neural network pruning and efficient training.

## Founding Story

Founded in 2021 by Naveen Rao (fresh from his Intel/Nervana experience) and Hanlin Tang, with Jonathan Frankle as chief scientist, MosaicML exited stealth with $37M in funding and a clear thesis: training large AI models was too expensive, too slow, and too inaccessible for most companies. They built tools and algorithms that enabled 2x-7x faster training compared to standard approaches, and with near-linear scaling, multi-billion-parameter models could be trained in hours instead of days.

## Product & Business Model

### What MosaicML Did

1. **MosaicML Training Platform** -- Cloud-based platform for training custom LLMs with automatic optimization.
2. **MPT Models** -- Open-source family of large language models (MPT-7B, MPT-30B) that demonstrated competitive performance with commercially permissive licenses.
3. **Composer** -- Open-source library of training efficiency methods that could be composed together for maximum speedup.

### Strategic Value to Databricks

Databricks acquired MosaicML to enable its customers to build, own, and secure their own generative AI models using their proprietary data -- complementing Databricks' data lakehouse platform with AI training capabilities.

## What Makes MosaicML Interesting

1. **Speed to $1.3B exit.** Founded in 2021, acquired for $1.3B just two years later -- one of the fastest major AI exits.
2. **Open-source strategy.** MPT models demonstrated that open-source could compete with proprietary models, driving adoption and talent acquisition.
3. **Naveen Rao's track record.** Second major AI exit (Nervana to Intel for $350M, MosaicML to Databricks for $1.3B).
4. **Training efficiency thesis.** Proved that algorithmic optimization could dramatically reduce the cost of training large models.

## Post-Acquisition

The MosaicML team and technology became the foundation of Databricks' generative AI capabilities, powering the Mosaic AI suite within the Databricks platform. The MPT model family evolved into DBRX, Databricks' flagship open-source LLM.
