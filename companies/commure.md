# Commure

## Overview
Commure was incubated by General Catalyst in 2017 as a healthcare developer platform. In October 2023, it merged with Athelas (AI healthcare workflow company) to form a $6B combined entity. The platform spans ambient clinical documentation (AI Scribe), revenue cycle management, patient engagement, and care coordination. Acquired Augmedix ($139M, clinical AI) and Memora Health in 2024.

## Investors
General Catalyst, Founders Fund, 8VC, Human Capital, Greenoaks Capital, Sequoia Capital, Y Combinator, Lux Capital.
