# MosaicML

## Overview
MosaicML built tools and infrastructure to make training large AI models more efficient and affordable. Acquired by Databricks for ~$1.3B in July 2023 — representing a ~6x jump from their $222M Series A valuation. All 62 employees joined Databricks. The company had approximately $20M in revenue at the time of acquisition.

## Acquisition
- **Acquirer:** Databricks
- **Announced:** June 26, 2023
- **Completed:** July 19, 2023
- **Price:** ~$1.3B (inclusive of retention packages)
