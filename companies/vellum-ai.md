# Vellum

## Overview

Vellum is an end-to-end AI development platform founded in 2023 by Noa Flaherty, Sidd Seethepalli, and Akash Sharma (Y Combinator batch). The platform helps teams define, evaluate, and deploy AI-powered applications with a collaborative environment for both technical and non-technical team members. Features include a visual builder, SDK, prompt management, evaluation workflows, and deployment tooling. The company hit $3.5M in revenue by 2024 and raised a $20M Series A led by Leaders Fund in July 2025.
