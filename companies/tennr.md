# Tennr

> AI-powered healthcare document automation that processes 10 million medical documents per month, fixing the broken patient referral system.

## Company Overview

Tennr is a New York City-based healthcare AI company that automates patient referral workflows and medical document processing. Founded in 2021, the company built RaeLM, a proprietary vision-language model trained on 100 million anonymized healthcare documents, 2.3 billion distinct data fields, and 8,000 sets of criteria. The platform now processes 10 million documents per month.

## Technology

Tennr built **RaeLM**, its proprietary vision-language model trained on:
- 100 million anonymized healthcare documents
- 2.3 billion distinct data fields
- 8,000 sets of referral criteria

The model automates the patient referral process end-to-end, parsing faxes, PDFs, and other unstructured medical documents to extract relevant clinical data and route referrals appropriately.

## Business Model

Enterprise SaaS for healthcare systems. The company is in the eight figures of revenue as of mid-2025, having tripled revenue between its Series B and Series C rounds. Customers include healthcare systems and specialty practices that handle high volumes of patient referrals.

## What Makes Tennr Interesting

1. **Massive, unsexy problem:** Patient referrals are one of healthcare's most broken, paper-intensive processes. Tennr's AI tackles a high-volume, high-pain-point workflow that directly impacts patient access to care.
2. **Proprietary model advantage:** RaeLM, trained on 100M healthcare documents, creates a data moat that is difficult for general-purpose LLMs to replicate.
3. **Revenue velocity:** Tripled revenue between Series B (Oct 2024) and Series C (Jun 2025), reaching eight-figure ARR.
4. **Blue-chip healthcare investor stack:** a16z, IVP, Lightspeed, GV, ICONIQ, and Frank Slootman (former Snowflake CEO) all backing the company.
5. **Cleveland Clinic talent pipeline:** A former Cleveland Clinic doctor recently joined Tennr, signaling clinical credibility.
