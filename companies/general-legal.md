# General Legal

## Overview

General Legal is an AI-native law firm targeting growth-stage companies. The platform combines AI automation with human lawyers to deliver same-day turnaround on legal work that traditionally takes weeks. Part of a notable cluster of legal-AI startups in YC's W26 batch (alongside Arcline, Vector Legal, and Legalos), General Legal represents Y Combinator's strong bet on AI transforming legal services delivery. The firm leverages AI to handle the majority of routine legal work while keeping experienced lawyers in the loop for review and complex matters.
