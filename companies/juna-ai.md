# Juna AI

## One-liner

AI-powered platform that transforms manufacturing facilities into intelligent, self-learning systems for autonomous process control, higher margins, and lower carbon footprint.

## Founders & Leadership

### Matthias Auf der Mauer -- Co-Founder & CEO
- Previously founded AiSight (predictive machine maintenance), which he sold to Swiss smart sensor company Sensirion in 2021.

### Christian von Hardenberg -- Co-Founder & CTO
- Former CTO of Rocket Internet and Delivery Hero.
- Scaled engineering teams of over 2,500 people, launching more than 50 internet companies.

## Product & Business Model

Juna AI develops AI systems that enable manufacturing facilities to operate as self-learning, adaptive systems. The platform optimizes complex industrial processes, delivering higher margins and a lower carbon footprint. The company focuses on energy efficiency and emissions reduction in heavy industry.
