# Surge AI

## One-liner

Data labeling platform connecting AI developers with expert human annotators for RLHF, training data, and structured annotation at scale, serving OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, Meta, and Anthropic.

## Founders & Leadership

### Edwin Chen -- Founder & CEO
- MIT dropout who bootstrapped Surge AI from his San Francisco apartment in May 2020.
- Grew the company to $1B+ revenue without a single penny of external funding.
- Featured on the Forbes Billionaires List.

## Product & Business Model

Surge AI connects AI developers with a curated network of expert human annotators, converting raw text, code, images, and conversation transcripts into structured training data. The platform specializes in reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF), RL environments, and annotating language data. Customers include OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, Meta, and Anthropic.
