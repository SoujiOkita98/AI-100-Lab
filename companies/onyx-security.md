# Onyx Security

## Overview
Onyx Security is an Israeli cybersecurity startup founded in 2024 that builds a platform to secure and manage AI agents as they become integral to enterprise operations. The company emerged from stealth in March 2026 with $40M in total funding (a $5M seed from Cyberstarts in 2024 and a $35M Series A led by Conviction). Co-founded by Maxim Bar Kogan (Unit 8200 veteran) and Gil Elbaz (former Nvidia AI researcher), Onyx Security addresses the growing need for governance and security controls over autonomous AI agents in enterprise environments. The company has over 70 employees across Israel, the US, and Canada, and its product is already used by several Fortune 500 companies.
