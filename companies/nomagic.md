# Nomagic

## Overview
Nomagic is a Polish AI robotics company that builds intelligent robotic arms for picking, packing, and sorting in logistics warehouses. The company's robots learn from millions of real operational tasks in 24/7 environments, building an adaptable Physical AI platform. Founded in 2017 by former Google engineer Kacper Nowicki, Nomagic has achieved 220% ARR growth and serves major logistics customers across Europe. Backed by Khosla Ventures, EBRD, Almaz Capital, and the European Investment Bank, the company is expanding into the US market. Nomagic is a rare example of a Central European AI robotics success story.
