# Verge Genomics

## Overview

Verge Genomics is a clinical-stage AI drug discovery company focused on neurodegenerative diseases including ALS, Parkinson's, and Alzheimer's. The company uses AI and machine learning applied to large-scale human genomic data -- not animal models -- to identify novel drug targets and develop therapeutics. Verge is one of the few AI drug discovery companies to have advanced a truly novel, platform-discovered target into clinical trials.

## Founder

### Alice Zhang -- Co-Founder & CEO

- **Origin:** Chinese-American. Her parents are both immigrants from mainland China. Her father was a political activist in China; her mother earned a master's degree in electrical engineering after immigrating.
- **Education:** Dropped out of a PhD program in neuroscience at UCLA to found Verge Genomics.
- **Recognition:** Named to Fortune's 2022 40 Under 40 list, Fierce Biotech's Fiercest Women in Life Sciences (2022), and a Paul & Daisy Soros Fellow for New Americans.
- **Notable:** Zhang is one of the few AI drug discovery founders who has advanced a platform-discovered, novel drug target entirely developed in-house to clinical stage.

## Chinese Founder Context

Alice Zhang represents the wave of second-generation Chinese-Americans building deep-tech AI companies in the US. Her parents' immigrant background and her academic trajectory through UCLA neuroscience reflect the pipeline of Chinese-American talent flowing into AI-driven biotechnology.

## Technology & Approach

Verge's platform analyzes thousands of human brain tissue samples using AI and ML to identify new drug targets for neurodegenerative diseases. Unlike many AI drug discovery competitors, Verge focuses on human genomic data rather than animal models, and has developed its own novel drug targets (rather than applying AI to known targets).

## Funding

Verge has raised over $148 million from investors including a16z, BlackRock, Casdin Capital, GV, and General Catalyst. The company's lead program is in clinical trials.

## What Makes Verge Interesting

1. **Rare clinical-stage AI biotech.** Most AI drug discovery companies remain preclinical; Verge has a novel target in the clinic.
2. **Human-data-first approach.** Using human tissue rather than animal models differentiates Verge from competitors.
3. **Founder credibility.** Alice Zhang dropped out of her neuroscience PhD to found Verge, bringing deep domain expertise.
4. **Chinese-American founder in biotech AI.** Represents the often-overlooked segment of second-generation Chinese-Americans founding significant AI companies in non-obvious verticals.
