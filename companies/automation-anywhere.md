# Automation Anywhere

## Overview
One of the "Big Three" RPA vendors. Founded in 2003 as Tethys Solutions, rebranded ~2010. All four co-founders are Indian-origin. Shifted from on-premise licensing to cloud-native SaaS starting ~2019. Now integrating generative AI for natural-language automation building. Co-founder Mihir Shukla transitioned out of CEO role in 2024. Company has explored potential IPO.

## Investors
NEA, Goldman Sachs, Salesforce Ventures, SoftBank Vision Fund.
