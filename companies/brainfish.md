# Brainfish

## Overview

Brainfish is an AI-powered knowledge base company founded in 2022 by Daniel Kimber and Ajain Vivek, both former product builders at SiteMinder. Headquartered in Sydney, Australia with a US expansion to San Francisco, the company builds "ambient AI" that delivers personalized product knowledge to every customer and agent, automatically updating based on user interactions and product changes. The company has raised approximately $10M across seed and pre-Series A rounds.
