# Warmly

## Overview

Warmly was founded in 2020 by Max Greenwald, Carina Boo, and Alan Zhao. The company is an AI-powered autonomous sales platform that identifies the warmest leads visiting your website and auto-engages them. Max Greenwald previously worked as a Product Manager at Google on Photos, Maps, and Chrome.

## Funding

Warmly raised a $4.5M seed led by NFX, an $11M Series A led by Felicis and NFX (with participation from Zoom and F-Prime Capital), and a $6M Series A extension led by RTP Global, bringing total funding to approximately $22M.

## Key Details

- Y Combinator alumnus
- Hit $3M ARR with 300+ customers including New Relic, Cyberhaven, and CircleCI
- AI identifies website visitors and autonomously engages warm leads
