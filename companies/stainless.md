# Stainless

## Overview

Stainless was founded in 2022 by Alex Rattray, a former Stripe engineer who worked on the developer platform team. The company uses a compiler to automatically generate SDKs from API specifications, eliminating the need for manual SDK authoring and maintenance.

## Funding

Stainless raised a $3.5M seed round from Sequoia and The General Partnership, followed by a $25M Series A led by Andreessen Horowitz with participation from Sequoia, Felicis, Zapier, and MongoDB Ventures, bringing total funding to approximately $35M.

## Key Details

- Powers official SDK libraries for OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta, Cloudflare, and others
- Founded by a former Stripe engineer who worked on API documentation and SDK infrastructure
- Angel investors include Claire Hughes Johnson (former Stripe COO) and Amit Agarwal (Datadog President)
