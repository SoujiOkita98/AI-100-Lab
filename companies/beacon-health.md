# Beacon Health

## Overview

Beacon Health builds AI agents for primary care, automating clinical workflows and patient management tasks. The company raised $5.4M from Accel prior to joining Y Combinator's W26 batch. Beacon Health represents the growing trend of AI agents entering healthcare verticals, handling administrative and clinical tasks that traditionally require significant physician and staff time. The platform aims to reduce physician burnout while improving patient care quality and access.
