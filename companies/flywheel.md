# Flywheel

## Overview

Flywheel is an AI-powered medical imaging data management platform that helps organizations manage, curate, and analyze medical imaging data for research, clinical trials, and AI model development. An Invenshure-founded company headquartered in Minneapolis with offices in the Bay Area, St. Louis, and Budapest, Flywheel provides comprehensive solutions for pharma companies, healthcare providers, payers, and AI developers. The company raised $54M in Series D funding co-led by Novalis LifeSciences and NVentures (NVIDIA), with Microsoft also participating. In December 2025, Flywheel raised an additional $27.5M in equity financing, bringing total funding to approximately $94M.
