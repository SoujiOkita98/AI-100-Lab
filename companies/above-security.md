# Above Security

## Overview

Above Security is an eight-month-old Israeli cybersecurity startup that raised a $43 million Series A round with just 10 employees -- an extraordinary per-capita raise that signals exceptional team pedigree and technology. The company is building AI-native security operations for cloud environments.

## Funding Story

The $43M Series A was co-led by Merlin Ventures, Ballistic Ventures, and Norwest Venture Partners. Raising this amount at eight months old with 10 employees is one of the highest seed-to-headcount funding ratios in cybersecurity.
