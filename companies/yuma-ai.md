# Yuma AI

## Overview

Yuma AI is an AI-powered customer support and sales automation platform built specifically for e-commerce brands on Shopify. Since launching in 2023, Yuma has automated over 5 million customer interactions for leading e-commerce brands. The platform uses generative AI to handle support tickets, respond to customer inquiries, manage social media interactions, and drive sales conversations -- reducing costs while maintaining high-quality customer experiences.

## Founders & Key People

### Guillaume Luccisano -- Co-Founder & CEO

- **Origin:** French, based in San Francisco

## Products & Technology

Yuma's platform provides autonomous AI agents for e-commerce:

1. **Support AI:** Automatically handles customer support tickets across email, chat, and social media
2. **Sales AI:** Proactive AI-driven sales conversations to increase conversion rates
3. **Social AI:** Manages social media customer interactions at scale
4. **Chat AI:** Real-time conversational AI for live chat support

The platform deeply integrates with Shopify, accessing order data, product information, and customer history to provide contextual, accurate responses.

## Business Model

- **SaaS subscription:** Usage-based pricing tied to the volume of automated interactions
- **Target customers:** Shopify-based e-commerce brands, from DTC startups to enterprise retailers
- **Value proposition:** Reduce customer support costs by 50%+ while maintaining or improving response quality and speed

## What Makes Yuma AI Interesting

1. **Exceptional angel investor roster.** Having Kyle Vogt, Emmett Shear, Garry Tan, Solomon Hykes, and Justin Kan as angel investors provides extraordinary access to advice, networks, and credibility in the startup ecosystem.

2. **Vertical AI focus.** Rather than building a general-purpose AI support tool, Yuma is deeply specialized in e-commerce/Shopify, allowing for deeper integration and better accuracy than horizontal competitors.

3. **Proven scale.** 5 million+ automated interactions demonstrates real production usage, not just pilot programs.

4. **Google backing via Gradient Ventures.** Google's AI-focused venture arm leading the seed round provides both capital and potential access to Google's AI infrastructure and expertise.
