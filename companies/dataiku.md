# Dataiku

## Overview
Enterprise AI/ML platform founded by four French technologists in Paris (2013). Flagship product Dataiku DSS bridges data engineers, scientists, analysts, and business users. Relocated HQ to New York. 750+ enterprise clients including J&J, Toyota, BNP Paribas. Preparing for US IPO with Morgan Stanley and Citigroup advising, targeting $6-9B valuation.

## Investors
Tiger Global, Wellington Management, ICONIQ Capital, Battery Ventures, FirstMark Capital, Stripes, CapitalG (Alphabet), Snowflake Ventures, Insight Partners.
