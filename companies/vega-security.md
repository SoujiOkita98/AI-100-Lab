# Vega Security

## Overview

Vega Security is building an AI-native security operations platform designed to detect threats directly where enterprise data resides -- across cloud services, data lakes, and distributed storage environments. Rather than centralizing all security telemetry into a traditional SIEM (Security Information and Event Management), Vega enables real-time detection across complex cloud infrastructures.

Founded in 2024 by two Unit 8200 veterans who were early employees at Granulate (acquired by Intel), the company has already secured multimillion-dollar contracts with enterprise customers across banking, healthcare, and Fortune 500 organizations, including Instacart.

## Funding Story

$65M Series A to $120M Series B in just five months -- one of the fastest Series A-to-B progressions in cybersecurity. Both rounds were led by Accel, with Cyberstarts, Redpoint, and CRV participating.

## What Makes Them Interesting

1. **Five-month Series A to B.** Raising $120M just five months after a $65M round signals explosive customer traction.
2. **Anti-SIEM architecture.** Detecting threats at the data source rather than centralizing logs is a fundamentally different approach to security operations.
3. **Unit 8200 + Granulate/Intel pedigree.** The founders built and sold a company to Intel before starting Vega, providing both technical credibility and operational experience.
4. **Enterprise traction.** Multimillion-dollar contracts with Fortune 500 customers within two years of founding.
