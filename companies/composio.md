# Composio

## Overview

Composio is a developer platform that provides the skills layer for AI agents, enabling seamless interactions with enterprise applications like Gmail, GitHub, Salesforce, and Slack. Founded in 2023 by IIT-Bombay graduates Soham Ganatra and Karan Vaidya (Y Combinator-backed), the company has been adopted by over 100,000 developers and 200+ paying customers. Composio raised a $25M Series A led by Lightspeed Venture Partners, bringing total funding to $29M. The platform generates over $1M in annualized recurring revenue.
