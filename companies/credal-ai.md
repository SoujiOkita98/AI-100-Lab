# Credal.ai

## Overview

Credal.ai is a secure AI agent platform that enables enterprises to connect their internal data to large language models while maintaining strict security, governance, and compliance controls. Founded by two former Palantir engineers who bonded over a shared frustration with enterprise data security gaps, Credal automatically redacts and anonymizes sensitive data before it reaches LLMs, provides comprehensive audit logs, and enforces access controls. The company notably won a contract with HHS (U.S. Department of Health and Human Services) for its GenAI agent platform.

- **Background:** 7 years at Palantir building AI-powered systems in financial institutions, life sciences organizations, and public health; previously led teams at Google, THG (IPO'd), and GoCardless (unicorn)

## Products & Technology

Credal's platform provides:

1. **Data Security Layer:** Automatic redaction, anonymization, and warning when sensitive data is about to be sent to LLMs hosted on public clouds
2. **Audit Logging:** Complete logs showing what data has been shared with which LLMs, by whom, and when
3. **Access Controls:** Role-based permissions ensuring users can only query data they're authorized to see
4. **AI Agent Platform:** Enterprise-ready AI agents that can safely access internal tools and data sources
5. **Integration Connectors:** Connect AI to every enterprise tool with security built in

## Business Model

- **Enterprise SaaS:** Sells to large organizations with strict compliance requirements
- **Target verticals:** Government, healthcare, financial services, legal

## What Makes Credal.ai Interesting

1. **Palantir DNA applied to LLM security.** The founders' deep experience building classified AI systems at Palantir gives them credibility and domain expertise in enterprise data governance.

2. **Government traction.** Winning an HHS contract validates the platform for the most security-sensitive use cases, a strong reference for other government and regulated-industry customers.

3. **Timing advantage.** As enterprises rush to adopt LLMs, the security and compliance gap is a real blocker. Credal is well-positioned as a "picks and shovels" play in the enterprise AI gold rush.

4. **Growing regulatory tailwinds.** EU AI Act, SOC 2 requirements, and HIPAA concerns around AI all increase demand for platforms like Credal.
