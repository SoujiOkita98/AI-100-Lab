# Tractable

## Overview
Tractable is a London-based AI company that uses deep learning and computer vision to automate vehicle and property damage assessments for insurers. Founded in 2014 by researchers from Imperial College and Cambridge University through Entrepreneur First, the company became the UK's first computer vision unicorn in 2021 at a $1B valuation. Major insurers in the US, UK, Japan, and Europe use Tractable's products to analyze damaged vehicle images and streamline claims processing. The company has offices in London, New York, and Tokyo.
