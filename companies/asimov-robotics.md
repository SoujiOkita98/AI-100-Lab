# Asimov

## Overview

Asimov is building an internet-scale marketplace for robot training data. As robotics companies race to train AI models that can manipulate objects, navigate environments, and perform complex physical tasks, the availability of high-quality training data has become the primary bottleneck. Asimov creates a marketplace where robotics companies, research labs, and data providers can buy, sell, and share robot manipulation and interaction data at scale. This mirrors what happened with internet data for LLMs -- whoever controls the data pipeline controls the ecosystem.
