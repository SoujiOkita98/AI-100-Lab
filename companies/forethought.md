# Forethought

## Overview

Forethought is an AI-native platform for enterprise customer support, built on a multi-agent architecture for omnichannel resolution. Founded in 2018 by Deon Nicholas (ex-Facebook, Palantir, Dropbox) and Sami Ghoche, the company is trusted by leading organizations including Upwork, Grammarly, Airtable, and Datadog. The company raised $115M total, with its most recent being a $25M strategic Series D led by Blue Cloud Ventures. Zendesk signed a definitive agreement to acquire Forethought in March 2026, with the transaction expected to close by end of Q1 2026.
