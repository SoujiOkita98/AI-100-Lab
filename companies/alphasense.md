# AlphaSense

## Overview
AI-powered market intelligence platform founded by a Finnish-Indian duo who met at Wharton. Uses NLP and generative AI to search across earnings transcripts, SEC filings, broker research, news, and expert transcripts (via 2024 Tegus acquisition for ~$930M). Serves most of the S&P 500. $500M+ ARR growing rapidly.

## Investors
Goldman Sachs, Viking Global, SoftBank, CapitalG (Alphabet), BOND (Mary Meeker), Innovation Endeavors (Eric Schmidt), BDT & MSD Partners.
