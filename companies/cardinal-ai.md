# Cardinal

## Overview

Cardinal is an AI platform for precision outbound sales, already serving 40+ YC companies as paying customers. Founded by 2x YC alumni, Cardinal uses AI to automate and optimize outbound sales workflows -- from prospect identification and research to personalized message crafting and follow-up sequencing. The strong early traction within the YC ecosystem suggests effective product-market fit in the competitive AI sales tooling space.
