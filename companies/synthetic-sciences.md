# Synthetic Sciences

## Overview

Synthetic Sciences is building AI co-scientists that can run research end-to-end, positioning itself as "Claude Code for Science." The company secured $1.9M in total funding (pre-YC plus the standard YC deal). The platform aims to accelerate scientific discovery by enabling AI agents to autonomously design experiments, analyze data, and generate hypotheses across scientific domains. This represents the growing trend of AI agents moving from software development into scientific research workflows.

## What Makes Synthetic Sciences Interesting

1. **AI for science:** Applies the autonomous agent paradigm proven in coding (Cursor, Claude Code) to scientific research.
2. **End-to-end research automation:** Goes beyond copilot assistance to fully autonomous research execution.
3. **YC W26 batch:** Part of the largest YC batch ever, focused on autonomous agents.
