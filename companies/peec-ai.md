# Peec AI

## One-liner

AI search analytics platform enabling marketing teams to track, analyze, and improve brand visibility across generative AI search engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity (generative engine optimization).

## Founders & Leadership

### Marius Meiners -- Co-Founder

### Tobias Siwona -- Co-Founder

### Daniel Drabo -- Co-Founder

The founding team met during an Antler Founder residency in Berlin in late 2024.

## Product & Business Model

Peec AI builds an analytics platform that enables marketing teams to analyze and improve their brand visibility in AI search. The platform specializes in generative engine optimization (GEO), helping marketing teams track, analyze, and improve brand performance on AI search platforms like ChatGPT and Perplexity.
