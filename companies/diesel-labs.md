# Diesel Labs

## Overview

Diesel Labs is a Cambridge, Massachusetts-based content intelligence company founded in 2016 that uses machine learning and generative AI to produce consumer insights for marketing teams. The company measures audience attention across the entertainment landscape and helps media companies determine what to create, where to sell, and how to measure success. More recently, Diesel Labs has developed PanelAI, a tool that combines generative AI chat with its proprietary dataset to deliver synthetic focus group capabilities.

## Founders

- **Anjali Midha** - Co-Founder & CEO. MBA from MIT Sloan, BA from Tufts; former Global Media Research Director at Twitter (via Bluefin Labs acquisition); VP at Bluefin Labs. Origin: United States.
- **Michael Fleischman** - Co-Founder. PhD in CS from MIT; co-founded Bluefin Labs; now CTO at OpenSpace; AI/NLP expert. Origin: United States.
- **Russell Stevens** - Co-Founder. Dartmouth (Government), Harvard Kennedy School (MPP); Head of Programs at MIT Center for Constructive Communication; co-founded Cortico. Origin: United States.

## Product

Diesel Labs' platform processes more than 2.2 billion daily signals across social media, video, and traditional media platforms including Facebook, X, Instagram, YouTube, Telegram, Discord, and Wikipedia.

**PanelAI** is the company's latest product, which merges generative AI chat with their proprietary dataset to create synthetic focus groups that deliver consumer insights without traditional survey overhead.
