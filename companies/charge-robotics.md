# Charge Robotics

## Overview

Charge Robotics is a robotics company founded in 2021 by Banks Hunter (CEO) and Max Justicz, focused on automating the most labor-intensive segments of solar farm construction. Headquartered in Oakland, CA (Y Combinator alumni), the company's flagship product, the Sunrise system, is a robotic construction unit that automates the assembly and installation of solar-tracking hardware. The company has raised approximately $39M across four rounds, including a $22M Series B in March 2025. Key investors include Y Combinator, Climate Capital, FoundersX Ventures, Hummingbird Ventures, and E14 Fund.
