# Carefam

## Overview
Carefam is an Israeli AI startup that emerged from stealth in March 2026 with $14.5M in funding led by Pitango HealthTech. The company builds an AI-powered platform for long-term care and healthcare human resources, deploying intelligent agents that automate candidate outreach, scheduling, credential screening, and onboarding for hospitals and care facilities. Co-founded by Matan Hoffmann (CEO, ex-IDF Special Forces) and Eyal Shulman (CTO, ex-Unit 8200), Carefam has achieved 900% growth in a single year with hundreds of US healthcare providers already using the platform. The company addresses the critical staffing shortage in US healthcare by freeing up HR teams from repetitive hiring workflows.
