# Dart

## Overview

Dart is a Redwood City, California-based AI-native project management tool that launched through Y Combinator's Winter 2022 batch. The platform lets users chat with AI to create tasks, plan roadmaps, generate reports, and handle project management work. Dart claims to save teams roughly a third of their project management time (about 7 hours per week). The company reported $2.4M in revenue with a 16-person team in 2024.

## Founders

- **Milad Malek** - Co-Founder. BS in Physics from Stanford; former Senior Ventures Associate at Plug and Play Ventures. Origin: United States.
- **Zack Swafford** - Co-Founder. BS + MS in CS (AI) from Stanford; first engineer at Plenty; co-founded Atch; AI/ML consulting and Stanford teaching. Origin: United States.

## Product

Dart is a fully-featured project management tool with deep AI integration:
- Conversational AI interface for task creation and management
- Automatic task property filling and subtask breakdown
- AI-powered roadmap planning and report generation
- Automation using generative AI models (GPT-4)
- Available as a free tier with premium features

The tool is used by startups, SMEs, law firms, and business consultancies.
