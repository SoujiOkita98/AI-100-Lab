# Gamma

## One-liner

AI-native presentation and content creation platform that replaces PowerPoint, enabling users to generate polished presentations, websites, and documents from text prompts.

## Founders & Leadership

### Grant Lee -- Co-Founder & CEO

- BS/MS in Mechanical & Biomechanical Engineering from Stanford.
- First finance hire and eventual CFO at Optimizely.
- COO at ClearBrain (acquired by Amplitude).

### James Fox -- Co-Founder

- Staff engineer at Optimizely with deep full-stack engineering experience.

### Jon Noronha -- Co-Founder

- VP of Product at Optimizely.

All three founders met at Optimizely and began prototyping Gamma as a side project in 2020.

## Product & Business Model

Gamma is an AI-powered platform for creating presentations, websites, and documents. 70 million users worldwide have created over 400 million pieces of content. The company hit $102M ARR in October 2025 (up from $30.5M at end of 2024) with a lean 50-person team. Gamma has been profitable for over two years, making it a notable example of capital-efficient growth in the AI space.
