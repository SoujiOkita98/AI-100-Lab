# CuspAI

> AI "search engine for new materials" -- backed by Nvidia, advised by Hinton and LeCun, with $130M raised to transform materials discovery for climate and industry.

## Company Overview

CuspAI is a Cambridge, UK-based AI startup building a generative AI platform for materials discovery. Founded in March 2024 by Dr. Chad Edwards (who previously scaled quantum computing unicorn Quantinuum) and Prof. Max Welling (an AI pioneer from Microsoft Research and Qualcomm), the company uses AI to discover new materials in months rather than decades. The platform targets applications in decarbonization, energy storage, carbon capture, and industrial chemistry.

## Founders

### Dr. Chad Edwards -- CEO

Chemist by training who transitioned into deep tech entrepreneurship. Previously helped scale Quantinuum, a quantum computing company that reached unicorn status. His background bridges chemistry domain expertise with technology commercialization.

### Prof. Max Welling -- Chief Scientist

One of the most cited AI researchers in the world. Former Distinguished Scientist at Microsoft Research and VP of Technology at Qualcomm. Co-inventor of the Variational Autoencoder (VAE), one of the foundational generative AI architectures. Professor at the University of Amsterdam.

## Business Model

CuspAI partners with industrial companies to discover novel materials for specific applications (catalysts, battery materials, carbon capture sorbents). Revenue model includes licensing, collaborative R&D partnerships, and potentially royalties on discovered materials.

## What Makes CuspAI Interesting

1. **Two Turing Award winners as advisors:** Having both Hinton and LeCun on the advisory board is essentially unprecedented for a Series A startup.
2. **Industrial backing:** Nvidia, Samsung, Hyundai, and Temasek signal that major industrial players believe in the platform's potential for real-world materials breakthroughs.
3. **Climate tech meets AI:** Positioned at the intersection of generative AI and decarbonization -- a rare combination of cutting-edge tech and environmental impact.
4. **Founder complementarity:** Edwards brings deep tech commercialization experience (Quantinuum), while Welling brings foundational AI research credentials (co-inventor of VAE).
5. **European AI champion:** One of the most well-funded AI startups to emerge from the UK/European ecosystem.
