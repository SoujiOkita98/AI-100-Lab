# Cambio

## Overview

Cambio is an AI-native commercial real estate (CRE) operations platform founded in 2022 by Leia de Guzman and Stephanie Grayson, who met as roommates at Stanford GSB. The platform uses large language models and agentic AI workflows to transform raw, unstructured building data into investor-grade decisions and reporting, compressing months of manual work into minutes. The company's mission is to decarbonize commercial real estate -- which accounts for roughly 40% of global carbon emissions -- by proving out the investment rationale for retrofits and sustainable building operations.

## Product & Technology

Unlike legacy real estate software that relies on static rules and manual inputs, Cambio is built from the ground up around agentic AI. The platform automatically collects building data and automates workflows for capital planning, compliance, and decarbonization strategy. It reasons over thousands of pages of unstructured building documents to help institutional investors evaluate retrofit opportunities across large portfolios.

## Traction

Cambio has scaled to 35 countries and more than 2 billion square feet in managed assets. Key enterprise customers include Principal Real Estate, Nuveen Real Estate, BGO, LaSalle Investment Management, Oxford Properties Group, Madison International Realty, and Beacon Capital Partners. The company also opened a London office to support EU and APAC expansion.

## Team

- **Leia de Guzman** (Co-Founder & Co-CEO): Nearly a decade in CRE; former Investments Director at Oxford Properties/OMERS deploying $8B globally; climate tech investor at G2 Venture Partners.
- **Stephanie Grayson** (Co-Founder & Co-CEO): ~10 years in CRE; launched platforms at Goldman Sachs, KKR ($30B REIT), and JPMorgan; deep expertise in building science and retrofit strategy.
- **Leon Chen** (CTO): 12+ years software engineering; previously at Faire (joined as fourth backend engineer).
