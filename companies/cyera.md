# Cyera

## Overview
Cyera is an Israeli AI-powered data security platform that helps enterprises discover, classify, and protect sensitive data across cloud, SaaS, IaaS, PaaS, and on-premises environments. The platform is deployed by 20% of Fortune 500 companies across financial services, retail, healthcare, technology, and telecom. Founded by IDF Unit 8200 veterans, Cyera has grown rapidly from a $3B valuation in late 2024 to $9B in January 2026, making it one of the fastest-growing Israeli cybersecurity companies ever.
