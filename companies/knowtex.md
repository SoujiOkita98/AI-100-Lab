# Knowtex

## Overview

Knowtex is an AI-powered ambient clinical documentation platform that creates visit notes from doctor-patient conversations and automates charge capture for revenue cycle management. The platform listens to clinical encounters, generates structured medical notes using AI, and captures billable codes (ICD-10 and CPT) to reduce physician burnout and recover revenue leakage. Knowtex was selected by the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) to deploy ambient clinical AI across its health system, a major validation for the technology.

## Products & Technology

1. **Ambient Clinical Documentation:** AI listens to doctor-patient conversations and automatically generates structured visit notes
2. **Charge Capture:** Automatically identifies and codes billable services (ICD-10 and CPT codes) from clinical encounters
3. **Revenue Cycle Optimization:** Reduces coding errors and captures missed billable events to recover revenue
4. **EHR Integration:** Integrates with electronic health record systems

## Business Model

- **SaaS subscription + government contracts:** Per-provider or per-encounter pricing for healthcare organizations
- **Target customers:** Hospital systems, clinics, and government healthcare (VA)
- **Key milestone:** Top 3 winner of the 2024 AI Tech Sprint for Ambient Scribe, leading to the VA contract

## What Makes Knowtex Interesting

1. **VA contract is a massive validator.** Being selected alongside Abridge for the $15M VA deployment means Knowtex's technology has been vetted against stringent government procurement standards -- an extraordinary achievement for an early-stage startup.

2. **Stanford AI + medical scribe experience.** Both founders combined Stanford CS with real experience as medical scribes, giving them an unusually deep understanding of both the technical and clinical sides of the problem.

3. **Revenue cycle capture.** Beyond just documentation, Knowtex's charge capture functionality directly recovers revenue for healthcare providers -- a concrete, measurable ROI that drives adoption.

4. **David vs. Goliath positioning.** Competing and winning alongside Abridge (which has raised $200M+) with a fraction of the funding demonstrates exceptional product quality and scrappy execution.
