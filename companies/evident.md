# Evidently AI

## Overview

Evidently AI is a London-based startup that builds an open-source framework for evaluating, testing, and monitoring machine learning models and LLM-powered systems. Founded in 2020 by Elena Samuylova and Emeli Dral, the company is a Y Combinator alumnus (W21) and has grown its open-source library to over 25 million downloads, used in thousands of companies from startups to enterprises. The platform offers 100+ pre-made metrics and a framework for custom ML, LLM, and data evaluations.

## Founders

- **Elena Samuylova** - Co-Founder & CEO
- **Emeli Dral** - Co-Founder & CTO

## Product

Evidently provides both an open-source framework and a commercial platform:

**Open Source (Evidently OSS):**
- ML and LLM observability framework
- 100+ pre-made evaluation metrics
- Data drift detection, model performance monitoring
- Support for tabular data through generative AI systems

**Commercial Platform:**
- Enterprise-grade monitoring and alerting
- Team collaboration features
- Dashboard and reporting capabilities

The company hit $1M in revenue in 2024 with a 7-person team.
