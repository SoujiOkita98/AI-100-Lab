# Chroma

## Overview

Chroma is an open-source AI-native embedding database designed to power LLM applications with retrieval, memory, and semantic search capabilities. Founded in 2022 by Jeff Huber (ex-Standard Cyborg) and Anton Troynikov, the company provides infrastructure for storing and querying vector embeddings. Chroma raised an $18M seed round led by Quiet Capital at a $75M valuation, with angel investors including Sam Altman's brothers, Naval Ravikant, and Guillermo Rauch. The project has over 15,000 GitHub stars and is one of the most popular open-source vector databases.
