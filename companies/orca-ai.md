# Orca AI

## Overview
Orca AI is an Israeli maritime technology company that uses AI and computer vision to enable safer and more efficient autonomous shipping. The company holds the world's largest marine visual dataset, built from over 80 million nautical miles of data. Orca AI's technology is used by leading global shipping firms including MSC, NYK, Scorpio, and Seaspan. The platform provides real-time situational awareness to bridge teams, reducing navigation risks and human error. In 2022, Orca AI partnered with The Nippon Foundation to complete the world's first commercial autonomous voyage. The company estimates its optimized routing cuts average fuel costs by $100,000 per vessel per year and avoided 195,000 tons of CO2 emissions in 2024.
