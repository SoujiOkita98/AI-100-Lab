# Sentrial

## Overview

Sentrial provides production monitoring specifically designed for AI products. As AI-powered products move to production, traditional APM (application performance monitoring) tools fail to capture the unique failure modes of AI systems -- hallucinations, quality degradation, prompt injection, model drift, and latency spikes. Sentrial fills this gap with monitoring infrastructure purpose-built for AI, giving teams real-time visibility into how their AI products perform in the hands of real users.
