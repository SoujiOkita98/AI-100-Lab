# Lightricks

## Overview
Lightricks is an Israeli creative AI company that has evolved from mobile photo editing (Facetune, with 200M+ downloads) into a full generative AI platform. The company launched LTX Studio, an AI-driven platform for video production and filmmaking, and is pivoting toward enterprise customers in 2026. Valued at $1.8 billion as of its Series D, Lightricks generates over $50M in ARR and is profitable. The company processes billions of images annually with customers including Amazon and DoorDash.
