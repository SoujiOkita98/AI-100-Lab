# Sarvam AI

## Overview
Sarvam AI is India's leading sovereign AI company building foundation models tailored for Indian languages and use cases. Founded in August 2023 by Vivek Raghavan and Pratyush Kumar (both associated with AI4Bharat at IIT Madras), the company was selected to contribute to India's sovereign AI model initiative under the IndiaAI Mission. The government provided subsidized access to 4,096 NVIDIA H100 GPUs. Sarvam AI's models support multiple Indian languages and are designed for deployment across government, enterprise, and consumer applications in India's unique multilingual context.
