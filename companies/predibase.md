# Predibase -- Fine-Tuning & Serving Open-Source AI Models (Acquired by Rubrik)

## Company Overview

Predibase was an AI infrastructure company that enabled software engineers to fine-tune and serve open-source AI models across cloud environments. The platform democratized deep learning through a declarative, low-code approach, allowing any software engineer to do the work of an ML engineer.

In June 2025, Rubrik acquired Predibase for an estimated $100M-$500M to accelerate agentic AI adoption.

## Founders and Team

### Devvret Rishi -- Co-Founder & CEO
- **Education:** Bachelor's and Master's in Computer Science from Harvard University, focused on ML.
- **Origin:** Indian-American. The name indicates Indian origin.
- **Prior experience:** Joined **Google** in 2016 as PM on Firebase, then Google Research, then **Vertex AI**. First product lead for **Kaggle** (8M+ users).

### Piero Molino -- Co-Founder & Chief Science Officer
- **Origin:** Italy. Creator of **Ludwig**, the open-source declarative ML framework (built at Uber AI Labs).

### Travis Addair -- Co-Founder & CTO
- **Origin:** United States. Former Uber ML Platform lead.

**Founder Pattern:** Devvret Rishi represents the "Indian-American PM-turned-founder" archetype -- Harvard CS to Google PM to AI startup. The Indian founder angle is not immediately obvious (Harvard-educated, Google career), making this a company easily missed in Indian founder tracking. The team's collective Uber + Google ML platform experience is a concentrated dose of production ML infrastructure expertise.
