# Assort Health

## Overview

Assort Health is a San Francisco-based healthcare AI startup founded in 2023 by Jeff Liu and Jon Wang. The company builds agentic AI systems that handle patient phone calls, scheduling, and engagement for healthcare providers, reducing average hold times from 11 minutes to 1 minute. The platform has processed over 42 million patient interactions.

Assort Health has raised $102M in total funding, most recently a Series B led by Lightspeed Venture Partners. The company was named to the 2025 Forbes Cloud 100 list alongside OpenAI and Anthropic.

## Funding story

**2023 -- Seed ($3.5M).** Initial capital to build the first generative AI system for healthcare call centers.

**April 2025 -- Series A ($26M).** Funded expansion of specialty-specific AI models for managing patient phone calls across different medical specialties.

**August 2025 -- Series B ($76M).** Led by Lightspeed Venture Partners, with participation from Felicis, First Round Capital, Chemistry, A* Capital, Liquid2, and Quiet Capital. Brought total raised to $102M. Came just four months after the Series A, reflecting exceptional growth.

## What makes them interesting

1. **Massive pain point.** Healthcare phone calls are universally hated by both patients and providers. 11-minute average hold times represent a genuine crisis in patient access. Assort's AI reduces this to 1 minute.

2. **Scale of adoption.** 42M+ patient interactions processed demonstrates real production deployment, not lab demos.

3. **Forbes Cloud 100.** Recognition alongside OpenAI and Anthropic validates the company's position as a leading AI application company in healthcare.
