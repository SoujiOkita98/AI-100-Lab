# Juicebox

## One-liner

AI-powered people search engine (PeopleGPT) that uses natural language queries to find and rank candidates from 800M+ profiles across 30+ data sources for recruiting and talent sourcing.

## Founders & Leadership

### David Paffenholz -- Co-Founder & CEO

- German-born, grew up in Dusseldorf. Attended the International School of Dusseldorf before graduating from Harvard University in three years with a BA in Economics.
- Prior experience includes Fifth Wall (Summer Analyst), NEA (intern), Snap Inc. (international expansion strategy), and Moonfare (Founders Associate).
- Was 22 years old when he co-founded Juicebox in 2022.

### Ishan Gupta -- Co-Founder & CTO

- From Kanpur, India. Attended Seth Anandram Jaipuria School before earning a BA in Computer Science from Dartmouth College.
- Background in software engineering, computer vision, and AI research. Previously a Software Engineer at BlueLearn and Research Assistant at Harcourt Butler Technical University.
- Was 19 years old when he co-founded Juicebox in 2022.

## Founding Story

David Paffenholz and Ishan Gupta founded Juicebox in 2022 and went through Y Combinator's Summer 2022 batch (YC S22). The company launched PeopleGPT in late 2023, an AI-powered people search engine that allows users to find candidates using natural language queries. The product gained immediate traction, growing organically from zero to 2,500+ customers without a sales team. The team bootstrapped to their first $1M in ARR with just four employees before raising significant capital.

## Product & Business Model

### What Juicebox Does

Juicebox builds AI-powered recruiting and talent sourcing tools:

1. **PeopleGPT** -- A natural language people search engine that searches 800M+ profiles across 30+ data sources (LinkedIn, GitHub, published papers, etc.) to find, rank, and surface relevant candidates.
2. **Juicebox Agent** -- Autonomous AI recruiting agents that handle candidate search, assessment, and automated outreach 24/7.

### Customers

Teams at Ramp, Perplexity, Quora, Anyscale, and leading AI labs use Juicebox. The platform serves 3,000+ customers ranging from early-stage startups to Fortune 500 companies and recruiting agencies.

### Pricing / Monetization

SaaS subscription model for recruiting teams and talent sourcers.

## Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Positioning |
|-----------|------------|
| **LinkedIn Recruiter** | Incumbent platform with massive data but traditional keyword-based search |
| **hireEZ (formerly Hiretual)** | AI-powered sourcing and candidate engagement platform |
| **Gem** | Talent CRM and sourcing platform for recruiting teams |
| **Seekout** | AI talent search with diversity hiring features |
| **Findem** | AI-powered talent intelligence and sourcing |

Juicebox differentiates through its natural language search interface (PeopleGPT), which lets recruiters describe ideal candidates in plain English rather than using Boolean queries or rigid filters.

## What Makes Juicebox Interesting

1. **Natural language search.** PeopleGPT lets recruiters describe who they are looking for in plain English, fundamentally changing how talent sourcing is done.
2. **Capital efficiency.** Bootstrapped to $1M ARR with four people before taking significant outside capital, demonstrating strong product-market fit.
3. **Organic growth.** Reached 2,500+ customers without a sales team, indicating strong word-of-mouth and product-led growth.
4. **Young founders.** David (22) and Ishan (19) built the company while still college-age, with backing from top-tier investors like Sequoia and Y Combinator.
5. **Large data moat.** Indexing 800M+ profiles across 30+ data sources creates a meaningful competitive advantage.

## Risks & Open Questions

- **LinkedIn dependency.** Heavy reliance on LinkedIn data creates platform risk if LinkedIn restricts access.
- **Competitive pressure.** The AI recruiting space is attracting significant funding and new entrants.
- **Enterprise scaling.** Transitioning from organic/PLG growth to enterprise sales will require building a sales organization.
- **Data accuracy.** Maintaining freshness and accuracy across 800M+ profiles from 30+ sources is an ongoing challenge.
