# Remy.Ai

## Overview

Remy.Ai automates dexterous tasks in warehouses using AI-powered robotic manipulation. While warehouse robotics has seen significant investment in navigation and transport (AMRs), dexterous manipulation -- picking, packing, sorting diverse objects -- remains one of the hardest unsolved problems. Remy.Ai tackles this challenge using advanced AI to enable robots to handle a wide variety of objects with human-like dexterity, addressing labor shortages and efficiency gaps in the $300B+ logistics industry.
