# Eos AI

## Overview

Eos AI is building the autonomous operating system for healthcare, deploying AI agents to manage both clinical and administrative workflows across healthcare organizations. The platform aims to be the central intelligence layer that coordinates patient scheduling, clinical documentation, billing, compliance, and care coordination -- replacing the patchwork of disconnected healthcare IT systems with a unified, AI-native operating system. Healthcare is one of the most complex and high-stakes verticals for AI agent deployment.
