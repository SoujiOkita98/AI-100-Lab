## Methodology Notes

- **Re-scrape VC portfolios quarterly** to catch new investments
- **Check YC batches** after each Demo Day (W and S batches, ~2x/year)
- **Monitor 36Kr English** weekly for Chinese founder AI startups
- **Cross-reference Hurun/Forbes China lists** annually
- **SEC Form D filings** catch stealth companies with zero press
- **HKEX/STAR Market IPO filings** reveal detailed funding histories in prospectuses
