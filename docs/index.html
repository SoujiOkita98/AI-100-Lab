6. Methodology

Data Collection

Multi-source research: VC portfolio pages (a16z, Sequoia, Khosla, Greylock, General Catalyst, Accel, Felicis, Bessemer, NEA, Thrive, Coatue), startups.gallery cross-reference (1,254 companies), Y Combinator directory (W22–W26), TechCrunch “$100M+ AI startups” lists, SEC EDGAR Form D filings, regional press (Sifted, TechNode, KrASIA, YourStory, Calcalist), and direct company website verification.

Quality Principles

1. Quality over completeness — empty field > fabricated field.
2. Every claim is sourced — funding amounts link to original reporting where available (88.5% coverage).
3. Uncertainty is explicit — per-company confidence rating; unconfirmed rounds marked.
4. Origin is inference — founder ethnicity/nationality inferred from names + public bios, not self-reported.

Validation

Random audit of 20 companies against Crunchbase and press: 8–9/20 had fully correct funding totals on initial check. Drove systematic back-fill of 121 missing rounds and normalization of 337 origin values. A second audit achieved 20/20 on the corrected records. Systematic undercounting of earlier rounds remains a known limitation.

Schema & Reproducibility

Each company stored as markdown with YAML frontmatter. Canonical schema: snake_case fields, monetary values in USD millions (suffix _m), dates as YYYY-MM. Exports: 3 CSV files (companies, rounds, founders), JSON, SQLite. All rebuild scripts in scripts/. Full source on GitHub.

7. Planned Interactive Features

Natural Language Query
Ask questions in plain English:
“Chinese-founded AI companies that raised Series A in 2025 in the Bay Area”

Translates natural language to SQL queries via Claude API. Every result includes source citations and confidence levels.
Relationship Network Graph
Force-directed graph of founder–company–investor relationships. Explore co-investment patterns, founder alumni networks (ex-OpenAI, ex-Google Brain), and VC portfolio overlap. Filterable by sector, geography, and origin.
Predictive Signal Scoring
Composite scoring based on: sector momentum, funding velocity, investor tier, and founder pedigree signals. Designed as a research prioritization tool with fully transparent methodology — not financial advice.
